Why is Necromancer in Darkwood?
 
Only place that has power for ritual. Cinder Cult hates being in here, both humans and hobgoblins. It is creepy, the shadow fey laugh at them, they never feel like the undead are fully under the Necromancer's control.
 
Cut down trees, built watchtowers, defenses to try to keep forest back and protect the ritual area. But forest is fighting back in subtle ways, so paths are always shifting. One encounter could be lost cultists.
 
Still, Cinder Court needs a way to navigate in the Darkwood -- what is it?
 
Shadow fey who inhabit Darkwood don't necessarily know about connection between Cinder Court and the Shadowfell. Many of them see the Cinder Court purely as intruders and thus may help the party. Others may help Cinder Court, or at least not actively hinder them.
