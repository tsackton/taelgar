**Captain Nugruk**  
_Medium undead, neutral evil_  
**Armor Class:** 15 (armor scraps)  
**Hit Points:** 67 (9d8 + 27)  
**Speed:** 30 ft

**Saving Throws:** Wis +3  
**Vulnerabilities:** Bludgeoning

**Condition Immunities:** Exhaustion, Poisoned  
**Senses:** Passive Perception 13  
**Languages:** Common  
**Challenge:** Pair (1 per 2 PC should be a balanced battle)  
**------------------**
 
**Charge.** While mounted, if Nugruk moves at least 20 feet straight towards a target and then hits it with a pike attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 10 (3d6) piercing damage.
 
**Spellcasting.** Nugruk is a 6th level spellcaster. It's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 14, spell attack +6). It regains expended spell slots when it finishes a short or long rest. It knows the following warlock spells:
 
Cantrips (at will): _eldritch blast_ (two beams, each with range 300 ft, 1d10+3 damage), _toll the dead, create bonfire_
 
Spells (2 3rd level spell slots): _counterspell, hellish rebuke, hex, hold person, mind spike, misty step, vampiric touch, bestow curse_ (can only be cast once per long rest)
 
Scroll of _spider climb_ on his person, which he will use as needed to escape, get his horse, and flee to Darkwood.
 
**ACTIONS**  
--------------  
**Pike.** _Melee Weapon Attack._ +6 to hit, reach 10 ft, one target. _Hit_: 9 (1d10 + 4) piercing damage, plus 10 (3d6) necrotic damage.
 
**Dreadful Glare**_. Bonus Action._ Nugruk targets one creature it can see within 60 feet of it. If the target can see Nugluk, the target must succeed on a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw against this magic or become frightened for 1 minute. The frightened creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. A target that succeeds on the saving throw is immune to this creature's Dreadful Glare for the next 24 hours.
