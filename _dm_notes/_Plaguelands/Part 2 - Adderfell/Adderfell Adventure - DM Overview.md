## Synopsis

This section of the campaign starts with the approach to Adderfell and the introduction to Earl Eofric, and ends with a council meeting and some final decisions about the party's next steps. In between, there are a variety of optional encounters, plus an attack from the Shadowfey assassins tracking the party, and the opportunity to explore the city for flavor, shopping, etc.
