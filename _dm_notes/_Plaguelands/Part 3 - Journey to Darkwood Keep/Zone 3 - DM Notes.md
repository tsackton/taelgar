## Overview:
 
This area is mostly wild now, although historically it was settled by hunters, trappers, woodsman, and others who made their living off the forest (although no one went too deep into the Darkwood).
 
## Description:
 
After you cross the last bridge, you notice immediately that the land becomes more wild. Fields give way to stands of trees and open woods, with the occasional meadow clearing or hill peaking out of the tree. The road is quiet, and there are few signs of settlement anywhere.
 
## Challenge:

**Navigation DC**: 10 off road, no check needed if just following the road  
**Forage DC**: DC 12 (generally decent amount of game and food, but season not ideal)  
**Danger Number:** 3  
**Discovery Number:** 1

Encounters; Flavor Effects; Hazard Cards; Global Effects; Plot Developments
