*arrow loops on all floors  
*battlements and defensive structures on top floor, along with ballista

**West Tower (Guard Tower):**
 
First floor is a guard house. Again, any equipment for war has been cleared out quickly, DC 15 Int (Investigation) check reveals info about rapid departure, weapons taken, etc -- if they have found other signs before, DC 10 (but beating DC 15 gives more info).
 
2nd floor is guard room, with trap door and ladder to roof.
