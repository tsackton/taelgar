The central open area of the castle is a large courtyard, about 40' wide and over 150' long. Some general features:
 
*towards the northern end, vegetable and herb gardens, which have been completely destroyed  
*large doors to great hall most prominent, but many other doors  
*towards the southern end, chicken coop and space near stables for a few pigs and horses.  
*in center, an open military parade yard, gravel
 
Can attempt to stealth to avoid notice of **skeleton archers** on the walls, otherwise if they are noisy will be spotted, and they will shoot arrows into courtyard if in range.
