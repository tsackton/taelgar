General [[Shemra Azem]] framework:
- Commanders/lieutenants: use the Priest of Osybus statblock https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/1680942-priest-of-osybus, but have the tattoo summon a lizardfolk statblock. The main tattoo is the Samso sigil, (sleep nevermore). This is ~2500 xp per, so can't use this for huge armies. 

 - Mooks - use a pair of minion statblocks and make the tattoo revival simpler. 

Alternatives would be any kind of CR 4-7 humanoids, e.g.:
- https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/4485796-bugbear-channeler
- https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/5900653-cultist-of-bhaal
- https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/4485899-human-storm-wizard
- https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/5195042-gladiator
- https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/5900684-zhentilar-paladin-of-bane

All of these gain the Priest of Osybus trait, and tattoos. Consider changing spells into Sleep.

(15k xp)

## Barrow Fight

This will be complicated. Run as groups of [[Shemra Azem]]. Each group is:

1 Priest of Osybus, https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/1680942-priest-of-osybus (2500 xp each = 10,000 xp); summon https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/4485933-lizardfolk-bloodeye or https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/4485935-lizardfolk-scaletooth and 5 Minions, https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/4485893-human-death-acolyte (90 xp each = 1800 XP) who summon lizardfolk grunt minions.
