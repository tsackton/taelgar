## Set up

This part starts after they have settled in Cairn Dor, and has two major sub-goals. 

(1) The party needs to find the lizardfolk dreamers
(2) The party needs to learn how to wake them 

### Basics

The "dreamer's dens" are basically long barrows, drawing from English pre-history; they can only be woken by light, basically by turning out the barrow. 

Unlike anywhere else in Cairn Dor, in these barrows there is an oppressive soporific weight. Probably something like the Sleep spell, DC 15 Wisdom. Make it an actual spell.
