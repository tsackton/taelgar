# Adventure Packet Template

## How to use this note

Use this as the home note for one adventure. Keep it short. This note is for orientation, links, current status, and open work. The actual world context belongs in the backstory note; the design logic belongs in the overview; the playable structure belongs in the Canvas scene map; details belong in scene and appendix notes.

## Ready-to-run checklist

- Backstory explains why this adventure exists in Taelgar.
- Overview defines the goal, central challenge, and likely outcomes.
- Canvas shows the adventure structure clearly enough to use in play.
- Substantial scenes have their own runnable notes.
- Nested sites or dungeons have their own inner structure.
- Important NPCs, monsters, treasure, and handouts are linked.
- Open canon questions are marked for review.
