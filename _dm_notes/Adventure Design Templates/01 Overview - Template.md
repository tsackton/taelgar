# Adventure Overview Template

## How to use this note

Use this note to make the adventure design deliberate before writing scenes. Keep it concise. This note should answer what the PCs are trying to do, why it is hard, what the adventure is mostly about, what makes it dramatic, and what different outcomes might mean.

Overall context: [True Scenario Designery Index](https://theangrygm.com/true-scenario-designery/).

## Brief overview

Summarize the adventure in a few plain sentences. What is happening, what do the PCs get drawn into, and what kind of adventure is this?
