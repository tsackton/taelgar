## Firstborn Races
 
**Elves:** elves reckon years based on ages of the world, of which the current era is the third or fourth  
**Dwarves:** dwarves reckon years from beginning of time, when Taelgar gained its own existence as a  
**Halflings:** for everyday use adopt the reckoning of nearby humans; but also have names for every year, going back to the first year, although many are buried deep in the racial memory

## Humans
 
**Drankor and descendants (Chardon, Sembara, Cymea at least):** reckon years from the founding of Drankor  
**Dunmar:** reckon years by dynasty; this is the sixth 'official' dynasty in Dunmari history (three pre-settlement of Dunmar, then the long dynasty of the horselords until the Great War, a short dynasty during the chaos, now the current dynasty)  
**Sembara:** also reckons years by regnal year
