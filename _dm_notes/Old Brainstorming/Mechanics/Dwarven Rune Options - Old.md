### Rune of Truth

_Major Rune_
 
**Passive Effect:** While this rune is on your person, you gain particular insight into the feelings and minds of Dwarves. You have Advantage Wisdom (Insight) checks to determine the true intentions of a Dwarf.
 
**Active Effect:** As an action, you may activate this rune. You create a magical zone extending in a 15 foot radius from you, and lasting for 10 minutes, that guards against deception. Until the effect ends, any creature besides yourself that enters the effect's area for the first time on a turn, or starts a turn there, must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failed save, a creature can't speak a deliberate lie while in the area of effect. You are know whether each creature succeeds or fails on its saving throw, and creatures that fail their saving throw are away of the effect.
 
Once you activate this rune, you cannot do so again until you finish a long rest.
 
### Rune of Calm

_Major Rune_
 
**Passive Effect**_:_ While this rune is on your person, you gain a degree of magical control over your thoughts. You have Advantage on saving throws against being charmed.
 
**Active Effect:** As an action, you may activate this rune. It remains active for up to 1 minute, while you maintain Concentration. When you do so, you attempt to suppress strong emotions in a group of people. Each humanoid in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on a point you choose within 60 feet must make a Charisma saving throw; a creature can choose to fail this saving throw if it wishes. If a creature fails its saving throw, choose one of the following two effects:￼

- You can suppress any effect causing a target to be charmed or frightened. When this spell ends, any suppressed effect resumes, provided that its duration has not expired in the meantime.
- Alternatively, you can make a target indifferent about creatures of your choice that it is hostile toward. This indifference ends if the target is attacked or harmed by a spell or if it witnesses any of its friends being harmed. When the spell ends, the creature becomes hostile again, unless the GM rules otherwise.
 
Once you activate this rune, you cannot do so again until you finish a long rest.
 
### Rune of Lore

_Major Rune_
 
**Passive Effect**_:_ While this rune is on your person, you gain a magical sense for the value of things. You have Advantage on ability checks to determine the worth, history, or properties of items.
 
**Active Effect:** With a ritual requiring 1 minute, you activate this rune. While doing so, you choose one item you can touch. If it is a magic item or some other magic-imbued object, you learn its properties and how to use them, whether it requires attunement to use, and how many charges it has, if any. You learn whether any spells are affecting the item and what they are. If the item was created by a spell, you learn which spell created it. At the DM's discretion, you may also learn something about the lore or history of the item.
 
Once you activate this rune, you cannot do so again until you finish a short or long rest.
 
### Rune of Life

_Major Rune_
 
**Passive Effect:** While this rune is on your person, you radiant a magical energy of rest and recovery. You have Advantage on Constitution saving throws against disease. In addition, during a long rest, up to 6 creatures you choose who are resting within 120 feet of you regain two additional Hit Dice at the end of the long rest.
 
**Active Effect:** As an action, you can activate this rune and touch a creature that has 0 hit points. That creature is stabilized. This has no effect on constructs or undead.
 
**Active Effect (requires 5th level)**_:_ As a ritual requiring 10 minutes of concentration and meditation on your rune, you cause this rune emit healing energy in a 30 foot radius. Up to six creatures you can see within this aura regain 2d8 + your Intelligence modifier hit points. Once you activate this effect, you may not do so again until you complete a long rest.
 
### Rune of Purity

_Major Rune_
 
**Passive Effect**_:_ While this rune is on your person, you have Advantage on saving throws against poison.
 
**Active Effect:** You may activate this rune, requiring 1 minute of uninterrupted time. All non-magical food or drink within a 5 foot radius sphere centered on the rune is purified and made free or poison and disease. 
 
**Active Effect (requires 5th level):** You may activate this rune as an action, and touch a creature. If it is poisoned, you neutralize one poison affecting it. For the next 1 hour, this creature has advantage on saving throws against poison, and is resistance to poison damage.
 
Once you activate this rune, you cannot do so again until you finish a long rest.
 
### Rune of Valor

_Major Rune_
 
**Passive Effect**_:_ While this rune is on your person, you have advantage on saving throws against being frightened.
 
**Active Effect:** You may activate this rune as an action, and touch one willing creature. This creature is imbued with bravery. Until the effect ends, the creature is immune to being frightened and gains temporary hit points equal to 1 plus your rune casting ability modifier at the start of each of its turns (minimum of 1). When the effect ends, the target loses any remaining temporary hit points from this rune. This effect lasts for 1 minute, while you maintain Concentration. Once you reach 3rd level, you may target 2 creatures with this effect, and once you reach 5th level you may target 3 creatures with this effect. Once you activate this rune, you may not do so again until you finish a long rest.
 
### Rune of True Meaning

_Major Rune_
 
**Passive Effect**_:_ While this rune is on your person, you gain a magical sense for the meaning of things. You have Advantage on ability checks to decipher the meaning of codes, ciphers, or text you do not comprehend.
 
**Active Effect:** As an action, you activate this rune, which remains active for 1 hour. For the duration, you understand the literal meaning of any spoken language that you hear. You also understand any written language that you see, but you must be touching the surface on which the words are written. It takes about 1 minute to read one page of text. This effect doesn’t decode secret messages in a text or a glyph, such as an arcane sigil, that isn’t part of a written language.
 
Once you activate this rune, you cannot do so again until you finish a long rest.
 
### Rune of Fire

_Major Rune_
 
**Passive Effect:** While this rune is on your person, you can manipulate the heat of a forge or fire to aid in crafting. You may add double your proficiency bonus, instead of your regular proficiency bonus, to any ability check that uses artisan's tools and requires control over the temperature of a forge or fire.
 
**Active Effect:** As an action, you may activate this rune. When you do, you choose a nonmagical flame that you can see within 60 feet and that fits within a 5-foot cube. You affect it in one of the following ways:
 
- You instantaneously expand the flame 5 feet in one direction, provided that wood or other fuel is present in the new location.
- You instantaneously extinguish the flames within the cube.
- You double or halve the area of bright light and dim light cast by the flame, change its color, or both. The change lasts for 1 hour.
- You cause simple shapes—such as the vague form of a creature, an inanimate object, or a location—to appear within the flames and animate as you like. The shapes last for 1 hour.
 
If you activate this rune multiple times, you can have up to three of its non-instantaneous effects active at a time, and you can dismiss such an effect as an action.
 
### Rune of Water

_Major Rune_
 
**Passive Effect:** While this rune is on your person, you can manipulate the properties and viscosity of water to aid in crafting. You may add double your proficiency bonus, instead of your regular proficiency bonus, to any ability check that uses artisan's tools and requires quenching or cooling material with liquid, or that uses liquids as a raw material.
 
**Active Effect:** As an action, you may activate this rune. When you do, you choose an area of water you can see within 30 feet and that fits within a 5-foot cube. You affect it in one of the following ways:
 
- You instantaneously move or otherwise change the flow of the water as you direct, up to 5 feet in any direction. This movement doesn’t have enough force to cause damage.
- You cause the water to form into simple shapes and animate at your direction. This change lasts for 1 hour.
- You change the water’s color or opacity. The water must be changed in the same way throughout. This change lasts for 1 hour.
- You freeze the water, provided that there are no creatures in it. The water unfreezes in 1 hour.
 
If you activate this rune multiple times, you can have no more than two of its non-instantaneous effects active at a time, and you can dismiss such an effect as an action.
 
### Rune of Stone

_Major Rune_
 
**Passive Effect:** While this rune is on your person, you can manipulate the properties and viscosity of earth and stone to aid in construction, mining, or similar endeavors. You may add double your proficiency bonus, instead of your regular proficiency bonus, to any ability check that uses artisan's tools and uses stone or earth as a raw material.
 
**Active Effect:** As an action, you may activate this rune. When you do, you choose a portion of dirt or stone you can see within 30 feet and that fits within a 5-foot cube. You affect it in one of the following ways:
  - If you target an area of loose earth, you can instantaneously excavate it, move it along the ground, and deposit it up to 5 feet away. This movement doesn’t have enough force to cause damage.
- You cause shapes, colors, or both to appear on the dirt or stone, spelling out words, creating images, or shaping patterns. The changes last for 1 hour.
- If the dirt or stone you target is on the ground, you cause it to become difficult terrain. Alternatively, you can cause the ground to become normal terrain if it is already difficult terrain. This change lasts for 1 hour.
 
If you activate this rune multiple times, you can have no more than two of its non-instantaneous effects active at a time, and you can dismiss such an effect as an action.

### Rune of Repair

_Minor Rune_
 
**Passive Effect:** While this rune is on your person, you gain an uncanny ability to see damage and repair it. You may add double your proficiency bonus, instead of your regular proficiency bonus, to any ability check that uses artisan's tools to repair a damaged item or object.
 
**Active Effect:** As an action, you may activate this rune. When you do so and spend a minute in concentration, you can repair a single break or tear in object you touch, such as a broken chain link, two halves of a broken key, a torn cloak, or a leaking wineskin. As long as the break or tear is no larger than 1 foot in any dimension, you mend it, leaving no trace of the former damage.  
While you can physically repair a magic item or construct, but you can’t restore magic to such an object.
