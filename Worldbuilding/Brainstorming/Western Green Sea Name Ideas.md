### Skaer Peninsulas

* **Lofnes** (_lof_ "praise, shelter" + _nes_ "headland" – could imply a sacred or protective place)
* **Skjarnes** (_skjar_ "rocky islet" + _nes_ "headland" – a rugged, rocky peninsula) or **Skjardal**
* **Torskund** (_torsk_ "cod" + _und_ "land" – evokes a coastal fishing region) or **Torsholm** or **Torskarn**
