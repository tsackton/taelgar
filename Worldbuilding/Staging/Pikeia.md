# Pikeia

Pikeia is [[Dilion|Dilion's]] living dog companion. Pikeia was taken during the events at [[Perdoli Manor]] and kept alive in [[Grimstone|Grimstone's]] storeroom beneath the manor.

After Grimstone was killed, Dilion found Pikeia tied up in the east storeroom of [[Grimstone's lair]]. The reunion was one of the final discoveries before the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] divided Grimstone's treasure and escaped the manor. Dilion left the road below the manor with both Pikeia and the [[steampup]].
