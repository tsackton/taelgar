After Grimstone was killed, Dilon found Pikeia tied up in the east storeroom of [[Grimstone's lair]]. The reunion was one of the final discoveries before the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] divided Grimstone's treasure and escaped the manor. Dilon left the road below the manor with both Pikeia and the [[steampup]].
