Vaelithar is a fey from [[Twilight's Edge]]. In DR 1715, he and his companion [[Liraene]] encountered the corrupted Singing Tree beyond the [[Nightsong Roots]], attacked it, and were caught in its roots. Vaelithar survived long enough to be cut free by the [[Addermarch Mercenaries]] after they defeated the tree.

While trapped, Vaelithar felt the tree's memories. He told the mercenaries that a mortal from the [[Material Plane]] had attacked the tree with an impossibly sharp battle axe set with a glowing green gem, cut out its heart, and left its song twisted toward anger and violence. He warned that the tree would regrow unless the stolen heart was returned.

Vaelithar did not know the mortal's identity, but directed the party toward Aurenxis, a transmuter and alchemist who had traveled widely in the [[Feywild]]. After [[Liraene]]'s body was recovered, Vaelithar took her pendant back to [[Twilight's Edge]] and gave the party a silver wooden wand and a braided silver ring set with small orange gems.
