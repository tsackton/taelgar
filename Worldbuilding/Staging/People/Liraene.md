Liraene was a fey from [[Twilight's Edge]] and the companion of [[Vaelithar]]. In DR 1715, she and Vaelithar encountered the corrupted Singing Tree beyond the [[Nightsong Roots]], attacked it, and were caught in its roots.

When the [[Addermarch Mercenaries]] defeated the Singing Tree and searched the clearing, they found two root-covered mounds. Vaelithar was still alive in one. Liraene had died in the other. After she was recovered, Vaelithar said goodbye to her and took her pendant back to [[Twilight's Edge]].
