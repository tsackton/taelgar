# Dinia
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A [[Northerners]] [[Humans|human]] (she/her)

Dinia is a Northerner bard and lore-keeper who lives in the mountains near [[Blacksilver Peak]], beyond the northern border of [[Chardon]]. She follows older northern religious traditions and is known as a guide to the glacier near [[Airion’s Floating Tower]].

%%^Campaign:none%%

## DM notes

- (DR:: 1747-07-22): The [[Silver Tempests]] encountered Dinia in a cave complex on [[Blacksilver Peak]] while searching for [[Airion’s Floating Tower]]. She offered to show them the path if they dealt with an abominable yeti troubling the mountain. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 1]]; [[Ice Landscape]].
- Dinia's DM notes describe her as an old Northerner woman living in exile, accompanied by a polar bear, with a warm cave that seemed larger inside than outside. She was a worshipper of the old northern gods. **Source:** [[Ice Landscape]].
- Dinia warned the party about glacier hazards, helped them reach the tower approach, and later provided a safe place to rest after they recovered the air scroll. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 1]]; [[Ice Landscape]]; [[Air Scroll - Return to Voltara]].
- Dinia told a story of [[Azzan]], [[Ikos]], the [[Great War]], and Chardon's abandonment of the Northlanders after the war. That story is also summarized on [[Azzan]]. **Sources:** [[Ice Landscape]]; [[Azzan]].
- A later PC note describes Dinia as a bard and member of the Whistlers, a lore-gathering organization without a current dedicated page. Treat this as useful but lightly sourced until the Whistlers are developed. **Source:** [[Nerissa]].
- (DR:: 1748-01-12): Dinia wrote that [[Airion’s Floating Tower]] vanished after a great storm and strange lights. The party received this letter after returning to [[Voltara]]. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 4]]; [[GL - Session 56 - DM Notes]].
- (DR:: 1748-07-16): [[Mabist]] woke in Dinia's cottage and recovered there for about a month. **Source:** [[Mabist]].
- (DR:: 1748-08-17): The [[Havoc Host]] attacked Dinia's home. Dinia told [[Mabist]] to flee through a secret passage and was gone when Mabist returned. **Sources:** [[Mabist]]; [[GL - Session 61 - DM Notes]].
- (DR:: 1748-08-24): Dinia was found under the psychic control of [[Ithu’rax|Ithu'rax]] in flooded caverns near [[Suwi]]. After the aboleth was defeated, she returned to herself and appointed herself caretaker of the recovering slime servants. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 4]]; [[Session 63 Background]].
- After the Suwi rescue, Dinia's precise ongoing whereabouts are not fully established. The existing frontmatter preserves her mountain home and treats the Suwi episode as a temporary captivity.
