Xameia the Forsworn was a human paladin of the [[Order of Twilight]], a religious order of [[The Night Queen]] devoted to the care and protection of those between life and death. Surviving fragments name her as Protector of Evensong and Dusk Guide of the Night Queen.

Centuries before the present day, Xameia betrayed her order for wealth, burned one of its temples, and left many of her fellow acolytes dead. She was killed for her crimes, but her soul could not rest while the temple remained desecrated.

Her tomb lies at [[Pykolon Lake]], where her curse later poisoned the waters used by nearby [[Centaurs]]. Xameia found peace in DR 1747, after the [[Order of Twilight Temple (Voltara)]] was reconsecrated and the corruption there was cleansed.

## DM notes

The backstory text in [[Xameia Backstory.png]] says Xameia was a paladin of the Night Queen who lived several hundred years ago, betrayed the order for money, and needed a shrine desecrated by fiends restored before she could rest. The [[Order of Twilight]] note adds the direct phrasing that the order protected and aided those between life and death, the sick, old, and infirm.
