Galius Filuso is a Chardonian priest of [[The Night Queen]] based in [[Voltara]]. He is a prominent advocate for the restoration of the [[Order of Twilight]], especially the recovery and reconsecration of lost Order sites in the northern provinces.

%%^Campaign:none%%

## DM notes

- Galius is described as an adventurer and traveler from [[Chardon]] who was interested in the history of the [[Order of Twilight]] and its restoration. **Source:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 1]].
- (DR:: 1747-07-04): The [[Silver Tempests]] were directed to Galius in [[Voltara]] after discovering that the [[Order of Twilight Temple (Voltara)]] held an [[Abyss|Abyssal]] portal that required powerful magic to cleanse. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 1]]; [[Session 11-13 - GL - Temple]].
- (DR:: 1747-07-06) to (DR_end:: 1747-07-07): Galius cast a 24-hour Hallow to seal the portal and reconsecrate the temple while the party defended him from demons and xvart attackers. The spell succeeded, the portal closed, and [[Xameia]] was able to rest. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 1]]; [[Session 11-13 - GL - Temple]].
- Temple DM notes suggested Galius would request the return of the temple silver and money for material components and restoration costs rather than claiming all recovered magic items. **Source:** [[Session 11-13 - GL - Temple]].
- [[Order of Twilight]] background says Galius had been scouting, recruiting, and searching for lost Order sites in the north; successful restoration would make him and the priests of the Night Queen allies able to speak on the party's behalf with the proconsul. **Source:** [[Order of Twilight]].
- (DR:: 1747-09-28): Galius sat on Voltara's war council as a representative of the [[Mos Numena]] establishment and the Order of Twilight during preparations for the [[Battle of Voltara]]. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 2]]; [[GL - Session 36 - DM Notes]].
- (DR:: 1748-03-12) to (DR_end:: 1748-03-13): Galius helped protect [[Tempest Towers]], casting a glyph after the party acquired and warded their manor. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 4]]; [[Tempest Towers]].
- Old staging notes included a D&D Beyond character sheet and approximate level 9 reference; those are mechanical references rather than world canon.
