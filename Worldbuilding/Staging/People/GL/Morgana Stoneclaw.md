Morgana Stoneclaw, later known as Morgana Frostclaw, was a fey hag whose schemes stretched from the [[Yuvanti Mountains]] to the [[Sentinel Range]]. In the Yuvanti, she preyed on the young [[Adrik]] while disguised as an elderly Dwarven woman. Years later, after leaving or being driven from the Yuvanti, she appeared in the Sentinels as Morgana Frostclaw, took control of the ice magic beneath [[Vangebekkr]], and brought unnatural cold to the southern holds of [[Am'khazar]].

The [[Silver Tempests]] killed Morgana in the ice caves beneath [[Vangebekkr]] on DR 1747-12-16. [[Adrik]] struck the final blow, taking revenge on the same hag who had haunted his childhood.

## DM notes

- Source naming is inconsistent but clearly points to the same figure: "Morgana Stoneclaw" is the name used in [[Adrik]]'s backstory and [[Cursed Cold DM Background]], while "Morgana Frostclaw" is the later name used in the [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 3|Arc 3 session record]] and [[Vangebekkr]].
- [[Adrik]]'s backstory places Morgana Stoneclaw in the [[Yuvanti Mountains]] near [[Narazara]] when Adrik was a child. She appeared as an elderly Dwarven woman, manipulated him, and was driven off by the villagers after he saw through her disguise and revealed her true form.
- Adrik believed Morgana may also have caused the pestilence that destroyed [[Narazara]] in DR 1746, but the available notes preserve this as Adrik's belief rather than a confirmed fact. **Source:** [[Adrik]].
- [[Cursed Cold DM Background]] says Morgana Stoneclaw later fled the Yuvanti mountains after being driven out by [[Agata|Agata Dustmother]], who did not want her interfering with her own plans for Dunmar. The approximate DR 1745 end date in the whereabouts chain is based on the "about two years ago" phrasing in that background note.
- After leaving the Yuvanti, Morgana traveled north into the high peaks north of the ruins of Urlich Monastery, nursed her wounds, transformed herself from an annis hag into a bheur hag, and began calling herself Morgana Frostclaw. **Source:** [[Cursed Cold DM Background]].
- As Morgana Frostclaw, she sought out the elemental ice shard at the heart of the glacier near [[Vangebekkr]], bent two hag sisters into a coven, corrupted the frost giants, and used the shard to spread unnatural cold through the southern reaches of [[Am'khazar]]. **Sources:** [[Cursed Cold DM Background]]; [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 3]]; [[Vangebekkr]].
- The notes in Morgana's workroom dated her experiments on the ice shard to roughly one year and included a bundle describing her transformation from annis hag to bheur hag. **Source:** [[Glacier Caves- DM Notes]].
- (DR:: 1747-12-16): The [[Silver Tempests]] freed imprisoned frost giants beneath [[Vangebekkr]], entered Morgana's lair, and killed her. [[Adrik]] struck the final blow, but was cursed with lycanthropy during the fight. **Source:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 3]].
