# Ithu'rax
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> An [[Aboleths|aboleth]]
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_PageDatedValue")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Ithu'rax was an aboleth active in flooded caverns near [[Suwi]] in DR 1748. It used the [[Havoc Host]] and psychically controlled servants in a series of kidnappings before it was killed.

%%^Campaign:none%%

## DM notes

- The standardized name in session notes is Ithu'rax or Ithu’rax. One prep note uses Ithu'rath when describing the Havoc Host's claim that they had been sent to bring [[Mabist]] to justice. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 4]]; [[GL - Session 61 - DM Notes]].
- In DR 1748-08, the [[Havoc Host]] attacked [[Dinia]]'s home and took her away after she helped [[Mabist]] escape. **Sources:** [[Mabist]]; [[GL - Session 61 - DM Notes]].
- The [[Havoc Host]] were also involved in kidnappings near [[Suwi]], including [[Mikel]], and were working for or under the influence of Ithu'rax. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 4]]; [[Havoc Host]].
- (DR:: 1748-08-24): The [[Silver Tempests]] followed the Suwi kidnappings upriver, entered ruins and flooded caverns near a lake, defeated Havoc Host mercenaries and slime servants, and confronted Ithu'rax in its lair. **Source:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 4]].
- During the battle, Ithu'rax used psychic control and body-warping powers; [[Dinia]] was under its control when the party arrived. After Ithu'rax was killed, Dinia returned to herself. **Source:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 4]].
- Arc 5 prep explicitly says the cleaned Arc 4 notes do not establish a direct connection between Ithu'rax, the [[Havoc Host]], the Suwi kidnappings, and the [[Umbral Covenant]] or [[Cairn Dor]]. Treat any direct Umbral connection as unconfirmed unless later play establishes it. **Source:** [[Session 63 Background]].
