# Silverstorm
>[!info]+ Biographical Info
> An [[Elementals|elemental]] (roc)
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Silverstorm is the short name of the roc called Silver Feathers of the Storm Cloud, Thunder and Lightning. The roc is associated with the northern mountains and with the recovery of a stolen roc egg from elemental conspirators.

%%^Campaign:none%%

## DM notes

- (DR:: 1747-07-26): [[Adrik]] summoned Silverstorm through a roc feather after the party escaped [[Airion's Floating Tower]]. Silverstorm carried the [[Silver Tempests]] back toward [[Voltara]] and helped defeat pursuing wyverns. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 1]]; [[Air Scroll - Return to Voltara]].
- Arc 1 DM notes say rocs live far to the north and use long names. The same note gives the name as Silver Feathers of the Storm Cloud, Thunder and Lightning. **Source:** [[Air Scroll - Return to Voltara]].
- In Arc 2, [[Brelith]] contacted Silverstorm for aid before the battle for [[Voltara]]. Silverstorm agreed only if the party recovered a stolen egg. **Source:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 2]].
- [[Theopheia]] and [[Herasto]] helped the party reach the mountains; the egg had been stolen by a dao attempting to petrify it. The party recovered the egg and Silverstorm later aided Voltara. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 2]]; [[Roc Quest - DM Notes]].
- Source inconsistency: [[Roc Quest - DM Notes]] uses the variant long name "Silver Feathers of the Storm Cloud, the Rains and the Thunder" and names a sister, Silver Talons of the Windstorm, the Grass and Air. The session and return notes support the page title used here.
