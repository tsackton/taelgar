# Theopheia
>[!info]+ Biographical Info
> A centaur (she/her)
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Theopheia is a centaur druid and the partner of [[Herasto]]. She advised Herasto's band and helped arrange magical aid during the recovery of a stolen roc egg.

%%^Campaign:none%%

## DM notes

- (DR:: 1747-09-17): The [[Silver Tempests]] met Theopheia at a centaur camp with [[Herasto]], who is identified as her partner. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 2]]; [[Herasto]].
- DM notes describe Theopheia as Herasto's wife, a druid, friendly, welcoming, devoted to nature, and accompanied by animals. **Source:** [[GL - Session 31 - DM Notes]].
- Theopheia promised aid if the party recovered Silverstorm's stolen egg, then provided a spell scroll that helped them reach the mountains. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 2]]; [[GL - Session 31 - DM Notes]].
- The centaur support connected to this quest later contributed to the defense of [[Voltara]]. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 2]]; [[Herasto]].
