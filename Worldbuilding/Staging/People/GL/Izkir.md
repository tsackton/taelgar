# Izkir
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A [[Deno'qai]] [[Humans|human]] (he/him)
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

Izkir is a Deno'qai godcaller associated with [[Bita]], the tanshi of bears. He served as a guide and divine spellcaster during the campaign against [[Nymthrax]] in the [[Blackwater Fens]].

%%^Campaign:none%%

## DM notes

- (DR:: 1748-01-29): The [[Silver Tempests]] encountered Deno'qai scouts while tracking the dragon that had displaced [[Bullywugs]] and threatened the Chardonian border. The Deno'qai council also wanted the dragon killed. **Source:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 4]].
- (DR:: 1748-02-01): After council discussion, the party left with Izkir, described in the cleaned session notes as a guide and sorcerer blessed by the [[Tanshi|tanshi]], heading for the [[Blackwater Fens]]. **Source:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 4]].
- Izkir was specifically associated with [[Bita]], the tanshi of bears. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 4]]; [[Bita]].
- During the first fight with [[Nymthrax]], Izkir's magic hastened [[Adrik]] and [[Aelar]] as they attacked the dragon. **Source:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 4]].
- DM notes for the dragon lair had Izkir raise an invisibility option during planning and included Nymthrax threatening Izkir's people directly. **Sources:** [[GL - Session 55 - DM Notes]]; [[GL - Session 54 - DM Notes]].
- After the dragon's death, Izkir thanked the party deeply and told them they would always be welcome among his people. **Source:** [[GL - Session 56 - DM Notes]].
- Older travel-zone notes list Izkir as one of the Deno'qai male names prepared for the Raha/Northerner region before the dragon arc. **Source:** [[Northerners Territory - DM Notes]].
