# Moondweller
>[!info]+ Biographical Info
> A [[Stoneborn|stoneborn]]
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Moondweller is a [[Stoneborn]] associated with a glacier village near [[Vangebekkr]]. During the cursed cold, Moondweller identified [[Morgana Stoneclaw|Morgana Frostclaw]] as the hag controlling the ice magic at the heart of the glacier.

%%^Campaign:none%%

## DM notes

- The [[Silver Tempests]] encountered Moondweller meditating in a Stoneborn village while searching for the source of the supernatural cold in the dwarven southlands. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 3]]; [[GL - Session 44 - DM Notes]].
- Moondweller explained that some frost giants had turned evil and that [[Morgana Stoneclaw|Morgana Frostclaw]] controlled corrupting ice magic from the glacier. **Sources:** [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 3]]; [[GL - Session 47 - DM Notes]].
- DM notes place Moondweller with the party on the way toward the frost giant stronghold and the first glimpse of [[Vangebekkr]]. **Source:** [[GL - Session 44 - DM Notes]].
- No source found establishes Moondweller's gender, exact village name, or later fate.
