# Petrona
>[!info]+ Biographical Info
> A woman connected to the old pirate hideout
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Petrona was the lost love of [[Jacopo Marcelli]], the pirate captain whose ghost haunted a private bedchamber in [[Perdoli Manor]]. She is known from a silver locket, a faded portrait, and love letters found by the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] in DR 1748.

The locket was inscribed to J.M. with love from P., and a folded note inside urged him to keep safe and come back. The letters suggest that Petrona wanted J.M. to abandon the pirate life and that she later left in panic after her father was caught by a navy. J.M. died without learning her fate, and Grimstone used that uncertainty to keep his ghost guarding the trapdoor to the old hideout.

Gaudin used Petrona's image to calm J.M.'s ghost. Once J.M. understood that [[Grimstone]] had lied to him, he stopped guarding the way below the manor and revealed the route to [[Grimstone's lair]].
