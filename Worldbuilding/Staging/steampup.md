# steampup
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A steel defender, companion of [[Dilion]]  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

The steampup is [[Dilion|Dilion's]] magical steel defender, built in the form of a dog-like construct. During the escape from [[Perdoli Manor]], the steampup served as scout, decoy, combatant, and companion.

At dawn on October 18th, 1748, Dilion sent the steampup east across the manor grounds to bark and draw away a hobgoblin patrol and nearby goblins. The distraction let the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] reach the formal gardens and ruined chapel. In the chapel crypt, the damaged steampup returned to the group and helped kill the hobgoblin smokebinder that followed it.

The steampup later led the party through the old pirate tunnels beneath the manor, descended into the shade-filled cavern, and survived the human guard's green petrification vial. During the final assault, it leapt across Grimstone's laboratory bench, helped fight the animated stone constructs, and left the manor with Dilion and [[Pikeia]].
