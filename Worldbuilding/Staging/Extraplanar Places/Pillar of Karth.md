# The Pillar of Karth
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Pillar of Karth is a landmark of the [[Elemental Plane of Earth]]. It is described as a towering spire of impossibly hard stone that vibrates slightly with the resonances of a hidden song of the earth.

The Pillar is said to stand at the very center of the plane. Some believe it is a remnant of the plane's creation and contains the raw essence of earth.

## Facts/questions about this thing

- Source note: [[Elemental Plane of Earth]].
- Fact: The Pillar of Karth is one of the relatively stable landmarks reported on the Plane of Earth.
- Fact: It is associated with a hidden song or resonance of earth.
- Fact: It may contain the raw essence of earth, but this is framed as belief rather than certainty.
- Question: What is Karth, and why is the Pillar named for it?
