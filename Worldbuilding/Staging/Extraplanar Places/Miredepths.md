# Miredepths
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Miredepths is a poorly described [[Energy Realms|Energy Realm]], associated in [[Airion|Airion the Mistspeaker]]'s system with mud, corruption, and the mingling of Water and Earth.

It is variously considered a compound elemental realm, mixing the [[Elemental Plane of Water]] and the [[Elemental Plane of Earth]]; a border region between those two planes; or its own unique realm of mud and corruption.
