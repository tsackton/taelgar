# The Shimmering Sea
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Shimmering Sea is a region of the [[Elemental Plane of Water]], traditionally distinguished by planar geographers as a light-filled expanse of warm waters. Extensive floating coral reefs and vast mats of tangled seagrass are commonly found there.

The famous tragedy [[The Siren's Embrace]] helped shape poetic images of the Plane of Water with a vision of a vast coral reef bursting with life beneath a shimmering sea.
