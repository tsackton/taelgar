# The Positive Energy Plane
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Positive Energy Plane is a hypothesized [[Energy Realms|Energy Realm]] associated with life. In [[The Endless Currents]], the lizardfolk mystic [[Aznoke|Aznoke]] speaks of the Plane of Blossoming Currents as the wellspring of vitality, where life flows into being like a river surging forth from its source. Some scholars in the [[Faculty of Metaphysics]] speculate that this may correspond to the Positive Energy Plane.

No planar traveler is known to have reached the Positive Energy Plane and returned safely.

## Facts/questions about this thing

- Source notes: [[Energy Realms]], [[The Endless Currents]], [[Multiverse]].
- Fact: The Positive Energy Plane is hypothesized rather than confirmed by safe planar travel.
- Fact: Aznoke's Plane of Blossoming Currents represents life as part of the Eightfold Essences.
- Fact: Some scholars connect Aznoke's Plane of Blossoming Currents to the hypothesized Positive Energy Plane.
- Question: Is the Positive Energy Plane a true Energy Realm, a poetic interpretation of life, or another kind of metaphysical phenomenon?
