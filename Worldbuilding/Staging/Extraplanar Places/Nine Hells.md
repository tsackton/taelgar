# The Nine Hells
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Nine Hells are a [[Spiritual Realms|Spiritual Realm]] and the home of [[Devils|devils]]. Tales of the Nine Hells are tales of hubris and fall: devils are said to work behind the scenes, turning the weak-willed and greedy toward evil purposes.

The archdevils of the Nine Hells exist in a rigid hierarchy of power and influence. Among devils, possession of armies of servants in the form of mortal souls is the surest way to gain strength and position.
