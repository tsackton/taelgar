# The Negative Energy Plane
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Negative Energy Plane is a hypothesized [[Energy Realms|Energy Realm]] associated with death. In [[The Endless Currents]], the lizardfolk mystic [[Aznoke|Aznoke]] speaks of the Plane of Still Waters as a tranquil, shadowed realm where the currents of life slow and pool, allowing death to cleanse and prepare for renewal. Some scholars in the [[Faculty of Metaphysics]] speculate that this may correspond to the Negative Energy Plane.

No planar traveler is known to have reached the Negative Energy Plane and returned safely. Rumors of an extraplanar weak point in the mountains south of [[Cedrano]] connect it either to a [[Shadowfolds]] domain or to the hypothesized Negative Energy Plane, but this remains unconfirmed.

## Facts/questions about this thing

- Source notes: [[Energy Realms]], [[The Endless Currents]], [[Multiverse]], [[Extraplanar Weak Point]].
- Fact: The Negative Energy Plane is hypothesized rather than confirmed by safe planar travel.
- Fact: Aznoke's Plane of Still Waters represents death as part of a cyclical natural process.
- Fact: Some scholars connect Aznoke's Plane of Still Waters to the hypothesized Negative Energy Plane.
- Question: Is the Negative Energy Plane a true Energy Realm, a poetic interpretation of death, or a misidentified Shadowfolds-related phenomenon?
