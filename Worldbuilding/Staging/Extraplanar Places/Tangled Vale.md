# The Tangled Vale
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Tangled Vale is a fey portal described in [[The Lore of the Feywild]]. It appears as a sheltered pocket of moss and small flowers within a dense brier of tangled thorns, and entering it is said to feel like stepping into a bright painting.

On the [[Material Plane]], the Tangled Vale is located in the [[Tawir Forest]], though its exact place supposedly changes. The crossing connects to [[Wildrun]], a fey realm roughly aligned with the Tawir Forest and the [[Mawakel Peninsula]].

## Facts/questions about this thing

- Source notes: [[The Lore of the Feywild]], [[Wildrun]], [[Tawir Forest]].
- Fact: The Tangled Vale is one of four reliable Feywild portals proposed by Ulfgar in [[The Lore of the Feywild]].
- Fact: The Material-side location is in the Tawir Forest, but the exact place shifts.
- Fact: The Feywild-side destination is Wildrun.
- Question: What conditions open the portal, if any? The available source identifies the crossing but does not describe activation rules.
