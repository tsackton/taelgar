The Sea of Fire is a possible landmark of the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]], described as a sea of lava, fire, and ash that supposedly spreads from the center of the plane.

## Facts/questions about this thing

- Source note: [[Elemental Plane of Fire]].

- Fact: It is described as lava, fire, and ash rather than water.
- Fact: It is said to spread from the center of the plane.
