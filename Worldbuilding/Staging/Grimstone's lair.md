# Grimstone's Lair
>[!info]+ Information
> A hidden laboratory complex in [[Perdoli Manor]]
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Grimstone's lair was a hidden laboratory complex beneath [[Perdoli Manor]]. It began as a Perdoli bolt-hole beneath the master bedroom, then was broken open and expanded by pirates into a rough tunnel and cave system. By DR 1748, [[Grimstone]] had taken over the old hideout as the center of his petrification experiments.

The lair was reached through a trapdoor in the former master bedroom, which led down into an old stone vault and then into pirate-dug passages. The lower cavern still held the burned remains and undead shades of pirates killed by the Tollender navy. Beyond it lay a guarded entrance hall, a treasure room, Grimstone's chambers, his chaotic alchemical laboratory, and a storeroom where [[Pikeia]] and stolen goods were kept.

On October 18th, 1748, the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] descended into the lair, destroyed the pirate shades, bluffed and killed Grimstone's human guard, defeated Grimstone and his animated stone constructs, recovered Pikeia, and stripped the laboratory of research and treasure before escaping the next morning.
