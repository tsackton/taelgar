# Lord Endless Ending's Manor

Lord Endless Ending's Manor is a fey manor in the [[Twilight Kingdom]], caught in the repeated ending of a party hosted by [[Lord Endless Ending]].

## Description

The manor stands behind a silver gate and a great hedgerow, with long lawns sloping toward a river, patios, open doors, bright and unevenly lit rooms, and lanterns whose candles are already burning low. The party throughout the house is always ending: glasses are empty, food is picked over, windows are left unevenly open, voices grow sharper, musicians call for one more piece, and guests linger in small groups as if departing without ever quite leaving.

The house includes a ballroom, a library, a bar and food room, a covered porch, and a prominent grandfather clock whose zero chime marks the sending of mortal guests toward the neighbor's house. Its present state began when a mortal wizard's naming contest transformed the estate from a party beginning into a party ending.
