# Rakshasa

The Rakshasa is a tiger-headed mortal guest at [[Lord Endless Ending's Manor]]. He is a massive, well-dressed tiger-man, about eight feet tall, who sits apart from the ending party on a back porch and smokes a pipe.

## Description

Unlike many guests at the party, the Rakshasa is sober, alert, coherent, and sharply self-possessed. He came to the [[Twilight Kingdom]] out of an obsession with twilight and with stories that can only be found in the moment after the past has ended but before the future has begun. In exchange for three of [[Tarek|Tarek's]] keys, he tells the story of how a mortal wizard defeated [[Lord Endless Ending|Lord Revel Beginning]] in a contest of poetry and naming, transforming the house and its lord into an endless ending.

The Rakshasa keeps his own counsel and acts for his own ends, but does not appear to lie about the bargain he describes.
