# Menestheus

Menestheus is a displaced Drankorian scholar of the [[Arithrimos Lamperum]]. He is a mathematician and geographer whose work concerns brilliant-number maps of weak places between planes and other spaces.

## Description

Menestheus came from the [[27th House]] with two or three dozen members of his order, drank at [[Lord Endless Ending|Lord Endless Ending's]] party, fell asleep in the library, and woke to find his companions gone and himself centuries out of time. He carries a satchel of papers, notebooks, and books, including works called *The Shape of Possibility* and *Brilliant Numbers*, and uses a translation ring to speak with later visitors after recognizing Old Tollish.

When he learns that [[Drankorian Empire|Drankor]] has fallen, Menestheus realizes that [[Thalestria|Thalestria's]] warnings about the empire's end may have been right. He then leads the party toward the lone door that appears to lead to the 27th House.
