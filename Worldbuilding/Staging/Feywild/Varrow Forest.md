# Varrow Forest

Varrow Forest is an old forest west or northwest of [[Tollen]], in northern [[Sembara]]. [[Alden]] led the [[Tollen Misfits]] through it on the way to [[Dandelion House]].

## Description

The forest grows older, denser, wilder, and less inhabited as one travels away from Tollen. Its trees include enormous oaks, ash, and elms in land that has not been clear-cut or farmed for a very long time. Paths fade from marked tracks to game trails and then into trackless woods, with dense canopy overhead and green-blue light on the forest floor.

As the party approached Dandelion House, wildlife grew scarce: deer tracks vanished, squirrels fell silent, and only birds high in the canopy remained evident. Near the manor, the forest gives way to overgrown walls, a ruined gate, roots underfoot, and the long-swallowed grounds of the estate.
