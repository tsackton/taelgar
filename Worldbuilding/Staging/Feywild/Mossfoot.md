# Mossfoot

Mossfoot is [[Edric|Edric's]] pony.

## Description

Mossfoot is stalwart, stubborn, loyal to Edric, and not especially brave, but capable under pressure. While traveling through [[Varrow Forest]], Mossfoot did not resist or shy away as the woods grew quieter, but became more alert and attentive to small sounds. At [[Dandelion House]], [[Colden]] took Mossfoot to the stables behind the manor, where the pony was later found to be doing fine despite the stables being crummy, overgrown, and decrepit.
