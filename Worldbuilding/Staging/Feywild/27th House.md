# 27th House

The 27th House is the neighbor's house north of [[Lord Endless Ending's Manor]] in the [[Twilight Kingdom]]. It is associated with the mortal wizard who tricked [[Lord Endless Ending]] into sending mortal guests toward it whenever the grandfather clock strikes zero.

## Description

The known approach to the 27th House is a westward footpath branching from the north road. The path runs along a narrowing promontory above cliffs that drop into deep darkness, ending not at a visible building but at a single freestanding door.

Lord Endless Ending says the center of the house contains the 27th room, whose walls are made of cold iron older than twilight. The room is locked by a glass key holding the last light of twilight; he believes that if the key is returned and broken, the sending bargain will be undone.
