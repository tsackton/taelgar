# Alden

Alden is a human servant and guide in Lord Holda's employ at [[Dandelion House]]. He led the [[Tollen Misfits]] west from [[Tollen]], through [[Varrow Forest]], and to the manor on behalf of [[Lord Holda]].

## Description

Alden is short, round, pale, quiet, and stronger than he looks. He is attentive and usually smiling, but rarely volunteers much conversation. He has a pronounced fear of fire, keeps his distance from campfires and open flame, and showed a deep, almost rapturous affection for Dandelion House and its dandelion imagery while guiding the party there.

He has a large family in the area around Dandelion House, including his cousin [[Colden]], and expected that his relatives would help restore the estate once Holda's claim was secure.
