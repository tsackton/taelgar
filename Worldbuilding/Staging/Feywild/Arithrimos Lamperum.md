# Arithrimos Lamperum

The Arithrimos Lamperum was a Drankorian magical society dedicated to maintaining the boundaries between the world, the [[Feywild]], dream, and other realms.

## Work

For centuries, the order cataloged, closed, repaired, and maintained weak places between different kinds of spaces. Its members used brilliant numbers as encoded location keys: four large numbers, each built from prime factors, could hide precise maps of planar weak points in plain sight from anyone who did not know the factors.

In the waning days of the [[Drankorian Empire]], [[Thalestria]], the head of the order, redirected its work toward permanently sealing known open breaches. [[Menestheus]], a mathematician and geographer of the order, came from the [[27th House]] with two or three dozen members of the society before becoming trapped out of time at [[Lord Endless Ending's Manor]].
