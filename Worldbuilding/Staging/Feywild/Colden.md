# Colden

Colden is [[Alden|Alden's]] cousin, one of the local relatives helping around [[Dandelion House]] after [[Lord Holda]] claimed the estate.

## Description

Colden resembles Alden: short, round, well-muscled, and somewhat pudgy, dressed in rough homespun. When the party reached Dandelion House, Alden called for him and Colden came out to take [[Mossfoot]] to the stables behind the house. He appeared competent in that role.
