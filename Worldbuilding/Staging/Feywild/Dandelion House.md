# Dandelion House

Dandelion House is an ancient late-[[Drankorian Empire|Drankorian]] manor in [[Varrow Forest]], northwest of [[Tollen]]. In 1740 DR, [[Lord Holda]] claimed the overgrown estate through an old deed that made his inheritance conditional on ending a treaty beyond a hidden door beneath the house.

## Description

The house is both majestic and decrepit: a large stone manor, hundreds of years old, with missing window panes, warped doors, vines, moss, brush, and saplings overtaking much of the structure. Despite the decay, its stonework remains solid, and magic appears to preserve parts of the building. Its facade bears a dandelion puffball motif, and its empty upper windows and open front doors can seem either like a masterpiece of late Drankorian architecture or like a face waiting to consume visitors.

The grounds include the remnants of an old wall, a ruined gate with traces of a portcullis, a long-swallowed moat, stables behind the house, upper rooms, a kitchen with magical conveniences, guest quarters, an enormous basement, and a root-covered lower chamber. A hidden door in that lower chamber leads into a vast root tunnel connected to the Feywild.
