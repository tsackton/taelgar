# Endlessly Ending's Butler

Endlessly Ending's Butler is the badger-like fey attendant of [[Lord Endless Ending]] at [[Lord Endless Ending's Manor]].

## Description

The butler appears as a humanoid badger about the size of a halfling, dressed in formal clothes and plainly responsible for the household. He presents Lord Endless Ending to guests, asks who may be presented to his lord, and helps manage the etiquette of gifts and introductions.

His surface thoughts reveal anxiety over the manor's disorder: broken glass, wine stains, interrupted duties, and the work of cleaning a household trapped in the end of a party. He also quietly prompts Lord Endless Ending when the conversation turns toward the clock, the Rakshasa, and other delicate matters.
