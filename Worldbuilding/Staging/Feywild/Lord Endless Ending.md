# Lord Endless Ending

Lord Endless Ending is the archfey host of an endlessly ending party in the [[Twilight Kingdom]]. Before a mortal wizard defeated him in a contest of poetry and naming, he was called Lord Revel Beginning and his estate was defined by a party beginning rather than a party ending.

## Description

Lord Endless Ending is very tall, elegant, and difficult to read. He wears long hair pulled back behind his neck, a velvet coat that fades from dark blue to pale blue like the evening sky, lace at his cuffs, and a simple black cane with a polished silver head. His smooth face appears almost mask-like, and prolonged attention suggests something older, wilder, and less containable beneath the face he shows.

The wizard's naming bound both the Lord and his house into the moment of an ending party. When the magical grandfather clock strikes zero, Lord Endless Ending sends another mortal guest toward his neighbor's house. He says the bargain can be undone if the glass key from the cold-iron 27th room is brought back to him and broken.
