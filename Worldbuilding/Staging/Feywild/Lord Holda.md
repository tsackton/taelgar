# Lord Holda

Lord Holda is a Sembaran human adventurer and sellsword who claimed [[Dandelion House]] through an ancient deed. He hired the [[Tollen Misfits]] to pass through a hidden door beneath the manor and convince the lord beyond it to end a long-held treaty.

## Description

Holda is a scarred, powerfully built man in his mid-to-late forties, with a grizzled gray beard and the wary bearing of an experienced fighter. He is proud, bitter, resentful, and deeply invested in becoming lord of Dandelion House. Alden warned that Holda needed patience and flattery because the estate represented the fulfillment of a lifelong desire for land, status, and command.

Holda says he spent nearly thirty years as an adventurer, sellsword, guard, mercenary, and treasure hunter around [[Sembara]], [[Tollen]], and the wider [[Green Sea]]. After killing a young dragon near Tollen in a fight that left the rest of his company dead, he received a brittle old deed from an elf archivist. The deed named him heir to Dandelion House and its treasures, but only if he brought a treaty beyond the hidden door to an end. Holda failed to persuade the lord beyond the door, then sent the party in his place and closed the door behind them despite promising to keep it open.
