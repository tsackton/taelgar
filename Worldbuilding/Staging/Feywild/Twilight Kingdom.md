 Feywild realm twenty perpetually minutes after sunset, of pale blue twilight at the horizon and darkness above. A river wends quietly through rolling grassy hills under the first few unfamiliar stars of twilight. There are within it a "dozen dozen houses" at least some of which are larger estates in a late Renaissance Italianate style. A road runs more or less north-south through it, with sunset over the hills to the west and a deeper darkness far off the the east. It is entirely empty of birds or animals or insects. There is a suggestion that guards of some kind patroll the roads to keep it safe.
