# Twilight Kingdom

The Twilight Kingdom is a [[Feywild]] realm of fixed gloaming, many distant houses, and roads between them. Its sun remains just below the horizon, leaving pale blue light at the edge of the sky and darkness above.

## Description

The realm contains a dozen-dozen houses, homes, and holes, though whether that number is literal is uncertain. Its landscape includes rolling grassy hills, a quiet river, unfamiliar first stars, scattered points of light from distant houses, and roads guarded by strange keepers who say they belong to the road rather than to any party. Near [[Lord Endless Ending's Manor]], a road runs north with sunset over the hills to the west and deeper darkness to the east.

The Twilight Kingdom is associated with things found only in twilight: lost things, places that cannot be returned to, and moments after the past has ended but before the future has begun.
