# Thalestria

Thalestria was a Drankorian wizard, [[Menestheus|Menestheus's]] master, and the head of the [[Arithrimos Lamperum]] in the waning days of the [[Drankorian Empire]].

## Work

Thalestria warned of the empire's coming end and redirected the Arithrimos Lamperum toward permanently sealing known breaches between the world, the [[Feywild]], dream, and other realms. Menestheus later treats the fall of Drankor as evidence that her warnings may have been correct.
