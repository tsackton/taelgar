Pandel is halfling, originally from Chardon, who travele drunken halfling guest at [[Lord Endless Ending's Manor]]. He and an unnamed halfling companion came from near [[Chardon]] and speak as if Chardon is still an imperial frontier.

## Description

Pandel and his companion arrived in the Feywild in the morning, rode a rabbit the size of a pony, took or "liberated" a boat, and reached [[Lord Endless Ending|Lord Endless Ending's]] party in full swing. To them, only a day has passed; to later visitors, their references suggest that they may have been in the Feywild for centuries. They describe the Lord of the Manor as serious, sad, fond of brain teasers, not someone to hug, and perhaps afraid of neighbors he claims only to respect.
