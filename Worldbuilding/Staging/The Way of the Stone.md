# The Way of the Stone
>[!info]+ Information
> A Draconic alchemical book
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

*The Way of the Stone* is a fragile alchemical book written in Draconic. It was found on [[Grimstone]]'s corpse after the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] killed him in his laboratory beneath [[Perdoli Manor]].

The book contains recipes for invulnerability, youth, immortality, and related elixirs. These recipes all require the [[Prime Arcana]], the substance Grimstone believed could be made from the right petrified living victim. Its presence in Grimstone's possession tied his prisoner-taking operation to his alchemical pursuit of "the immortality of stone."

After Grimstone's defeat, the party recovered the book along with his key, research notes, stolen goods, and laboratory supplies before escaping the manor.
