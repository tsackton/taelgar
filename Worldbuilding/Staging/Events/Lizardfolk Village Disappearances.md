# Lizardfolk Village Disappearances

The Lizardfolk Village Disappearances were a series of unexplained vanishings among [[Lizardfolk|lizardfolk]] communities in the northwestern Chardonian frontier. In DR 1737, [[Samso]] returned to [[Urkabi]] to find the village empty, with black rot clinging to the trees and polluting the streams. The only clue was the fading [[Cairn Dor Sigil|green sigil]] left on the ground.

By DR 1748, the [[Silver Tempests]] had learned of additional villages vanishing. Their investigation led through [[Castrella]], the [[Kedron]] valley, the [[Cleaver-Stone]], and ultimately [[Cairn Dor]]. There they discovered that lizardfolk dreamers had been taken into [[Cairn Dor]] by the forces of [[Yeshara]] and the [[Shemra Azem]].

On DR 1748-09-20, the [[Silver Tempests]] used Drankorian instructions at the [[~Drankorian Cleaver-Stone Fort~|Drankorian Cleaver-Stone Fort]] to lock the portal and shut down the soul compass, apparently halting further raids. In DR 1752, after defeating [[Yeshara]], they escorted surviving lizardfolk dreamers out of [[Cairn Dor]] through the [[Enderra]] portal.
