Dilon is a [[Humans|human]] artificer and battlesmith who left a life of working around animals and the sea to seek new work and discoveries elsewhere. That earlier experience gave him practical skill with tools, mechanisms, and animal care, which he continued to refine during his travels.

Dilon travels with [[Pikeia|Kaiko]], his trusted dog, and the [[steampup]], his magical steel defender. He is most comfortable on the road and in the wilderness, still looking for a place to settle.

Dilon was captured while spending a few days in Lunacosta, a coastal market town with a river harbor. After dinner at an inn, Kaiko bolted up the upriver road barking; while Dilon chased the dog, goblins shot him, bound him, threw him into a cart, and clubbed him unconscious. He woke in [[Perdoli Manor]] as one of the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]].

After escaping the manor, Dilon left with both Kaiko and the steampup, returning to the wilderness with no settled destination.
