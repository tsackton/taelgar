# Dilion
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A [[Humans|human]] (he/him), of the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]]  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

Dilion is a human tinkerer and battlesmith who escaped [[Perdoli Manor]] with the other [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] in DR 1748. He was captured by goblin raiders in [[Western Cymea]] and imprisoned in the manor's abandoned smithy before helping break through the rotten wall of the prison cell.

Dilion traveled with both [[Pikeia]], his living dog, and the [[steampup]], his magical steel defender. The steampup became central to the party's escape, serving as scout, distraction, and combatant during the manor infiltration and the descent into [[Grimstone's lair]].

Dilion used his tools and magic throughout the escape, from purifying scavenged food to sending the steampup as a decoy, setting up the chapel crypt ambush, and healing [[Acescale]] before petrification could finish taking hold. In Grimstone's storeroom, he found Pikeia tied up alive.

After Grimstone was defeated, Dilion left the manor with Pikeia and the steampup, returning to the wilderness still looking for a place to settle.
