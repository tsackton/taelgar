# Silver Amulet

The Silver Amulet was found in the same chest as the vial of stolen [[Sentient Ocean]] water and [[The Ciphered Scroll]] in the tidal cave beneath the [[North Cliffs]]. The amulet was silver and of a strange design.

The amulet was part of the cache that linked the merfolk crisis in [[Mawar Adventures Episode 01]] to the later investigation into [[Azar the Lost]], [[Airion]], [[Nimessa]], and [[Gazankoa]]. [[Wazir]] took the coded scroll from the chest to decipher it, but the session notes do not state who kept the amulet or what its design meant.
