# Golden Amulet

The Golden Amulet was carried by the fire mage who held [[Chaessenth]] captive in [[Tzingola]]. After [[Ryu]], [[Nerissa]], and [[Ander]] freed Chaessenth and killed the mage, the party noticed that the amulet matched the symbol on [[Hiyasa's Treasure Map]].

The matching symbol pointed toward the same island north of the [[Mawakel Peninsula]] that [[Chaessenth]] associated with [[Khathayi|Khathayi the Flametongue]]. Chaessenth said the fire mage wanted [[Gatza|gatza]] to speak with Khathayi, who was buried or associated with a lost tomb on that island.
