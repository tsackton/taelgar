# Papers from Yerkir-Khor's Tomb

The Papers from Yerkir-Khor's Tomb are ancient papers recovered by the [[Adventurers of Mawar]] from the [[Tomb of Yerkir-Khor]] in DR 1747. They were found in the inner sanctum, protected by yellow dust inside one of the six gem-stoppered jars behind the altar.

The papers were recovered alongside archaic coins, rough gemstones, a pearl, spoiled wine, and an obsidian jar that shattered after removal from the tomb. The party brought the papers back to [[Hamri]] for [[Wazir]] to decipher.

The papers may preserve information connected to [[Yerkir-khor]], the tomb's earth-themed cult or memorial practice, or the older pre-Mawar history of the [[Mawakel Peninsula]], but the current session notes do not give their contents.
