# Guzo Shrine Texts

The Guzo Shrine Texts are preserved books and records found in a damaged stone chest inside the [[Shrine to Guzo the Mariner]] in DR 1749. The chest held a prayer book, a saints' story book, and a ledger of prayers left by earlier visitors to the shrine.

The texts preserve important evidence for [[Mawar Confederacy|Mawaran]] saint practice at the shrine. A [[Mawar Confederacy|Mawaran]] inscription directed visitors to kneel and pray [[Guzo the Mariner|Guzo's]] ancestor prayer. When [[Nerissa]] found and sang the prayer, the hidden slab door opened, revealing the chamber where a trapped [[Marid|marid]] held the [[Song Fragment Crystal]].

The prayer book appears to preserve Guzo's ancestor prayer. The saints' story book preserves stories of Guzo and other saints. The prayer ledger records prayers and hopes left by past visitors to the shrine.
