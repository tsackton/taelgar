# Song Fragment Crystal

The Song Fragment Crystal is a crystal fragment connected to the [[Sentient Ocean]]. In DR 1749, [[Rayna]] came to [[Hamri]] after visions and songs told her that a lost part of the ocean's song was trapped beneath rock but still resting on the waves. Her vision showed [[Guzo the Mariner]], a white-sand shrine, and a fragment of song that needed to be restored.

The [[Adventurers of Mawar]] found the crystal in the buried [[Shrine to Guzo the Mariner]] in the [[North Cliffs]]. A [[Marid|marid]] had broken into the shrine and seized the crystal, triggering a blue divine protection that blocked its escape. When the party opened Guzo's hidden chamber, the marid still held the song crystal.

After the fight, [[Nerissa]] heard a faint melody of peaceful sleep and gratitude from the crystal. When the party handed it to Rayna, everyone shared a vision of an undersea home being rebuilt among kelp and fishfolk. Rayna recognized that the crystal could guide her west, and she later sailed toward [[Apporia]] and the merfolk city of [[Omi]].

The crystal belongs to the wider pattern of Sentient Ocean fragments, songs, living water, and names in [[Mawar Confederacy|Mawaran]] lore. [[Gazankoa]] and its servants appear to seek such fragments because they reveal or expose something about the Sentient Ocean.
