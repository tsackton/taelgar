# The Cairn Dor Sigil
>[!info]+ Information  
> ([[Shadowfolds]] symbol)  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

The Cairn Dor Sigil is a shimmering green mark associated with the disappearance of [[Urkabi]] and other [[Lizardfolk Village Disappearances|lizardfolk villages]] in the DR 1730s and DR 1740s. It was often seen, shimmering sickly green, in the aftermath of the disappearance of a village. 

The source and cause was not discovered until much later, when 

[[Samso]] found a fading sigil on the ground after [[Urkabi]] vanished in DR 1737. In DR 1748, the [[Silver Tempests]] found a matching greenish sigil in the chamber of the [[Cleaver-Stone]], where stone plinths and obsidian and bronze mechanisms formed part of the soul compass used by the [[Shemra Azem]] to draw dreamers into [[Cairn Dor]].
