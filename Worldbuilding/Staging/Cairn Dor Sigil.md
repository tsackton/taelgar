# Cairn Dor Sigil

The Cairn Dor Sigil is a shimmering green mark associated with the disappearance of [[Urkabi]] and other [[Lizardfolk Village Disappearances|lizardfolk village disappearances]]. [[Samso]] found a fading sigil on the ground after [[Urkabi]] vanished in DR 1737. In DR 1748, the [[Silver Tempests]] found a matching greenish sigil in the chamber of the [[Cleaver-Stone]], where stone plinths and obsidian and bronze mechanisms formed part of the soul compass used by the [[Shemra Azem]] to draw dreamers into [[Cairn Dor]].
