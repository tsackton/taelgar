Loria Underbough is a [[Halflings|halfling]] wanderer and ranger from an extended traveling family in the [[Enst]] river valley. Her family followed the usual halfling pattern of moving between cities, towns, and villages, but Loria was always more comfortable in forests and river country than in crowded settlements.

Loria was shy for a halfling and less drawn to the performing side of her family's troupe. She made herself useful behind the scenes by repairing tools, fixing costumes, building performance sets, and hunting when money was short. Those habits helped shape the ranger skills she relied on after setting out alone.

After coming of age, Loria traveled east through [[Tyrwingha]] and the lizardfolk villages of [[Latazaro]], intending to reach [[Western Cymea]] and then seek a way south toward [[Orenlas]]. She was captured on a lonely road by goblin raiders and taken to [[Perdoli Manor]], where she became one of the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]].

After escaping the manor, Loria remained uncertain about combat as a path for herself. She left the other prisoners on the mountain road and continued south toward Orenlas.
