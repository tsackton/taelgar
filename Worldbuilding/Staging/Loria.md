# Loria Underbough
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A [[Halflings|halfling]] (she/her), of the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]]  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

Loria Underbough is a halfling wanderer and ranger who escaped [[Perdoli Manor]] with the other [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] in DR 1748. She was captured by goblin raiders in [[Western Cymea]] and imprisoned in the manor's abandoned smithy before the prisoners broke free.

Loria recovered her short swords from the first dead goblin guards and became one of the party's most dangerous close-quarters fighters. She understood Goblin and used silent speech to feed lines to [[Gaudin]] during the failed prisoner-transfer ruse. When that ruse collapsed, she filled the path with fog, cut down the goblin holding her, and helped the party capture [[Folcan]].

Inside the manor, Loria helped set the chapel crypt ambushes, fought hobgoblin guards in the ruined library, and entered [[Grimstone|Grimstone's]] laboratory under invisibility before accidentally giving away the party's approach. She killed Grimstone with a decisive Zephyr Strike, then took the ring of bird calls from his treasure.

After the escape, Loria apologized for her fear and uncertainty and decided that combat was not her path unless forced on her. She left the others on the mountain road below the manor and headed south toward [[Orenlas]].
