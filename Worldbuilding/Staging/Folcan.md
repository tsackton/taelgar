# Folcan

Folcan is a goblin cursespitter and leader among the goblins who held prisoners at [[Perdoli Manor]] for [[Grimstone]]. He led the faction of goblins who accepted Grimstone's prisoner-taking arrangement as easy raiding: the goblins kept most stolen goods, turned over gold and choice treasure, and were paid in food and drink for providing one prisoner each week.

On October 17th, 1748, Folcan was drawn out by a failed prisoner-transfer ruse staged by the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]]. He ordered [[Loria]] seized after spotting the deception, then was captured alive in the fog-covered fight outside the barracks. Under interrogation, he described Grimstone's weekly visits, the hobgoblin guards, and the tunnel routes from the barracks, kitchen, and possibly the chapel toward the manor's wine cellar. He promised to return to the barracks and claim that he had handled the escaped prisoners.

Folcan's later fate is not recorded. When Grimstone failed to appear for the usual ritual on October 19th, the goblins fled the estate in the resulting confusion.
