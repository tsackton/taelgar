# The Serpentine Register

The Serpentine Register was a [[Rangers|Ranger]] record of known [[Serpentine]] weapons and their fates. [[Arvanko]] believed the local copy was lost, though another copy might survive in [[Adderfell]] or [[Sembara]]; a dead ranger at [[Serethwyn Tor]] later confirmed that the Rangers once kept such a register.
