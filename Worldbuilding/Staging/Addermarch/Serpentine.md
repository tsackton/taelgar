# Serpentine

Serpentine is a green gemstone associated with mind-altering enchantments and [[Avatus]]-era cursed weapons. It appears in the [[Serpentine Dagger]], the [[Serpentine Battleaxe]], and the mine beneath [[Serethwyn Tor]], where undead scrape or consume serpentine residue from the stone.
