# Serpentine
>[!info]+ Information  
> (material)  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

Serpentine is a pale green gemstone that is associated with necromancy and enchantment magic. 

Serpentine weapons, produced by [[Avatus]], played a major role in the wars between the [[Dominion of Avatus]] and [[Addermarch]] associated with mind-altering enchantments and [[Avatus]]-era cursed weapons. It appears in the [[Serpentine Dagger]], the [[Serpentine Battleaxe]], and the mine beneath [[Serethwyn Tor]], where undead scrape or consume serpentine residue from the stone.
