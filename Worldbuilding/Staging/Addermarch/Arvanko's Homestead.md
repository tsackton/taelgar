# Arvanko's Homestead

Arvanko's Homestead is the isolated hill-country home of [[Arvanko]], a Stoneborn ranger in western Addermarch. The site is marked by a growing stone monument to Rangers who died in western Addermarch, including [[Cadan]], who fell at [[Serethwyn Tor]].
