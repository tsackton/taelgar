# Ulgna
>[!info]+ Biographical Info
> A [[Goblins|goblin]] (she/her), reluctant agent of [[Grimstone]]

Ulgna is a goblin bonesplitter associated with the goblin occupation of [[Perdoli Manor]] in DR 1748. She led the goblin faction that disliked serving [[Grimstone]] and resented the hobgoblin authority around the manor, preferring independent raiding to Grimstone's prisoner-taking arrangement.

The [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] did not meet Ulgna directly. They first heard her name from drunk goblin guards, who complained that she did not like working for Grimstone, and later from [[Folcan]], who blamed her as cowardly and unreliable. DM records identify Ulgna's followers as many of the barracks lackeys and outside guards.

When Grimstone failed to appear for the usual ritual on October 19th, the goblins fled the manor. Ulgna's precise fate after the flight is not recorded.
