# The Patelios Family
*(pah-TAY-lee-ohs)*
>[!info]+ Information
> A chalyte oligarch family
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Patelios Family is one of the four major oligarch families that controlled [[Chardon]]'s [[chalyte]] mines, transport, and refineries. [[Chalyte Rumors from Tiberius]] identifies the Patelios alongside the [[Auratan Family]], [[Caloren Family]], and [[Veldari Family]] as one of the families behind Chardon's chalyte monopoly.

During the DR 1749 chalyte reforms, the Patelios and Veldari families were convicted of bribery, stripped of their properties, and their scions fled north to [[Voltara]]. Before those reforms, workers in Chardon associated the Patelios with unsafe refinery conditions.

## Facts/questions about this thing
- Fact: [[Session 126 (DuFr)]] records worker resentment toward the chalyte families and notes that the Patelios had a reputation for unsafe refineries.
- Fact: [[Session 129 (DuFr)]] records the Patelios conviction, property seizure, and flight of family scions to [[Voltara]].
- Question: It is not yet clear whether the family survives as an active organization in Voltara after the seizure of its Chardonian properties.
