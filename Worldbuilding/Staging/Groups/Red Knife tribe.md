The Red Knife tribe is a [[Goblins|goblin]] tribe known from an attack on travelers on the road north toward [[Voltara]] in DR 1747. During that encounter, two goblins were slain and two were spared after the travelers learned that [[Hobgoblins]] and goblins lived in the area.

## Facts/questions about this thing
- Fact: [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 1]] is the only substantive source found for the Red Knife tribe in this pass.
- Fact: The encounter occurred as [[Brelith]], [[Adrik]], [[Samso]], and [[Alton]] traveled north toward [[Voltara]] to meet Archivist [[Lyra]].
- Question: The tribe's territory, leadership, and relationship to nearby hobgoblins are not described beyond this encounter.
