# Praecanti Vigiles
*(pry-KAHN-tee VEE-gel-ays)*
>[!info]+ Information
> City mage guard of Chardon
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Praecanti Vigiles are [[Chardon]]'s public mage guard and city watch. They keep watch over strategic civic spaces such as the [[River Bridge (Chardon)|River Bridge]], and [[Chardon]] notes describe them as technically "of the people."

They are distinct from the Praecanti Primis, the Magistros' personal mage guard. The [[Mitus Verina Auratan|Magistros]] commands both the Praecanti Vigiles and the Praecanti Primis, while the [[Hetaeri Magica]] coordinates common rules with the Vigiles as the imperial mageguard.

## Facts/questions about this thing
- Fact: [[Chardon]] identifies the Praecanti Vigiles as the mage guard or city watch and places them under the Magistros' command.
- Fact: [[Hetaeri Magica]] says the Hetaeri coordinates rules with the imperial Mageguard, the Praecanti Vigiles.
- Question: The ranks, leadership, and practical division of authority between the Vigiles, the Primis, and ordinary civic bureaucracy are not yet fully detailed.
