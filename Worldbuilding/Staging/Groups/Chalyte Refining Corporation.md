The Chalyte Refining Corporation is a Chardonian chartered corporation created in DR 1749 to centralize legal [[chalyte]] refining in [[Chardon]]. The [[Mitus Verina Auratan|Magistros]] claimed monopoly rights over chalyte refining for the corporation after the collapse of the old chalyte order, with control divided between the [[Windcallers]] and chalyte workers.

Its settlement gave equal board seats to the [[Windcallers]] and the [[Guild of Chalyte Workers|workers' guilds]]. The Magistros appointed members on [[Kenzo]]'s advice and held a tie-breaking vote, while agreeing to abstain from profit-sharing decisions. The corporation took control of three of Chardon's safest and highest-quality refineries, with profits to be reinvested under board governance.

- Fact: [[Session 129 (DuFr)]] is the main source for the corporation's creation, board structure, and control of selected refineries.
- Fact: [[Windcallers]] and [[Hetaeri Magica]] both place the corporation in the larger reorganization of Chardon's chalyte trade after [[Fausto]]'s fall.
- Question: The corporation's long-term legal status and relationship to the [[Imperial Chalyte Mining Company]] are not yet described outside the immediate DR 1749 reforms.
