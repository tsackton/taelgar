# The Guild of Chalyte Workers

The Guild of Chalyte Workers represents workers in [[Chardon]]'s [[chalyte]] refining trade. It emerged as a formal power during the DR 1749 chalyte reforms, when [[Kenzo]] pressed for worker rights and durable worker representation in the new [[Chalyte Refining Corporation]].

Under the settlement, the guilds held equal seats with the [[Windcallers]] on the corporation's board. This gave chalyte workers a formal place in governing Chardon's refineries after the old oligarchic order was broken.

## Facts/questions about this thing
- Fact: [[Session 129 (DuFr)]] says [[Kenzo]] helped establish and cement the guild's power during the weeks after the new corporation was announced.
- Fact: [[Hetaeri Magica]] lists the guild alongside the [[Windcallers]] and [[Chalyte Refining Corporation]] as part of the new imperial chalyte order.
- Question: The guild's internal structure, leaders, and membership rules are not described in the available notes.
