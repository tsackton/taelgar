# The Veldari Family
*(vel-DAR-ee)*
>[!info]+ Information
> A chalyte oligarch family
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Veldari Family is one of the four major oligarch families that controlled [[Chardon]]'s [[chalyte]] mines, transport, and refineries. [[Chalyte Rumors from Tiberius]] identifies the Veldari alongside the [[Auratan Family]], [[Patelios Family]], and [[Caloren Family]] as one of the families behind Chardon's chalyte monopoly.

In the investigations and reforms around DR 1749, the Veldari were especially implicated in the bribery that helped [[Fausto]] gain covert control of Chardon's chalyte market. Soon afterward, the Patelios and Veldari families were convicted of bribery, stripped of their properties, and their scions fled north to [[Voltara]].

- Fact: [[Session 126 (DuFr)]] names the Veldari in connection with bribery and the chalyte market crisis.
- Fact: [[Session 129 (DuFr)]] records the Veldari conviction, property seizure, and flight of family scions to [[Voltara]].
- Question: It is not yet clear whether the Veldari continued refinery operations from Voltara or were fully broken by the DR 1749 reforms.
