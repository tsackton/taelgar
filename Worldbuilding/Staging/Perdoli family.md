# The Perdoli Family
>[!info]+ Information  
> A [[Cymea|Cymean]] family  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

The Perdoli family were a wealthy Cymean merchant family associated with [[Western Cymea]] and the old winemaking trade around Vistalba. In DR 1479, they built [[Perdoli Manor]] as a country villa in the hills, drawing their wealth from wine, trade, and control of a marble quarry.

The family maintained private archives, business records, portraits, and a private chapel dedicated to [[The Mother]], patron deity of the winemakers' guild. Their old records survived in a hidden archive beneath the ruined manor and showed winemaking, mercantile shipping, double books, and stored wealth.

The Perdoli family declined after the [[Great War]], lost much of its wealth, and eventually sold the villa. The estate passed to later owners who could not maintain it, and by the late sixteenth century the manor had fallen into ruin.
