# Grimstone
>[!info]+ Biographical Info
> A masked alchemist (he/him)
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Grimstone was a masked alchemist who operated from a hidden laboratory beneath [[Perdoli Manor]] in [[Western Cymea]]. He dressed in heavy black robes and a white marble death mask, and at least one hobgoblin called him Lord Rockbinder. The name Grimstone came from the mask.

By DR 1748, Grimstone controlled goblin raiders, hobgoblin guards, a chained basilisk, and animated stone constructs. The goblins captured travelers and held them in the estate's abandoned smithy, then delivered one prisoner each week into the manor. Grimstone expected many victims to arrive petrified, and his research notes show that he believed living victims turned to stone might yield the [[Prime Arcana]], a substance needed for elixirs of invulnerability, youth, and immortality.

On October 18th, 1748, the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] reached Grimstone's lair through the old pirate hideout below [[Jacopo Marcelli]]'s room. After failed attempts to lure him out, they attacked him in his laboratory. Grimstone fought with fire, poison smoke, animation potions, a stone spider swarm, and a stone wasp whose poison nearly petrified [[Acescale]]. [[Loria]] killed Grimstone with a decisive _Zephyr Strike_, and the party destroyed his constructs before recovering his research, key, stolen treasure, and [[Pikeia]].
