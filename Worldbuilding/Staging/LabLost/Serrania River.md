# The Serrania River
*(Seh-rrah-nee-ah)*
>[!info]+ Information
> A river in [[Western Cymea]]
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Serrania River is a rushing, rocky river in [[Western Cymea]], flowing from the [[Plataca Hills]] to the [[Western Gulf]]. Its lower reaches are the easiest part of the river to travel: only the first ten miles or so from the coast are easily navigable.

[[Lunacosta]] stands at the river's mouth, where the Serrania reaches the coast and forms a useful harbor and market point. Farther inland, small villages such as Castelluccio lie along the river and scrape by on agriculture, making the Serrania one of the local anchors for settlement between the hills and the gulf.
