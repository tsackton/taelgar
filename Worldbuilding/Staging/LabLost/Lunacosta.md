Lunacosta is a coastal market town in [[Western Cymea]], located at the mouth of the Serrania River on the [[Western Gulf]]. Its harbor makes it a natural gathering point for river traffic, coastal trade, and travelers moving between the coast and the roads inland.

The town is modest but active by local standards, with inns and markets that draw farmers, fishers, sailors, and visitors from the surrounding countryside. It is less prominent than [[Vistalba]], but its position at the river mouth gives it practical importance along this stretch of coast.
