# Vistalba
*(VEESS-tahl-bah)*
>[!info]+ Information
> A trading port in [[Western Cymea]]

Vistalba is the largest town in its part of [[Western Cymea]], a trading port on the [[Western Gulf]] with a decent harbor. The harbor dates to [[Drankorian Empire|Drankorian]] days, when Vistalba was richer and handled the movement of Cymean wine, silver, and other goods.

The town has declined from that earlier prosperity, leaving it with a run-down, lost-in-the-past character. Even so, its old harbor and trading history still make it one of the main reference points for the surrounding coast and hill country.

[[Perdoli Manor]] lies about a day's journey inland from Vistalba, in the hills where the [[Perdoli Family]] once drew wealth from wine, trade, and control of a marble quarry.
