# Jacopo Marcelli
>[!info]+ Biographical Info
> A pirate captain's ghost (he/him)
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Jacopo Marcelli was the pirate captain whose ghost haunted a private bedchamber in [[Perdoli Manor]]. His initials, J.M., appeared on a silver locket and love letters found in the nearby study, where pirate maps and smuggling papers preserved traces of the manor's old use as a hideout.

In life, Jacopo loved [[Petrona]], who urged him to return safely and abandon the pirate life. He died without learning her fate, and his ghost remained bound to the bedroom. [[Grimstone]] exploited that grief by promising news and rest, then used him as a guard for the trapdoor to the old hideout beneath the room.

On October 18th, 1748, the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] reached the room while exploring the manor. Gaudin used magic to take Petrona's appearance and convince Jacopo that Grimstone had lied to him. Jacopo refused to keep guarding the way below, revealed the trapdoor to [[Grimstone's lair]], warned that Grimstone raved about the immortality of stone, and faded away.
