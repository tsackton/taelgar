# Elven Treaty Stones

The Elven Treaty Stones are ancient stone markers associated with old agreements between [[Elves|elves]] and humans. One such stone in the [[Blackwater Fens]] had fallen on its side before the [[Silver Tempests]] repaired it during their hunt for [[Nymthrax]]. The stone was about 60 feet high and marked friendship between elves and humans.
