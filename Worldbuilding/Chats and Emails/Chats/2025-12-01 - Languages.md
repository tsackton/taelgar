[2025-12-01 05:59 PM] Deciusmus: A clean worldbuilding explanation for Taelgar
    
    Here’s a strongly coherent narrative you can adopt:
    
    1. Old Zimkovan (Northros) was the ancestral language.
    Its old heroic names, river names, and mythic terms still appear in ritually preserved contexts.
    
    2. During a period of Eastros influence (migration, empire, or religious conversion), Eastros names became highly prestigious.
    The upper classes began naming children with Baltic-like names.
    
    3. Within 2–3 generations, naming fashion shifted almost completely.

    4. Modern Zimkovan today has:
    
    personal names: overwhelmingly Eastros (Baltic-like)
    
    toponyms: mixed — some ancient Northros survivals, some Eastros later names
    
    vocabulary: 25–30% old Northros substrate
    
    grammar: still largely Northros-derived
    
    identity: strongly Zimka, tied to ancestry not language
    
    This is historically very realistic.
