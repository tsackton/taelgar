## Recap View

### recap-001 — Portents and the Fall

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: beat-001
- Top Level: Months of worsening signs around the Great Chasm culminate in the party being dragged through a white storm with only the gear they can cling to.
- Intermediate: A prologue tracks the chasm's escalating omens and the disappearance of the Vejo Vaikai expedition sent with Kemeko warriors. The party is then swept into darkness and forced to choose what it can keep while falling.
- Long: Impossible weather, stranger portents, and the vanished investigators have already made the Great Chasm a source of dread before anyone falls. Against that backdrop, the party and the terrified woman later identified as Kalima are seized by a blinding white storm and carried away, clutching only a few possessions before the fall ends somewhere unknown.

### recap-002 — Strangers on the Ice

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: beat-002
- Top Level: The party wakes on a floating ice platform, sizes each other up, and tries to calm the terrified woman before Eolo provokes the guardians.
- Intermediate: The group finds itself on a platform suspended in blackness beneath a howling white storm, with frozen wolves guarding the only visible bridges away. Introductions begin, Finnan briefly soothes Kalima, and Ekko immediately senses that whatever this place is, it does not feel like the chasm he knows.
- Long: The platform proves to be an enclosed, hostile threshold rather than a normal reach of the chasm. As the adventurers introduce themselves and try to get a few words from the terrified woman, the two frozen wolves at the bridge mouths become the obvious danger. The tension breaks when Eolo walks up to inspect one of them and triggers the fight.

### recap-003 — Ice Wolves Awaken to Darkness and Final Blows

- Kind: combat
- Beat IDs: beat-003, beat-004, beat-005
- Enemies: ice wolf guardians, wind-shades, chasm wisps
- Top Level: The frozen wolves awaken, wind-shades and chasm wisps join the fight, and the party barely holds the platform long enough to win.
- Intermediate: Eolo's approach wakes the ice wolf guardians and the opening exchange is rough, with him mauled and the group scrambling into formation. The battle then worsens as wind-shades blow in, Kalima is dropped, light fails, and chasm wisps burst from the shattered wolves before the party revives her and clears the field.
- Long: One continuous platform battle escalates in waves rather than breaking cleanly. The ice wolf guardians establish the danger of the slick edge and close-quarters fighting, then the wind-shades turn the platform into a psychic and positional nightmare while Kalima collapses in the middle of it. By the time the chasm wisps are loose and the light is failing, the fight has become a chaotic cleanup under bad visibility, but the party eventually kills everything still moving and wins a narrow foothold.

### recap-004 — Kalima Names the Labyrinth

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: beat-006
- Top Level: After the fight, Kalima explains that they are in Zeyfa's Labyrinth and that the only way out lies deeper in.
- Intermediate: Finnan and Justas coax a fragmented history from her: her people hid here generations ago after an ancient cataclysm, but the place became Zeyfa's domain and now demands appeasement. Justas steadies her with Romil's token even as she insists ordinary prayers do not reach this place.
- Long: With the immediate danger past, Kalima becomes the first real source of truth about where they are. She names the place as Zeyfa's Labyrinth, describes her people as survivors of an ancient flight into this region, and insists that no one truly escapes once taken inside. Her fear is not abstract: the only hope she can offer is that the beast deeper in the maze somehow matters to getting out, even while she believes the Kestavo cannot hear prayers here.

### recap-005 — Stories on the Platform

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: beat-007
- Top Level: With the immediate danger gone, the party trades proper introductions and compares what each of them knows about the chasm, Kemeko, and the missing expedition.
- Intermediate: Jrain explains that she was escorting Kalima to people who could understand her, while Ekko connects the mystery to his mentor Eidrikas and the vanished Vejo Vaikai party. The group also tests whether this place is truly the chasm and shares sharply different views of Kemeko and the Kestavo.
- Long: The conversation shifts from survival to orientation. The companions finally explain why they were near the Great Chasm, how Kalima entered the story, and why the missing investigation matters personally to several of them. At the same time, they still do not agree on what this place is, as Ekko insists it feels wrong for the chasm while the others compare what they know of Kemeko, the Vejo Vaikai, and the local small-gods tradition.

### recap-006 — Rest, Recon, and Descent

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: beat-008
- Top Level: The group takes a short rest, confirms the void below cannot be flown, scouts the far side of the bridges, and commits to entering the maze with Kalima.
- Intermediate: They press Kalima for a little more, rest, and watch the storm above grow more hostile while Finnan finds no clear magic in the environment. Experiments with lighted ice, a failed leap into the updraft, and Rala's scouting all point the same way: the only practical route is through the snowy tunnels ahead.
- Long: Information turns into decision. The party learns a little more about sacrifice, the beast, and the maze's indifference to their route, then verifies by experiment that the apparent exits are deceptive: the void below is sealed by violent wind and the bridges lead only toward deeper chambers. Once Rala confirms a snow-filled room with branching tunnels ahead, they persuade Kalima to come between Finnan and Justas and jump into the labyrinth proper.
