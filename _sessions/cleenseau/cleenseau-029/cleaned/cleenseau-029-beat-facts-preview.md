## Dragon Rumors East
*b02*

**Date**: unknown
**Time Window**: unknown
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: Vahaiya brings news of a marauding dragon, and the party rides east to Marwick to assess the threat and volunteer to face it.
**Long Summary**: In high summer, Vahaiya warns the party that a dragon is ravaging the border between Tyrwingha and Addermarch and may be linked to an older, more terrible draconic threat from the Great War. With local rulers short on troops and the broader southern crisis tying down the Sembaran response, the party decides to intervene. They travel east to Marwick, where they find a half-emptied stronghold, learn how the dragon has been destroying the summer villages, and confirm that it is dangerous but not the ancient terror Vahaiya feared.

## Kobolds Take the Cow
*b04*

**Date**: unknown
**Time Window**: evening
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: The party intercepts a kobold foraging group, talks them into surrender, and turns their grudging testimony into an ambush plan for the dragon.
**Long Summary**: Using cows and the concealed ballista as bait, the party waits in a ruined village and catches a small kobold group sneaking in ahead of the dragon. Viepuck and Celyn help force a surrender, and the party teases out a surprising amount of information from the captives: the kobolds were driven north from the south by the same ash-cloud disaster now unsettling the realm, their dragon commands several rival factions, and not all of them are eager to die for its glory. By the end of the conversation Robin has persuaded the least enthusiastic kobolds to stay nearby and support a trap when the dragon returns.
**Location**:
- ruined mountain village near the dragon's lair: the baited ambush site with concealed ballista and cow decoy
  Note: Most of the beat is a tense interrogation scene at the ruined village.
**NPCs**:
- the winged kobold leader (encountered): captured spokesperson. Talkative, proud of her wings, and more pragmatic than openly loyal.
- the winged kobold's sister (encountered): captured second-in-command. More patient and less impressed by her sister's authority.
- Vahaiya (mentioned): earlier warning source. The kobolds confirm that the feared southern dragon was reportedly destroyed by an ash-cloud horror.
