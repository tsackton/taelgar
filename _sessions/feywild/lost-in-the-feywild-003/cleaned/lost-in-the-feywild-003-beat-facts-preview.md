## Monetheus And The Door
*B08*

**Date**: 1740-10-05
**Time Window**: evening
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: Monetheus realizes he is centuries out of time, explains Telestria's boundary work and brilliant-number maps, and leads the party toward the lone door.
**Long Summary**: When the party asks when Monetheus is from, he realizes Drankor has been gone for centuries and that Telestria may have been right about the empire's end. He explains that his society works to maintain the porous walls between the world, the Feywild, dream, and other spaces, and that Telestria redirected their order toward closing known holes as the emperor's project consumed more and more resources. He names his master as the wizard Telestria and his order as Erythromos Limperum, then launches into brilliant numbers: encoded coordinate systems that can hide maps of weak points between spaces in plain sight. Tarek slips Monetheus's book back into his satchel, Monetheus admits he is a guide rather than a containment worker, and he says he came with a large group in the year that should have been late in the empire. After learning the current date and the empire's fall, he presses onward toward the 27th house; the party follows him down the north road to a westward footpath and a single door on a promontory, while Lord Endless Ending speaks a distant poem urging them to bring back the key and set him free.
