#### Short
The party descends from the entrance bridge into Zeyfa's Labyrinth, steadies Kalima, and tests the first branching tunnels as the place begins to feel unnervingly alive.

#### Intermediate
After catching breath from the earlier fight, the party helps Kalima down from the icy bridge and enters the first chamber of Zeyfa's Labyrinth. The early tunnels branch, dead-end, and already feel more awake than any ordinary reach of the Great Chasm.

#### Long
After the short rest, the party regroups on the icy bridge above the labyrinth's entrance chamber while Kalima clutches Romil's token and prays against the snow. They ease her down, set their light and marching order, and start probing the first forked tunnels. The early passages already feel wrong: the stone is familiar enough, but the place itself seems active rather than abandoned as they press inward.

#### Short
A warm chamber split by a dark fissure halts their advance, and the rising wind turns their experiments at the crack into a dangerous commitment to cross.

#### Intermediate
The next room opens around a wide crack breathing warm air, with ice ferns trembling at the edge. As light tests and scouting worsen the swirling wind, the fissure stops looking like a curiosity and starts looking like the only road forward.

#### Long
The branching tunnels open into a broad chamber split by a dark fissure, with warm air rising from below and ice ferns bending in the draft. The party tests the space with light, distance, and scouting, but the room answers with harder wind and thicker snow. By the time Eolo slips on his leap and nearly goes over the edge, the group is no longer choosing whether to cross so much as figuring out how to survive it.

#### Short
Flight, rope, and stubborn strength carry everyone over the gap, only for the far passages to close in with howling wind, shifting exits, and ice growing underfoot.

#### Intermediate
They improvise a crossing with flight, levitation, rope, and brute force, and Justas briefly drops into hot, lightless blackness inside the gap. On the far side, the storm presses in harder as corridors shift, visibility falls, and fresh ice forms under every step.

#### Long
Getting across takes every trick they have: Eolo ferries companions, Jrain levitates to help with Kalima, and Justas trusts rope and strength more than caution. When he drops into the crack, the gap proves stranger than expected, full of heat and sudden blindness before he is hauled back up. The reward for crossing is no safety at all, only tighter passages, shifting exits, and ice that grows under their boots as the storm drives them deeper.

- NPCs: Unnamed man of Melusa, Kalima

#### Short
In a frozen side chamber, a dead villager from Melusa speaks of sacrifice, Zeyfa, and a soul still trapped in the maze before the party takes what useful tokens it can.

#### Intermediate
A corpse pinned in ice proves worth the pause when Speak with Dead draws out Melusa's name, the village's sacrificial bargain, and the dead man's trapped wandering. Kalima confirms the place, Finnan ties it to the old name Melua on the Teft, and the party takes a ring, torches, daggers, and arrows before moving on.

#### Long
A small frozen side chamber interrupts the storm with a corpse pinned to the wall, and Finnan spends his scroll to make the dead speak. The man names Melusa, says he entered as the village's chosen sunrise sacrifice, and describes a cliffside settlement fed by a river and haunted by the same maze that now traps them. Kalima recognizes the name at once, Finnan connects it to the older name Melua on the Teft, and the party leaves with a plain silver ring and the few pieces of gear the cold has not ruined.

### recap-005 | The Looping White Maze

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: beat-005

- Locations: Zeyfa's Labyrinth
- NPCs: Kalima, wind-creatures
- Organizations: none

#### Short
Whiteout corridors and looping rooms reduce the maze to a tightening trap, Kalima is hurt in the wind, and the party finally slips past the shadowy hunters through the last remaining exit.

#### Intermediate
Beyond the corpse chamber, booming passages and fingered snow hubs keep folding back into the same blizzard room while wind-creatures sweep through the whiteout. Kalima is dropped and bruised in the crossing, Justas heals her, and the group finally uses stealth magic and patience to edge through the last opening.

#### Long
The way onward turns into the labyrinth at its cruelest: booming gusts, narrow passages, and looping snow rooms that seem to erase their own exits. Kalima is dropped and badly bruised when the kenku try to carry her through the wind, and Justas has to heal her before forcing a slower passage onward. Shadowy wind-creatures herd the group from room to room until Finnan's ink mark proves the loop, repeated circuits strip the maze down to one remaining path, and stealth magic finally gets them through.

#### Short
A collapse drives everyone down a black chute into a stormless chamber, where Kalima hides and the shaggy white beast finally answers Justas's challenge.

#### Intermediate
A quieter passage leads only to another hazard: the booming gap returns, a curtained ice tunnel collapses under Ekko's fire, and an avalanche leaves darkness as the only escape. The slide spills them into a black-stone chamber, where Kalima cries out that the beast is here just before shaggy shapes begin forming in the ice.

#### Long
The calm beyond the loop lasts only long enough to lead them back to the booming threshold and into a round snow chamber with a right-hand tunnel draped in hanging ice. Ekko's bonfire shatters the veils, collapses the walls, and sends an avalanche after them, leaving a blind jump into darkness as their only way out. The slide ends in a stormless black-stone chamber, where Kalima hides behind Finnan's illusion and a towering white beast emerges while shadowy shapes gather in the ice around the party.
