[u0005] The party battles darkmantles for [[uligium]] and travels back towards [[Roscombe]].
[u0006] ## Timeline
[u0007] - **(DR:: 1715-05-14), morning:** Over fried-mushroom pancakes in **[[Zibzig Sparkscale]]’s** lair, the kobold inventor shares directions to an [[uligium]] cache and warns of “lurkers above the water.” - **(DR:: 1715-05-14), midday:** Reaching a pool-filled cavern beyond a chasm, the party is plunged into magical darkness as four **darkmantles** drop from the ceiling; [[Yvan Greenrabbit|Yvan]]’s fiery surges, [[Fazoth de Brune|Fazoth]]’s lightning-guided pseudodragon, and [[Drou]]’s tenacity shatter the creatures.
[u0008] Heated glass lures fresh **[[uligium]]** from the tunnel walls.
[u0009] - **(DR:: 1715-05-14), late afternoon:** Guided back to the surface route, they pacify the ogre and wolf at the cave mouth with [[Fazoth de Brune|Fazoth]]’s _Calm Emotions_ and escape unharmed, bearing four vials of [[uligium]].
[u0010] - **(DR:: 1715-05-14), night:** The heroes trek eastward and camp in open farmland en-route to [[Roscombe]].
[u0011] - **(DR:: 1715-05-15), evening:** After a day’s march and a stop in [[Brantor]] to deliver their alchemical prize, the party arrives safely in **[[Roscombe]]**.
[u0012] ## Cast of Characters
[u0013] - **[[Drou]]** – [[Elves|elven]] bard-monk, hand-to-hand specialist in the dark - **[[Fazoth de Brune]]** – [[Humans|human]] warlock-alchemist; pacifies foes, guides lightning - **[[Yvan Greenrabbit]]** – [[Halflings|halfling]] wild-magic sorcerer whose heat and fire attract [[uligium]] - **[[Zibzig Sparkscale]]** – chatty kobold “Master of Light & Shadow,” breakfast host and guide - **Ogre & Wolf** – dim-witted cave sentries calmed during the escape - **Darkmantles** – ceiling-dwelling ambushers defeated at the [[uligium]] pool
[u0014] ## Places
[u0015] - **[[West Stonewolds Caves]]** – sprawling subterranean network and [[Zibzig Sparkscale]]'s home - **[[Stonewolds]]** – rolling hills and grazeland around [[Roscombe]].
[u0016] ## Narrative
[u0017] ### Friday, May 14th, 2 Robert I
[u0018] We begin in the morning, as the party wakes to the smell of fried mushroom pancakes and a smoky tea being prepared for breakfast by [[Zibzig Sparkscale]].
[u0019] The kobold wizard is in a chatty mood, discussing the magic that he will invent, with time, and asking for help for memorable names for the spells he will be discovering.
[u0020] Grateful for the party's help with the [[Addermarch - Session 09|cave fisher the day before]], Zibzig gives them directions to another source of [[Uligium]] he knows of, and the way out of the cave.
[u0021] He does warn them that the [[uligium]] is defended by strange predators who lurk over the water.
[u0022] The party follows the directions, finding their way to a cavern with a chasm to one side, across which lies a still pool of water and a small tunnel.
[u0023] As the party approaches, suddenly impenetrable blackness falls as the party attacks the darkmantles.
[u0024] Four of them detach from the ceiling to attack; [[Yvan Greenrabbit|Yvan]] surges with magical power as he fires bolts of fire blindly into the darkness.
[u0025] Meanwhile lightning crackles from [[Fazoth de Brune|Fazoth]], as his pseudodragon uses its blindsight to aid him in the fight.
[u0026] [[Drou]] struggles to get a purchase on the enemy in the dark, but eventually the darkmantles are defeated.
[u0027] With magical energy still ricochetting around [[Yvan Greenrabbit|Yvan]], he approaches the tunnel, heating a glass bottle with his hand and attracting the [[Uligium]].
[u0028] Following [[Zibzig Sparkscale]]'s directions, the party returns to the cave entrance without trouble.
[u0029] At the entrance, the ogre and his wolf still stand guard, but [[Fazoth de Brune|Fazoth]]'s _Calm Emotions_ spell keeps them indifferent to the party for a moment as they flee out of the cave.
[u0030] With their four vials of [[uligium]], the party heads back torwards [[Roscombe]], traveling for the whole day before camping in the fields.
[u0031] ### Saturday, May 15th, 2 Robert I The party spends the day traveling, arriving back at [[Roscombe]] in the evening.
