[u0003] Starting from their resting place in the upper mine chambers, the party finds a ventilation shaft into deeper levels, survives a careless fall, and fights serpentine ghouls as they press deeper into the mines below Serethwyn Tor.
[u0004] ## Timeline
[u0005] - (DR:: 1715-05-27), **morning**: After a long rest in the upper mine, the party backtracks through Duncan's camp and finds a ventilation shaft.
[u0006] - (DR:: 1715-05-27), **midday**: The party enters the mine, defeats serpentine ghouls, and descends to the lowest levels following signs of Duncan's passage.
[u0007] ## Cast of Characters
[u0008] - **[[Yvan Greenrabbit]]** - Halfling sorcerer whose spells and a decisive fireball help break the second ghoul fight.
[u0009] - **[[Drou]]** - Elven bard-monk who takes the lead on the rope, calls on the Staff of the Python, and brings _Moonbeam_ to bear in the deepest tunnels.
[u0010] - **[[Fazoth de Brune]]** - Warlock whose _Witch Bolt_ and quick _Misty Step_ keep the party moving in the tight passages.
[u0011] - **[[Duncan of Haldrenn]]** - Missing shepherd; the party follows evidence of his movements into the lowest levels.
[u0012] - **Fazoth's pseudodragon** - Scout used to probe the ventilation shaft and harry enemies, though its poison proves ineffective.
[u0013] - **Serpentine ghouls** - Undead encountered on multiple levels, drawn to serpentine residue in the stone.
[u0014] ## Places
[u0015] - **[[Serethwyn Tor]]** - The hill-fort above the mine, where the party climbs to locate an alternate route downward.
[u0016] - **Ventilation shaft** - A vertical route into the lower levels of the mine.
[u0017] - **Lower mine levels** - Tight, rubble-choked tunnels where serpentine ghouls lurk .
[u0018] ## Narrative
[u0019] ### Thursday, May 27th, 2 Robert I
[u0020] After a long rest in the upper chambers, the party backtracks out of the mine, and scales the hill above the mine entrance, locating a ventilation shaft into the lower works.
[u0021] Fazoth's pseudodragon scouts ahead and spots serpentine ghouls below.
[u0022] The descent goes badly: a poorly anchored iron spike pulls free under the combined weight of Drou and Yvan, sending both tumbling down, and forcing Fazoth to descend with _Misty Step_.
[u0023] The ghouls are dispatched, though Drou's _Shatter_ rings through the rock and announces the party's presence to whatever waits below.
[u0024] They burn the bodies and pocket roughly 300 silver pieces before pressing on.
[u0025] Following the trail of Duncan's passage through scattered equipment and bodies that mercifully stay down, they push to the lowest level.
[u0026] Here, ghouls lick serpentine from the walls and turn to attack in the cramped tunnels.
[u0027] The fight is brutal.
[u0028] Drou's summoned constrictor snake absorbs punishment and is briefly paralyzed, while she works with daggers, fists, and _Starry Wisp_.
[u0029] Fazoth's _Witch Bolt_ crackles effectively, and Yvan seals the battle's end as he steps forward into darkness and launches a _Fireball_ that blasts the next wave ghouls moving towards the noise of combat.
[u0030] In the silence that follows, a rhythmic thudding echoes from the passage ahead; with the dungeon fully alerted and no safe rest in sight, the party advances as our session ends.
