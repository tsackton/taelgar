[u0019] ## Narrative
[u0020] ### Wednesday, May 26th, 2 Robert I
[u0021] We begin in the late afternoon, as the party fights a group of skeletons in the officer's barracks.
[u0022] Several of them are immune to fire, which Yvan realizes after attempting to hit them with a sorcerous burst.
[u0023] Though this initially confounds Yvan's strategy, with Drou's giant constrictor snake and Fazoth's fey ally, the party avoids taking many hits from the skeletons.
[u0024] However, the snake is badly wounded in the fight, as skeletons keep exploding in shards of fire or bone when they die, though Drou is able to turn it back into a staff before it dies.
[u0025] After a quick search reveals no more enemies (along with signs of ancient fire damage, and some possible bodies of Rangers buried under collapsed stone), the party takes a breather.
[u0026] After a quick rest, and a short walk through the barracks uncovers nothing more interesting than [[avatus-symbol-sketch.png|Avatus' symbol]] on some gear, the party enters the mine area.
[u0027] Here, they quickly explore a work shed, finding what is probably Duncan's camp, before heading into the mine.
[u0028] The mine cuts horizontally into the hill for a few hundred feet before it dead-ends in a massive cave-in.
[u0029] In the cave-in chamber, the party encounters skeletons and serpentine wights.
[u0030] Drou's _Hypnotic Pattern_ puts most of them to sleep as the battle opens, and Yvan's wild magic surge summons a unicorn, who makes quick work of the undead.
[u0031] Though the unicorn quickly departs, she shares that she can sense no more undead in these upper mine chambers, and suggests this would be a safe place to rest.
[u0032] The party takes advantage of this advice, and our session ends the next morning.
[u0033] ### Thursday, May 27th, 2 Robert I
[u0034] The party wakes after a long rest in the mine, with signs that the cave-in was intentional and with footprints suggesting another entrance somewhere beyond the blockage.
