[u0003] The party follows worg tracks to a Feywild portal, kills a Hollow Man, and enters the Feywild.
[u0004] ## Timeline
[u0005] - **(DR:: 1715-05-18), afternoon:** The party tracks the worgs to a strange foggy hollow.
[u0006] - **(DR:: 1715-05-18), evening**: After some experimenting they recreate the songs needed to open a portal to the [[Feywild]].
[u0007] A hollow man emerges, which they kill.
[u0008] Entering the Feywild, they defeat thorn sprites and a flying archer-fey, and hear a fey melody from a singing tree in the distance.
[u0009] ## Cast of Characters
[u0010] - **[[Drou]]** – elven bard-monk, expert tracker and thunderous spellcaster - **[[Fazoth de Brune]]** – human warlock-alchemist with a fey-tracking lantern - **[[Yvan Greenrabbit]]** – halfling wild-magic sorcerer, lethal with Chromatic Orb
[u0011] ## Places
[u0012] - **[[Highmoor]] Downs** – rolling hills east of [[Roscombe]], dotted with ridges - The [[Feywild]] - the home of the [[Fey]]
[u0013] ## Narrative
[u0014] ### Tuesday, May 18th, 2 Robert I
[u0015] [[Fazoth de Brune|Fazoth]], [[Yvan Greenrabbit|Yvan]], and [[Drou]] continue to follow a worg trail across the [[Highmoor]].
[u0016] Several hours pass as the trail continues up and down the next ridge, and then turns northeast towards a strange foggy depression some miles off.
[u0017] By late afternoon, [[Fazoth de Brune|Fazoth]], [[Drou]], and [[Yvan Greenrabbit|Yvan]] arrive at the [[Nightsong Roots|foggy depression]].
[u0018] Their is a deep unnatural feeling to the fog, as it clings and clusters to the roots of a massive fallen tree with no regard for the sun.
[u0019] An occasional faint wind rustles the roots, which hang strangely like wind chimes.
[u0020] After some experimentation by [[Yvan Greenrabbit|Yvan]] with _Elementalism_, which causes the wind-chime-like roots to play haunting sounds, [[Drou]] is able to replicate the notes and opens a portal in the fallen tree.
[u0021] A hollow man emerges and attacks.
[u0022] Remembering the rhyme, the party focuses on fire damage, and the creature is quickly dispatched.
[u0023] Stepping into the [[Feywild]], the party follows a path through a dense, dark forest.
[u0024] On the path, they are attacked by small fey, a group of thorn-like creatures and a flying archer with ice arrows.
[u0025] Our session ends as these attackers are defeated and the party hears the disquieting sounds of a singing tree ahead.
