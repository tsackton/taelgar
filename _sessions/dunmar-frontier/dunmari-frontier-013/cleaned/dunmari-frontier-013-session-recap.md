The party follows the taint to the bridge below Raven's Hold, where foul water, red vines, and a runic circle reveal the shape of the corruption. Breaking the circle draws out a demonic plant creature from the river, but once it is slain the river clears, the remains of a used scroll are found, and Seeker leads the animals out of captivity.
