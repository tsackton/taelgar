- Tagline: in which the party confronts a vile demon

#### Short
At dusk in the north tower, the party debriefs Vola Forena and commits to scouting the keep before daring to rest.

#### Long
After rescuing Vola Forena, the party learns that Agata Dustmother's servants captured her while waiting for a ritual at Raven's Hold to succeed. Weighing Vola's warning, the captive animals, and the growing sense of urgency, they choose an invisible scouting run over a full night's rest.

### Apr 12th, 1748 (night)

- Timeline Segment: timeline-002
- Timeline Key: (DR:: 1748-04-12), night
- Resolution: part-of-day
- Beat IDs: b03, b04, b05, b06, b07
- Locations: Raven's Hold

- Organizations: Five Siblings
- Items: elixir of health, hobgoblin papers
- Combat Beats: b05, b06

#### Short
The night's scouting exposes Oduk and its ritual hall, and the party attacks at once, kills the demon and its undead gnolls, then searches the keep for clues.

#### Long
Kenzo and Wellby uncover a desecrated chapel, an undead gnoll sentry, and Oduk pacing the keep beside skull-lined fires and heaps of carcasses, convincing the party that the ritual cannot wait until morning. Their ambush becomes a hard fight, but Oduk falls, the last undead gnolls are destroyed, the ritual fires are banked, and the keep yields only more old hobgoblin papers instead of a deeper answer.

- Organizations: none
- Items: none

#### Short
After a troubled rest in the tower, the party wakes shaken but alive and turns its plans toward the remaining gnolls, penned animals, and corrupted river.

#### Long
The tower proves safe enough for the night, but not peaceful: some of the party wake from vivid nightmares with lingering weakness and exhaustion. At dawn they compare notes, confirm that the wider operation at Raven's Hold is still unfinished, and set their sights on the gnolls, the animals, and the source of the corruption.

## Recap

### recap-001 | Vola's Story

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b01
- Date: 1748-04-12
- Time: evening
- Source Range: u0001 -> u0479
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: Vola Forena
- Organizations: none
- Items: none

#### Short
Sheltering at dusk in Raven's Hold's north tower, the party shares food with Vola Forena and hears how Agata Dustmother's servants captured her while waiting on a ritual.

#### Intermediate
Sheltering in the north tower at dusk, the party gets Vola talking after her rescue. She says she came seeking Drankorian relics, was seized by Agata Dustmother's servants, and was meant to be taken away once a ritual at Raven's Hold succeeded.

#### Long
Sheltering in the north tower at dusk, the party finally hears Vola Forena's full account of the danger around them. She explains that she came to Raven's Hold in search of Drankorian relics, was taken by an orc and jackalweres serving Agata Dustmother, and was told she would be carried off once the ritual here was complete. Her story binds the captives, the animals, and the haunted atmosphere of the fortress into one threat.

### recap-002 | Scouting Plan

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b02
- Date: 1748-04-12
- Time: evening
- Source Range: u0480 -> u0679
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: Vola Forena
- Organizations: none
- Items: none
- Enemies: none

#### Short
From that account, the group weighs rest against urgency and chooses an invisible scouting run instead of waiting for morning.

#### Intermediate
From there, the party turns Vola's warning into a plan. They argue over whether the water, the penned animals, and the ritual can be left alone, then settle on sending Wellby under invisibility with Kenzo close behind while the others recover what they can.

#### Long
From there, the party turns information into action. The questions multiply around the river, the captive beasts, and how long they have before the fortress notices Vola's escape, but the answer is still the same: they need eyes on the keep tonight. By the time the debate ends, they have chosen stealth over sleep and set Wellby and Kenzo on the scouting route.

### recap-003 | Reconnaissance Run

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b03
- Date: 1748-04-12
- Time: night
- Source Range: u0680 -> u0979
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: Oduk, undead gnoll
- Organizations: Five Siblings
- Items: none
- Enemies: none

#### Short
Under cover of night, Kenzo and Wellby cross the misty courtyard, find a desecrated chapel stuffed with carcasses, and spot Oduk pacing the keep beside an undead gnoll.

#### Intermediate
Under cover of night, Kenzo and Wellby slip across the steaming courtyard and inspect the keep through its broken windows. They find the smashed chapel of Aagir choked with animal remains, glimpse an undead gnoll outside the hall, and confirm that a horned demon waits inside among bonfires and skulls.

#### Long
Under cover of night, Kenzo and Wellby make the scouting run the party has been circling around all evening. The keep proves worse than rumor suggested: the old chapel of Aagir has been desecrated and stuffed with bones and rotting carcasses, while an undead gnoll still patrols outside the main hall. Beyond it, Oduk paces in the firelight amid unnatural heat, skulls, and waiting ritual fires, giving the party the proof it needed.

#### Short
Back in the tower, the scout report convinces them the ritual cannot be left alone, and they return across the courtyard to spring an ambush that same night.

#### Intermediate
Back in the tower, the group connects the scene in the keep to demon lore, cursed flesh, and the possibility that the gnoll threat is still being made there in real time. Rather than trust the night to pass quietly, they decide to cross back over at once and strike Oduk before morning.

#### Long
Back in the tower, the scout report turns speculation into resolve. Seeker ties the ritual to demonic stories of corrupted flesh, while Kenzo senses that Oduk is tethered to something beyond the world and therefore too dangerous to leave in place. After one last argument over caution, the party crosses the courtyard in darkness and sets up the ambush outside the keep.

### recap-005 | Ambush in the Keep to Oduk Falls

- Kind: combat
- Beat IDs: b05, b06
- Date: 1748-04-12
- Time: night
- Source Range: u1439 -> u2084
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: Vola Forena, Oduk, undead gnolls
- Organizations: none
- Items: elixir of health
- Enemies: Oduk, undead gnolls

#### Short
The ambush turns into a brutal melee, but after poison, panic, and emergency healing, the party brings down Oduk and the last undead gnolls.

#### Intermediate
Surprise attacks give the party its first edge, but Oduk quickly turns the fight into a desperate close-quarters struggle with poison, fear, and undead gnolls pressing in from the hall. Delwath nearly drops, Seeker has to keep him alive, and Vola's conjured blade helps create the opening that lets Kenzo break the demon before Riswynn smashes down the last gnoll.

#### Long
The ambush lands cleanly, but the fight refuses to stay clean for long. Oduk meets the party head-on with claws, poison, and a black, hateful will, while the undead gnolls trap people in the doorway and around the fires. Delwath falls and has to be hauled back with healing and an elixir, Kenzo fights through rotting flesh, and Vola proves far more dangerous than her exhaustion suggests. At last Kenzo breaks one of Oduk's horns and the demon vanishes, after which Riswynn crushes the final gnoll and the hall goes still.

### recap-006 | Searching the Ruins

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b07
- Date: 1748-04-12
- Time: night
- Source Range: u2085 -> u2449
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: Vola Forena
- Organizations: none
- Items: hobgoblin papers
- Enemies: none

#### Short
With the hall finally quiet, the party banks the ritual fires, searches the blasted chapel and upper rooms, and finds more old hobgoblin papers but no deeper secret.

#### Intermediate
With Oduk gone, the party studies the carcasses, skulls, and fires it had been using, trying to piece together the method behind the ritual. The search yields another bundle of old hobgoblin papers and some confirmation that the keep has no obvious hidden lower chamber, but not the larger answer they hoped to find.

#### Long
With Oduk gone, the party can finally look instead of merely survive. They bank the ritual fires, inspect the carcass-clogged chapel, and climb through the battered upper rooms, piecing together what little they can from the remains of the site. The keep gives up more old hobgoblin papers and signs of long occupation, but no hidden sanctum and no simple explanation for the corruption running through Raven's Hold.

### recap-007 | Troubled Rest

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b08
- Date: 1748-04-13
- Time: dawn
- Source Range: u2450 -> u2810
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: Vola Forena, remaining gnolls
- Organizations: none
- Items: none
- Enemies: none

#### Short
They retreat to the tower for what rest they can manage, wake from corrupted dreams at dawn, and plan to face the gnolls, animals, and river still left to investigate.

#### Intermediate
They fall back to the north tower, where the gate holds and the remaining gnolls only briefly test it before drifting away into the dark. The rest itself is foul: nightmares, incomplete healing, and exhaustion leave the party shaken at dawn, but still determined to deal with what remains outside.

#### Long
They retreat to the north tower because it is safer than the keep, not because it is safe. The gate holds against a brief nocturnal check from the gnolls, but the sleep that follows is poisoned by nightmares of beasts changing shape and hunting through the dark. By dawn the party is battered, some of them only partly restored, yet the morning conversation still hardens into a plan: deal with the gnolls, the penned animals, the river, and the unanswered questions before leaving Raven's Hold behind.
