## Vola's Story
*b01*

**Date**: 1748-04-12
**Time Window**: evening
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: In the north tower, the party shares food with Vola and learns how Agata Dustmother's servants captured her while waiting for a ritual at Raven's Hold to succeed.
**Long Summary**: Holed up in the north tower at dusk, the party gets its first real conversation with Vola Forena after rescuing her from captivity. Vola explains that she came to Raven's Hold hunting Drankorian relics, was seized by an orc and two jackalweres serving Agata Dustmother, and was told she would be taken away once an important ritual here was complete. Her account ties the fortress, the captive animals, and the growing nightmares into the same threat.
**Location**:
- Raven's Hold: the north tower chamber overlooking the inner courtyard
  Note: The party remains in the tower while debriefing Vola after her rescue.
**NPCs**:
- Vola Forena (companion): rescued prisoner and Chardonian adventurer. She recounts her capture and what her captors told her about the ritual.
- Agata Dustmother (mentioned): the power behind Vola's captors. Vola says the orc and jackalweres serve her and meant to take Vola to her.
**Items**: none
**Organizations**:
- Society of the Open Scroll (mentioned): Vola identifies herself with the Society while explaining why she came to the fortress.

**Time Window**: night
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: Kenzo and Wellby slip through the misty courtyard, uncover a ruined chapel stuffed with animal carcasses, and spot an undead gnoll and a waiting demon inside the keep.
