## Josue's Spearheads
*b05*

**Date**: 1748-03-29
**Time Window**: evening
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: Josue confirms that Arcas brought him unusual spearheads and wanted them mounted, but the metal resisted every attempt to work it.
**Long Summary**: The party catches Josue at the end of his workday and follows him back to Krom's for a longer talk. He confirms that Arcas came through Kerala looking for ancient Dunmari relics, left behind silver spearheads to be mounted on shafts, and never returned to collect them. What troubles Josue most is that the spearheads seem unnaturally resistant to heat and tools, reinforcing the party's suspicion that Arcas found something more than ordinary battlefield salvage.
**Location**:
- Kerala: Josue's smithy and then Krom's inn in the evening
  Note: The party moves briefly to the smithy before finishing the conversation back at the inn.
**NPCs**:
- Josue (encountered): blacksmith. He explains what Arcas asked of him and how the spearheads defied normal smithing.
- Arcas (mentioned): artifact-seeking traveler. Josue says Arcas was interested in Great War relics and left spearheads in his care.
**Items**:
- silver spearheads (mentioned): Arcas left these with Josue, but the metal resisted melting, reforging, and even being driven into wood.
**Organizations**: none

## Kendrasar's Truth
*b08*

**Date**: 1748-03-29
**Time Window**: night
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: In the temple, Candrosa tests the party with a zone of truth and confesses that Shakun's protection is failing across the Dunmari border.
**Long Summary**: Once the party reaches the Temple of Shakun, Candrosa drops his reserve and subjects them to a zone of truth before speaking plainly. He explains that after the Great War, Shakun's protection and the work of her mystai kept evil things from crossing the border, but that the recent attacks, a spreading madness, storms, and fresh reports from Havdar all suggest those defenses are breaking down. Though shaken and embarrassed by his secrecy, he begs the party to help watch over Kerala through the coming festival and to consider aiding Dunmar more broadly as the border crisis deepens.
**Location**:
- Kerala: a side chamber inside the Temple of Shakun carved into the Red Mesa
  Note: This is a late-night private audience with Candrosa after his summons from the oasis.
**NPCs**:
- Candrosa (encountered): priest of Shakun and keeper of Kerala's deeper secrets. He reveals his fear that the border wards have failed and asks the party to help.
- Havdar (mentioned): war leader returning from the border. Candrosa says Havdar has just come from fighting dangerous creatures on the Dunmari side of the border.
**Items**:
- black iron staff (encountered): Kenzo throws the staff onto the table as proof of what the party destroyed.
- healing mud vials (encountered): Candrosa gives each of them a vial as a gesture of good faith before sending them to rest.
**Organizations**:
- Shakun Mystai (encountered): Candrosa speaks as one of Shakun's guardians and admits the order is failing to understand the new danger.
