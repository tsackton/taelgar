## Parley with Tharandros
*beat-010*

**Date**: 1748-04-12
**Time Window**: morning
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: The party parleys with the centaur leader Tharandros, learns that poisoned water is spreading outward from Raven's Hold through a planar tether, and persuades the centaurs to warn Karawa before seeking safety farther west.
**Long Summary**: What begins as a tense dawn encounter becomes a long exchange of fragments, translation, and theory. The centaur leader, Tharandros, explains that his people are abandoning the region because streams and watering holes have been poisoned, a corruption he believes spreads outward from Raven's Hold while some tether to another realm remains in place. The conversation turns to demon-haunted planes, old human beliefs that gave shape to such horrors, and the possibility that healing the land will require severing the tether rather than merely surviving its consequences. Before the party moves on, Delwath and the others convince Tharandros to ride south toward Karawa and share what his people know so the Dunmari are not left blind to the danger.
**Location**:
- Approach to Raven's Hold: the dawn campsite near the pass, with Raven's Hold and the western storm visible from the plains
  Note: The scene remains at camp until the centaurs turn south and the party prepares to continue toward the hold.
**NPCs**:
- Tharandros (encountered): centaur leader speaking for his people. He describes the poisoned water, the tether to another realm, and the centaurs' decision to leave the region.
- centaurs (encountered): Tharandros's migrating band. They are moving west and south in search of clean water and safer pastures.
- Candrosa (mentioned): possible contact in Karawa. The party suggests the centaurs may be able to find wisdom or aid among the remaining sages in Karawa.
