Each member of the party settles into the morning in their own way, but the routine ends when gnolls and a small demon are seen approaching the hold.

### recap-002 | Demon Falls at the Gate to Chasing the Fleeing Gnoll

- Kind: combat
- Beat IDs: recap-02, recap-03, recap-04, recap-05, recap-06
- Date: 1748-04-13
- Time: morning
- Source Range: u0338 -> u2094
- Locations: Raven's Hold, gatehouse, outer plaza near the stables
- NPCs: Vola Farina, bat-like demon, gnoll raiders, gnoll leader, last gate gnoll, fleeing gnoll, outer plaza gnolls
- Organizations: none
- Items: none
- Enemies: bat-like demon, gnoll raiders, gnoll leader, last gate gnoll, fleeing gnoll, outer plaza gnolls

#### Short
Wellby drops the bat-like demon with an opening shot, and the fight grows from a tower defense into a running battle at the gate before the last gnolls are hunted down in the outer plaza.

#### Intermediate
The attack begins with Wellby's arrow killing the demon before it can do more than lead the charge, leaving the gnolls to press the gate under fire from above. Kenzo holds the breach while the others support him from the towers and walls, and when one gnoll escapes the party races after it into the outer plaza, where the last attackers are cut down near the stables.

#### Long
Wellby opens the battle by killing the bat-like demon outright, and the party turns its height and cover into a killing ground while the gnoll raiders try to force the gate. Kenzo braces himself at the breach as Vola, Delwath, Riswynn, and Seeker support from above, and the fight shifts from arrows and spells into a tight melee when the doors finally give way. Even with Kenzo nearly overwhelmed, the party stabilizes the line, kills the gnoll leader, and leaves only a single runner to carry the fight outward. Kenzo and Wellby chase that last gnoll through the hold into the outer plaza, the rest of the party converges behind them, and together they finish the remaining gnolls near the animal pens and parade ground.

### recap-003 | Exploring the Outer Plaza

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: recap-07
- Date: 1748-04-13
- Time: morning
- Source Range: u2095 -> u2472
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: Vola Farina
- Organizations: none
- Items: Dunmari spear, metal vial
- Enemies: none

#### Short
With the fighting over, the party searches the outer plaza, checks the starving animals in the stables, and finds clues in the ruined buildings and on the gnoll leader's body.

#### Intermediate
The sudden quiet after the battle gives way to the noise of the penned animals, drawing Seeker and Kenzo toward the stables while the others scout the surrounding buildings and walls. Their search turns up ruined military spaces, signs of older Dunmari occupation, a well-made spear bearing a running horse emblem, and a small hissing vial on the gnoll leader.

#### Long
Once the last gnoll falls, the party spreads through the outer plaza to secure the area and understand what they have found. Seeker and Kenzo approach the starving hyenas, jackals, lions, and other animals crammed into the stables, while Wellby and Riswynn inspect the adjacent buildings and Delwath keeps watch over the plains for fresh danger. The ruined barracks and parade ground still carry traces of Raven's Hold's older Dunmari military life, and the gnoll leader's gear adds two sharper clues: a Dunmari spear marked with a running horse design and a small metal vial full of acrid, hissing liquid.

### recap-004 | Theorizing Over Breakfast

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: recap-08
- Date: 1748-04-13
- Time: morning
- Source Range: u2473 -> u2658
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: none
- Organizations: none
- Items: none
- Enemies: none

#### Short
Over breakfast and a short rest, the party argues through the evidence and begins to suspect that demonic corruption is transforming the animals in the hold.

#### Intermediate
The discussion turns to whether the animals can be released safely, whether the water or the demon's claw is spreading the corruption, and whether gnolls might arise from infected beasts. By the time breakfast is finished and the party has taken a short rest, the group has settled on the need to investigate the green pits and inner courtyard more carefully.

#### Long
As the immediate danger passes, the party's attention shifts from survival to explanation. They debate whether to free the captured animals, whether the poisoned water matters, how the antelope carcasses were being corrupted, and whether gnolls might be the result of demonic infection spreading through ordinary beasts. The argument does not solve every mystery, but it does narrow the field: the fort is momentarily secure, breakfast and a short rest are possible, and the next step is to examine the green pits and inner courtyard with clearer heads.
