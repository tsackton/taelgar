## Dreamworld Initiation
*b01*

**Date**: 1748-03-30
**Time Window**: dawn
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: Candrosa wakes Kenzo before dawn, brings him to the mesa, and sends him through the Dreamworld with the temple staff.
**Long Summary**: On a secluded mesa above Karawa, Candrosa confides that his dreams have shown a cracked jade hand connected to Kenzo's amulet. Using the Dreamweaver Staff, he guides Kenzo into the Dreamworld, where Kenzo restores the jade hand, meets a robed figure with a jade arm, and is told to seek the Order of the Awakened Soul. He returns changed, able to glimpse auras and carrying a stronger sense of the strange power tied to his amulet.
**Location**:
- Karawa: a hidden mesa overlook east of the village where Candrosa leads Kenzo for a private rite
  Note: The beat never leaves the mesa, even while Kenzo's consciousness travels into the Dreamworld.
**NPCs**:
- Candrosa (encountered): mystic of the Temple of Shakun. He shares his recurring jade-hand vision and performs the rite that sends Kenzo into the Dreamworld.
- jade-handed figure (encountered): mysterious robed man in the Dreamworld. He tells Kenzo to seek the Order of the Awakened Soul before shattering into fragments.
**Items**:
- Dreamweaver Staff (encountered): Candrosa uses the staff to open a path between the waking world and the Dreamworld.
- jade amulet fragment (encountered): Kenzo uses the fragment to complete the cracked jade hand in the vision.
**Organizations**:
- Order of the Awakened Soul (mentioned): The Dreamworld figure tells Kenzo to seek this order.

## Jasu's Silver Spearpoints
*b04*

**Date**: 1748-03-30
**Time Window**: afternoon
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: At Jasu's forge, the party inspects Arcus's silver spearpoints and learns they were meant to be remounted for another journey east.
**Long Summary**: The party visits Jasu after lunch and studies the odd silver-coated spearpoints Arcus left behind. Jasu explains that he was supposed to mount them back onto spear shafts, but the relics resist heat and ordinary smithing despite still holding a deadly edge. Between Jasu's testimony and the party's magical inspection, the spearpoints look like Great War weapons made to pierce undead defenses, sharpening the mystery of what Arcus was hunting and why he never returned.
**Location**:
- Karawa: Jasu's smithy during the afternoon follow-up to the mesa discussion
  Note: The beat is centered on the forge and the relic examination.
**NPCs**:
- Jasu (encountered): blacksmith. He shows the spearpoints, explains Arcus's commission, and confirms Arcus expected to return within a week or two.
- Arcus (mentioned): artifact hunter turned to stone. Jasu remembers him as a traveler interested in Great War relics who headed east and never came back.
**Items**:
- silver spearpoints (encountered): The relic spearpoints appear to be magical Great War weapons meant for fighting undead.

*b10*

**Date**: 1748-04-04
**Time Window**: morning
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: Kenzo's proposal becomes the council's plan: evacuate west, fortify the temple, send for the king, scout south lightly, and send the party north to Raven's Hold.
**Long Summary**: Kenzo argues that Karawa's people matter more than the town's buildings and that the safest plan is to move them west under Havdar's protection while still preparing the temple as a fallback stronghold. Suma and Kisa back the evacuation, Candrosa accepts a smaller defensive holdout at the temple, and Alesh volunteers to help split the wider response between a ride to the king and fresh scouting to the south. By the meeting's end, the broad strategy is set: the villagers will begin withdrawing after the festival, the temple will be fortified, and the party will head north toward Raven's Hold to pursue the strongest lead they have.
**Location**:
- Karawa: the council chamber at the close of the emergency meeting
  Note: The beat is the final decision phase of the same council scene.
**NPCs**:

- Candrosa (encountered): mystic remaining with the temple. He agrees to stay with the temple and trust the party's wider judgment.
- Havdar (encountered): mounted scout captain. He commits to protecting the evacuation west and then regrouping for later action.
- Alesh (encountered): scout and courier. She offers to divide responsibilities between seeking the king's help and supporting further reconnaissance.
- Suma (encountered): priestess of Jeevali. She supports warning the wider people during the festival and helping lead the withdrawal west.
