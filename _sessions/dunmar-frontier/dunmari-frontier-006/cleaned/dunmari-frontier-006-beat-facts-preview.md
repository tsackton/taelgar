**Time Window**: unknown
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: In the battle's wake, Karawa abandons any delay: Havdar orders flight at first light, Candrosa and the mystai seal themselves in, and the party turns north at dawn.
**Long Summary**: With initiative over, the group quickly learns that the surviving gnoll cannot be questioned usefully and that the maw-creatures decay with unnatural speed. Havdar arrives bloodied from a parallel skirmish, sees enough to decide the town can no longer wait, and orders the evacuation to begin at first light rather than after another day. Kisa and Candrosa thank the party, the temple is loaded like a siegehold for the mystai who will stay behind, and after a tense night of reflection, packing, and Kenzo's unsettling trance, the town finally pours west toward Tokra while the party picks up the attackers' tracks and rides north toward Raven's Hold.

  Note: The beat begins at the ambush site and resolves with the town's evacuation west and the party's departure north.

- Candrosa (encountered): temple leader preparing for a siege. He and the remaining mystai begin hauling food and water into the Temple of Shakun for a desperate holdout.
