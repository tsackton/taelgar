### recap-001 | Council Aftermath

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b01

- Source Range: u0001 -> u0318
- Locations: Karawa
- NPCs: none
- Organizations: none

#### Short
Leaving the council behind, the party quickly turns Karawa's fears into a working plan for the road to Raven's Hold.

#### Intermediate
Rumors are already spreading through town when the party steps out of the council meeting and starts sorting priorities instead of arguing possibilities. They settle on a divided effort: Alesh will scout south, Candrosa's people will hold the temple as long as they can, and the party itself will ride north once preparations are done.

#### Long
The party emerges from the council into a Karawa already changing shape, with frightened talk in the streets and Havdar gathering anyone able to ride and fight. Rather than linger over doubts, they work through the evacuation plan, accept that the temple must become the town's last redoubt, and commit themselves to Raven's Hold while Alesh carries the southern burden.

### recap-002 | Errands in Karawa

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b02
- Date: 1748-04-04
- Time: afternoon
- Source Range: u0319 -> u0620
- Locations: Karawa
- NPCs: Candrosa, Callie Charmheart
- Organizations: Shakun Mystai
- Items: none
- Enemies: none

#### Short
Scattered across Karawa, the party spends the afternoon hunting for every scrap of knowledge the town can offer about the road north.

#### Intermediate
Wellby presses the Charmhearts for a clearer picture of Raven's Hold while Kenzo questions Candrosa about the north, old shrines, and Aagir. At the same time, Seeker studies the Red Mesa and the temple built into it, leaving the group with more context than certainty.

#### Long
The party breaks apart to work the town from several angles at once, trying to replace rumor with something more useful before they leave. Callie Charmheart gives Wellby a practical sense of the route and of Ander's disastrous journey toward Raven's Hold, Candrosa answers what he can about the northern frontier and Aagir, and Seeker's attention to the Red Mesa only deepens the sense that older powers still lie under Karawa.

Delwath scrounges up healing potions and red healing mud while the group works out where to leave word if either scouting party fails to return on time. They gather what healing they can, talk through Tokra, Havdar, and the best place to leave messages if plans go wrong, and then watch Alesh and her companions head south under a sky that makes every parting feel heavier than it should.

### recap-005 | Festival Ambush Begins to Gnolls Broken

- Kind: combat
- Beat IDs: b05, b06, b07
- Date: 1748-04-05 to 1748-04-06
- Time: unknown
- Source Range: u1190 -> u2292
- Locations: Karawa
- NPCs: Suma, Ander Charmheart, Havdar
- Organizations: none
- Items: rebirth shrine
- Enemies: gnolls, maw-creatures

#### Short
The Festival of Rebirth begins in solemn hope, only to collapse into screams when gnolls and maw-creatures strike from the north.

#### Intermediate
Suma leads Karawa through Jeevali's story, the burning shrine casts embers west, and the townsfolk take the sign as blessing for the road to Tokra. By the next day's procession the mood is strained but orderly, until panic at the edge of the crowd sends the party running toward an assault by gnolls and monstrous maws.

#### Long
Karawa enters the Festival of Rebirth in a mood that is more desperate than joyful, listening to Suma recount Jeevali's tears, the greening of Dunmar, and the god's return through the faith of her people. The burning shrine seems to answer that faith when its embers drift west, and even the subdued procession the next day carries a trace of hope until the first shouts break across the crowd and the party charges north to meet gnolls and ravenous maw-creatures at the edge of town.

### recap-006 | Flight at First Light

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b08
- Date: 1748-04-06 to 1748-04-07
- Time: unknown
- Source Range: u2293 -> u2743
- Locations: Karawa, Raven's Hold
- NPCs: Havdar, Candrosa, Kisa
- Organizations: Shakun Mystai
- Items: maw tooth
- Enemies: none

#### Short
That night Karawa gives up any last delay, and at dawn the town flees west while the party rides north.

#### Intermediate
With the ambush over, Havdar orders the evacuation moved up to first light and the town begins preparing in grim silence. Candrosa and the mystai haul provisions into the temple for a siege, Kisa comes to thank the party, and by dawn the refugees are streaming toward Tokra while the party takes the northern trail.

#### Long
The night after the battle is filled with the kind of labor that follows a decision no one can afford to question. Havdar orders Karawa out at first light, Candrosa and the remaining mystai turn the Temple of Shakun into a sealed refuge, and Seeker keeps a black maw tooth as proof that the attack left something tangible behind. When dawn comes, the people of Karawa pour west toward Tokra and the party finally leaves in the opposite direction, following the attackers' trail toward Raven's Hold.
