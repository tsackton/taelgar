## Shattering the obsidian
*b10*

**Date**: 1748-03-25
**Time Window**: evening
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: The party exploits the flaw in the obsidian crystal, shatters it after several painful attempts, and feels the entire fort settle into calm before camping in the temple.
**Long Summary**: As insects begin gathering around them, the party abandons any thought of carrying the relic away and instead attacks it where it stands. Kenzo first drives Aagir's Everlight into the flaw and cracks the crystal at personal cost, Wellby widens the fracture, and Delwath finally shatters the obsidian with a shadow-forged blade. The psychic pressure vanishes, the surrounding creatures calm, and the party decides to bring the statue and their finds back to the temple and rest there through the night.
**Location**:
- Dunmari Fort (Gomat): the lower relic chamber, then the temple above where the party makes camp
  Note: The beat begins at the obsidian relic and ends with the party settling in the fort temple.
**NPCs**: none
**Items**:
- Aagir's Everlight (encountered): Used as the silver spearpoint that opens the flaw in the obsidian crystal.
- black iron staff with obsidian crystal (encountered): Destroyed in place when the cracked crystal is finally shattered.
**Organizations**: none
