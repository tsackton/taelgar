#### Short
The Dunmar Fellowship explores the ruined fort east of Gomat, uncovers old Dunmari relics, and tracks the spreading corruption into the caves below.

#### Long
The company surveys the fort, kills an ankheg at the headquarters, and secures the temple, towers, and lower passages. In the caves they find Arcus petrified, recover a floating-disc coin, avoid a cockatrice nursery, defeat skeletons and an undead hobgoblin, and identify the obsidian staff as the source of the fort's corruption.

The party shatters the obsidian crystal, the fort falls quiet, and they make camp in the temple.

#### Long
By evening the cracked obsidian crystal is smashed apart with Aagir's Everlight and Delwath's shadow-forged blade. The psychic pressure vanishes, the beasts settle, and the party carries its finds back up to the temple to rest.

## Recap

### recap-001 | Surveying the fort

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b01
- Date: 1748-03-25
- Time: afternoon
- Source Range: u0001 -> u0437
- Locations: Dunmari Fort
- NPCs: none
- Organizations: none
- Items: none

#### Short
The Fellowship combs the ruined fort's outskirts, reading tracks, campsites, and broken walls for signs of what has stirred here.

#### Intermediate
They confirm that only a few structures still stand, find evidence of a lone recent camper, and follow disturbed tracks from the causeway toward the fort's central buildings.

#### Long
After reviewing the skeletons destroyed earlier that day, the party inspects the disturbed campsite and the mounds outside the fort, then studies the surviving towers, headquarters, temple, and cistern. The signs point to recent intrusion and to something still moving within the ruins, so Wellby creeps closer to the central building while the others hold back and watch.

### recap-002 | Ankheg ambush

- Kind: combat
- Beat IDs: b02
- Date: 1748-03-25
- Time: afternoon
- Source Range: u0438 -> u0721
- Locations: Dunmari Fort
- NPCs: none
- Organizations: none
- Items: none
- Enemies: ankheg

#### Short
The ground erupts beneath Wellby as an ankheg lunges from the earth and forces the party into a sudden fight.

#### Intermediate
Delwath realizes too late what sort of burrowing hunter they have stirred up, but the party frees Wellby, turns the creature back from its ambush, and kills it in the forecourt.

#### Long
What begins as a careful approach collapses into chaos when the ankheg bursts up under Wellby and clamps him in its mandibles. Delwath names the threat, Seeker and Kenzo help break the beast's momentum, and the party brings it down before it can drag anyone underground.

### recap-003 | Temple and respite

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b03
- Date: 1748-03-25
- Time: afternoon
- Source Range: u0722 -> u1092
- Locations: Dunmari Fort
- NPCs: none
- Organizations: none
- Items: Aagir's Everlight
- Enemies: none

#### Short
With the ankheg dead, the party searches its feeding ground, finds the cave entrance below the fort, and takes shelter in the intact temple.

#### Intermediate
They note the many beast tracks around the hole, identify the five-sided shrine as a temple of the Five Siblings, and recover Aagir's Everlight while deciding the temple is the safest place to pause.

#### Long
The fort's corruption now seems to extend beneath the surface, with bones, tracks, and a sloping tunnel showing that beasts have been moving in and out for some time. Before descending, the party inspects the temple, recognizes its surviving altars, salvages Aagir's Everlight and other religious objects, and uses the shrine as a place to rest and gather themselves.

#### Short
A search of the gate towers turns up the remains of an old scout and a magical Dunmari horn marked with a running horse.

#### Intermediate
One tower proves to be little but rubble, but the other still holds a long-dead outrider from the Great War era, and the glint beside the bones leads the party to a horn worth keeping.

#### Long
After the temple rest, the party returns to the entrance towers to finish clearing the fort aboveground. The better-preserved tower holds a skeleton in light gear, likely a scout or outrider, and beside it lies a magical horn with Dunmari silverwork in the running-horse style, another relic they decide to carry below.

### recap-005 | Statue and floating disk

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b05
- Date: 1748-03-25
- Time: afternoon
- Source Range: u1214 -> u1630
- Locations: Dunmari Fort
- NPCs: Arcus
- Organizations: none
- Items: Floating Disc Coin
- Enemies: none

#### Short
Below the fort, the party finds a recently petrified explorer and a heavy old coin that unfolds into a floating disk.

#### Intermediate
The first chamber beneath the fort holds the torn stone body of Arcus and other broken petrified remains, and Seeker's experiments with the strange coin turn it into a disk of force that begins following him through the caves.

#### Long
The descent opens into a chamber that feels less ancient than the fort above, with a human turned to stone so recently that the scene still looks violent rather than eroded. While the party studies the petrified explorer, the severed arm, and the scattered remains around him, Delwath confirms the nearby coin is magical, and Seeker flips it into the floating disk that becomes their newest and strangest tool.

### recap-006 | Cockatrice nursery

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b06
- Date: 1748-03-25
- Time: afternoon
- Source Range: u1631 -> u1965
- Locations: Dunmari Fort
- NPCs: none
- Organizations: none
- Items: none
- Enemies: none

#### Short
A cockatrice nursery blocks one branch of the caves, and the party retreats rather than risk drawing the parents while Seeker is depleted.

#### Intermediate
They watch hatchlings peck a lion in and out of stone, decide the room is too dangerous for a direct fight, and loop through the cistern cavern instead as Seeker keeps tracing the obsidian presence deeper south.

#### Long
The next chamber proves to be a nest of baby cockatrices, their eggs half-buried in mud and their prey trapped in a grotesque cycle of flesh and stone. Unwilling to fight in cramped tunnels with unseen adults nearby, the party backs away, crosses the dry cistern cavern, and presses on toward the darker source Seeker has been sensing under the fort.

### recap-007 | Skeletons in the dark to The black staff

- Kind: combat
- Beat IDs: b07, b08
- Date: 1748-03-25
- Time: afternoon
- Source Range: u1966 -> u2499
- Locations: Dunmari Fort
- NPCs: undead hobgoblin staff-bearer
- Organizations: none
- Items: black iron staff with obsidian crystal
- Enemies: three animated skeletons, undead hobgoblin staff-bearer

#### Short
Skeletons drop from the ceiling in the deeper dark, and the party fights toward the glow behind them until the true guardian appears.

#### Intermediate
The narrow approach turns into a grinding cavern fight as Delwath holds the front, Kenzo closes, Wellby fires from behind, and Seeker tries to use the floating disk as cover before the chamber's deeper master steps into view.

#### Long
As the party reaches the last chamber, the pressure of the place sharpens and skeletons descend from above to meet them in the dark. They form a cramped battle line in the passage and cut through the first wave, only to see the larger threat emerge from the back of the cavern carrying the source of the corruption itself.

### recap-008 | Reading the relic

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b09
- Date: 1748-03-25
- Time: afternoon
- Source Range: u2500 -> u2804
- Locations: Dunmari Fort
- NPCs: none
- Organizations: none
- Items: black iron staff with obsidian crystal
- Enemies: none

#### Short
With the guardian dead, the party studies the staff, connects it to old war magic, and identifies the flaw that might shatter the obsidian crystal.

#### Intermediate
Kenzo feels the relic pulling at him, Delwath suspects a surviving weapon of the Great War, and Seeker reads the crystal closely enough to spot the imperfection that gives them a chance to break it.

#### Long
The chamber is quieter now, but the staff proves more disturbing in stillness than it was in battle. As the party debates whether it belongs to Cha'mutte or some other Great War corruption, Seeker recognizes the crystal's structural flaw and the group slowly shifts from frightened speculation to a plan for destroying it where it stands.

### recap-009 | Shattering the obsidian

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b10
- Date: 1748-03-25
- Time: evening
- Source Range: u2805 -> u3098
- Locations: Dunmari Fort
- NPCs: none
- Organizations: none
- Items: Aagir's Everlight, black iron staff with obsidian crystal
- Enemies: none

#### Short
The party drives Aagir's Everlight into the flaw, shatters the obsidian after several painful attempts, and carries their finds back to the temple as the fort finally grows calm.

#### Intermediate
Kenzo first opens the crack with the everlight at great personal cost, Wellby widens it, and Delwath finishes the work with a shadow-forged blade, after which the insects disperse and the desert no longer feels poised to turn on them.

#### Long
As the staff tries to draw them into hesitation, the party commits to breaking it in the cavern before it can spread its influence any farther. Kenzo uses Aagir's Everlight to split the flaw, the others widen the damage despite psychic backlash, and Delwath lands the final stroke that bursts the crystal into shards, leaving the whole fort suddenly quiet enough for them to haul the statue and their relics back to the temple and rest.
