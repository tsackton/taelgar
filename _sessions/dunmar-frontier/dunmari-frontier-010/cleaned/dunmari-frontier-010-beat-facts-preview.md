## Vola's Warning
*b09*

**Date**: 1748-04-12
**Time Window**: evening
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: Back in the safer tower, Vola explains that she serves the Society of the Open Scroll and warns the party that Agata Dustmother is hunting old imperial relics in Raven's Hold.
**Long Summary**: Once the fight is over, the party relocates to the quieter tower rooms, returns Vola's weapons, and questions her about why she was imprisoned here. Vola reveals that she is Chardonian and part of the Society of the Open Scroll, that Arcas was another member, and that her captors serve a dangerous fey power named Agata Dustmother who is searching Dunmar and the blasted plains for relics of lost empire. By the time the party finishes comparing what each side knows, it is about seven in the evening and they agree they still need to learn more before trying to escape.
**Location**:
- Raven's Hold: the abandoned first tower where the party withdraws to question Vola after the rescue
  Note: The conversation happens in the safer tower rooms after the captors in the second tower are dealt with.
**NPCs**:
- Vola (encountered): rescued Chardonian relic-hunter. Vola identifies herself, recovers her gear, and explains why Agata's servants took her captive.
- Agata Dustmother (mentioned): dangerous fey seeker of relics. Vola says Agata and her servants are gathering imperial relics for some harmful purpose.
- Arcas (mentioned): another member of Vola's circle. Vola confirms that Arcas was also associated with the Society of the Open Scroll.
**Items**:
- imperial relics (mentioned): Vola says both her group and Agata's servants are searching the frontier for old relics left from imperial expansion and from Drankor's ruins.
**Organizations**:
- Society of the Open Scroll (encountered): Vola identifies herself and Arcas as members of this relic-hunting Chardonian society.
- Great Library (mentioned): Vola connects the Society of the Open Scroll to hidden circles associated with the Great Library in Chardon.
