#### Short
The party enters Raven's Hold through the hidden tunnel, destroys undead hobgoblins in an old prison, and maps the gnoll-guarded approaches before nightfall.

#### Long
Unable to pick the rusted gate, the party forces its way into Raven's Hold through a hidden tunnel and regroups in abandoned chambers below the hold. After a long debate over the valley, the iron guardian, and the risk of pressing on, Delwath scouts ahead, the party destroys two undead hobgoblins in a prison chamber, and they finish the afternoon by studying the hold's entrance plaza, towers, and animal pens from safer vantage points.

### Apr 12th, 1748 (evening)

- Timeline Segment: timeline-002
- Timeline Key: (DR:: 1748-04-12), evening
- Resolution: part-of-day
- Beat IDs: b06, b07, b08, b09
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: undead hobgoblins, Vola, orc, jackalwere guards
- Organizations: Blackened Claw, Society of the Open Scroll
- Items: none
- Combat Beats: b08

#### Short
At dusk, Kenzo scouts the towers, the party rescues Vola from an orc and two jackalwere guards, and Vola reveals Agata Dustmother's hunt for old relics.

#### Long
Once darkness gives them better cover, Kenzo slips through the first tower, crosses into the second, and discovers both a wounded prisoner and the enemies holding her. The party follows, kills the orc and the two jackalwere captors in a hard tower fight, then withdraws with Vola to a safer room where she explains that Agata Dustmother and her servants are searching Raven's Hold for relics of lost empire.

#### Intermediate
Unable to pick the rusted lock, the party lets Riswynn break the gate and then pushes through the hidden tunnel beneath Raven's Hold. The passage twists up into an abandoned storeroom where they haul everyone inside and rejoin Wellby.

#### Long
Unable to pick the rusted lock, the party gives up on finesse and lets Riswynn smash through the chained gate. Beyond it, a long hidden tunnel slopes down, spirals upward, and finally opens into a dusty storeroom beneath Raven's Hold. Once Riswynn climbs the last shaft and drops a rope, the others join her inside and regroup with Wellby after his separate look around the old dwarven outpost.

- NPCs: none

#### Short
Inside the abandoned storeroom, they compare notes about the valley, the hyena-like raiders, and the iron guardian before deciding to scout deeper.

#### Intermediate
Inside the abandoned storeroom, the party trades what each of them has seen of Raven's Hold, the valley below, and the dwarven defenses across the way. The talk turns into maps, theories, and arguments over whether they should retreat or keep moving forward.

#### Long
Inside the abandoned storeroom, the party finally has a moment to compare what each of them knows about Raven's Hold. Wellby's report from outside, the noise of the hyena-like raiders, and the sight of the great iron guardian across the valley all make the place feel more dangerous rather than less. Even so, after a long argument over maps, split-up plans, and the odds of surviving a deeper probe, they conclude that turning back now would leave too much unanswered.

### recap-003 | Delwath Scouts Ahead

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b03
- Date: 1748-04-12
- Time: afternoon
- Source Range: u0651 -> u0979
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: undead hobgoblins
- Organizations: none
- Items: none

#### Short
Delwath slips ahead invisibly and finds an old prison, two undead hobgoblins, and routes toward both the entrance plaza and the southern towers.

#### Intermediate
Delwath creeps forward under invisibility and discovers that the larger caverns ahead once held prisoners. There he spots two undead hobgoblins among the bones, hears animals near the northern entrance side, and confirms that a southern passage leads straight toward the towers and inner hold.

#### Long
Delwath's invisible scout turns the party's guesswork into something more concrete. The chambers ahead prove to be an old prison, where two undead hobgoblins rummage among skeletons and rotting remains. From there he also identifies the two crucial routes deeper in: a northern approach that seems to connect to the entrance plaza and its noisy animals, and a southern bridge route leading toward the towers that guard the inner stronghold.

Once Delwath reports what he found, the party decides the undead cannot be left wandering at their backs. They stage a fast ambush in the prison room, destroy both creatures, and then search the chamber for clues.

#### Long
Back in the prison chamber, the party decides that caution now means striking first. They set a quick ambush, destroy the two undead hobgoblins before the noise can spread far, and then look more carefully at the room the creatures had claimed. Iron manacles, decapitated skeletons, and the remains of an execution setup make it clear that the chamber once served as a hobgoblin prison and killing place.

### recap-005 | Watching Raven's Hold

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b05
- Date: 1748-04-12
- Time: afternoon
- Source Range: u1307 -> u1639
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: gnolls
- Organizations: none
- Items: none
- Enemies: none

#### Short
From the prison exits, they watch gnolls at the entrance, hear the penned animals nearby, and settle on sending Kenzo to scout the towers.

#### Intermediate
With the prison room secured, the party studies Raven's Hold from both the northern overlook and the southern tower approach. The northern side shows gnolls and a loud collection of animals, while the southern side offers the clearest path toward the towers and the keep.

#### Long
With the prison room secured, the party turns its attention outward. From the north they watch gnolls rotating guard near the entrance plaza while dogs, hyenas, jackals, and at least one lion make a constant racket nearby. From the south they confirm that the bridge, towers, and inner keep are still intact and still in use. By the end of the discussion, the best plan is clear: wait for darker cover and send Kenzo ahead invisibly.

- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: undead hobgoblins

#### Short
At dusk, Kenzo enters the first tower invisibly and finds it mostly empty except for signs of old Blackened Claw occupation.

#### Intermediate
Once darkness begins to help them, Delwath turns Kenzo invisible and sends him across the bridge into the first tower. Kenzo finds the lower rooms empty, sees more undead hobgoblins in the courtyard, and discovers an old command room marked with the Blackened Claw sign.

#### Long
At dusk, Kenzo slips into the first tower under Delwath's invisibility and starts climbing. The lower levels are empty, but the arrow slits and upper rooms give him a much better sense of the stronghold. He sees more undead hobgoblins moving in the courtyard below and finds an old commander's chamber whose painted Blackened Claw mark confirms that the hold was once occupied by that clan.

#### Short
Crossing into the second tower, Kenzo finds an orc, two lurking guards, and a wounded prisoner named Vola on the upper floor.

#### Intermediate
Kenzo presses on into the second tower and quickly realizes it is still occupied. An orc has made a home on one floor, two human-seeming guards prowl the level above, and a badly wounded prisoner lies bound in one of the upper rooms.

#### Long
Crossing from the first tower into the second, Kenzo moves from dead ruins into an occupied stronghold. He finds an orc using one level as living quarters, then climbs higher and discovers two guards watching over the upper floor. In one cramped room he finds a badly wounded armored prisoner tied on a bed of straw, calms her long enough to learn that her name is Vola, and then slips back to bring the others in before the chance disappears.

### recap-008 | Tower Rescue Fight

- Kind: combat
- Beat IDs: b08
- Date: 1748-04-12
- Time: evening
- Source Range: u2075 -> u2560
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: Vola, orc, jackalwere guards
- Organizations: none
- Items: none
- Enemies: orc, jackalwere guards

#### Short
When the party moves in, the rescue becomes a tower fight that ends with the orc and both jackalwere guards dead.

#### Intermediate
Kenzo guides the party into position, but once the guards react the rescue turns into a hard fight on the upper floors of the second tower. The orc joins the battle, the guards reveal themselves as jackalweres, and the party has to kill all three before Vola is truly safe.

#### Long
When the party finally moves in, stealth gives way to violence. One guard falls asleep for a moment, the orc charges into the fight, and the other captors shed their human-seeming shapes to reveal jackal-like hybrid forms that resist ordinary steel. The battle runs through the upper floors of the second tower until the party cuts down the orc and both jackalweres, secures the stairs, and finishes freeing Vola.

### recap-009 | Vola's Warning

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: b09
- Date: 1748-04-12
- Time: evening
- Source Range: u2561 -> u2875
- Locations: Raven's Hold
- NPCs: Vola
- Organizations: Society of the Open Scroll
- Items: none
- Enemies: none

#### Short
In the quieter tower, Vola identifies herself as a relic-hunter and says Agata Dustmother's servants are searching Raven's Hold for old imperial remains.

#### Intermediate
Once the party withdraws to the quieter tower, Vola recovers her weapons and explains who she is and why she came to Raven's Hold. She says she serves the Society of the Open Scroll and warns that Agata Dustmother's followers are hunting old relics across the frontier.

#### Long
In the quieter tower, the party finally has time to question the woman they rescued and return her gear. Vola explains that she is Chardonian, that she and Arcas both belong to the Society of the Open Scroll, and that she came north looking for old relics before Agata Dustmother's servants captured her. Her warning reframes everything in Raven's Hold: the place is not merely occupied, but part of a wider hunt for imperial remains that may already be reaching far beyond this ruin.
