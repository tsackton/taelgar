## Portents and the Fall
*beat-001*

**Date**: 1730-01-25
**Time Window**: afternoon
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: Omens worsen around the Great Chasm, the first investigators vanish, and the party is swept through a white storm while clutching what gear they can keep.
**Long Summary**: The session opens with a prologue about the Great Chasm's escalating signs and the disappearance of the Vejo Vaikai investigation party sent with Kemeko warriors. The beat then shifts into the fall itself, as the adventurers are torn through a blinding white storm and forced to decide what belongings they can hold onto before landing somewhere unknown.
**Location**:
- Great Chasm -> Zeyfa's Labyrinth: swept by unnatural storm into darkness below
  Note: The beat covers the omens over the chasm and the fall before the party awakens on the platform.
**NPCs**:
- Zeyfa (mentioned): cruel west wind of Zimkovan legend. Many people interpret the worsening omens around the chasm as signs of Zeyfa's anger.
- Kalima (encountered): terrified Zimkovan woman caught in the fall. Jrain tries to hold onto the woman as the storm carries everyone away.
**Items**: none
**Organizations**:
- Kemeko Monastery (mentioned): Its kenku are described as seeing the chasm as refuge and sending warriors with the investigation.
- Vejo Vaikai (mentioned): Their mystics view the chasm as a place of elemental balance and send the missing expedition.

## Kalima Names the Labyrinth
*beat-006*

**Date**: 1730-01-25
**Time Window**: afternoon
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: With the fight over, Kalima names Zeyfa's Labyrinth, describes her people's old flight into it, and warns that only the beast offers any hope of escape.
**Long Summary**: Finnan and Justas use the brief calm after the battle to draw a fuller account from the woman, who says they are back in Zeyfa's Labyrinth and that her people came here long ago to hide from a cataclysm. She insists that no one escapes, that the only way out is further in toward the beast, and that ordinary prayers do not carry here, though Justas steadies her with Romil's token and its light.
**Location**:
- Zeyfa's Labyrinth: ice platform after the battle
  Note: The group remains on the platform while Kalima gives her first coherent explanation of the place.
**NPCs**:
- Kalima (encountered): first coherent witness. She explains that they are back in the labyrinth and that the beast is tied to any chance of escape.
- Zeyfa (mentioned): cruel west wind. Kalima identifies the labyrinth as Zeyfa's domain and frames it as a place of demanded appeasement.
**Items**:
- Romil's token (encountered): Justas places a glacial token of Romil in Kalima's hands and later makes it glow to reassure her.
**Organizations**:
- Kestavo (mentioned): Kalima insists the Kestavo cannot hear prayers here, while Justas argues their reach is not wholly absent.

## Stories on the Platform
*beat-007*

**Date**: 1730-01-25
**Time Window**: afternoon
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: The party trades introductions, compares what they know about the chasm and the missing expedition, and learns that Kalima's people send sacrifices into the labyrinth at sunset.
**Long Summary**: Once Kalima is a little calmer, Finnan turns to the others, introduces himself properly, and frames her story against old tales of swamps, forgotten wars, and peoples who hid from disaster. Jrain explains the missing Vejo Vaikai expedition she was tracking, Ekko connects Eidrikas to his own past, and the group compares what it knows about Kemeko, the Kestavo, fey-like interpretations of the West Wind, and whether this place is truly the chasm. Finnan then coaxes one more answer from Kalima: when the sun sets and the new ones are old enough, the West Wind demands one of her people as a sacrifice into its labyrinth.
**Location**:
- Zeyfa's Labyrinth: same platform during a long conversation
  Note: The group stays on the platform while turning from Kalima's testimony to their own stories and theories.
**NPCs**:
- Kalima (encountered): huddled witness. She remains nearby while the party explains what they have learned and who they are.
- Eidrikas (mentioned): missing mystic and Ekko's mentor. Jrain identifies him as part of the vanished investigation party.
- Pakrinek (mentioned): missing Kemeko companion. Jrain names Pakrinek as one of the investigators who disappeared with Eidrikas.
- Zeyfa (mentioned): West Wind demanding sacrifice. Kalima describes the West Wind as demanding that one of her people be sent into its labyrinth.
**Items**: none
**Organizations**:
- Vejo Vaikai (mentioned): Their missing expedition becomes a central reason for Jrain's and Ekko's concern.
- Kemeko Monastery (mentioned): The party compares its public reputation with Ekko's account of it as a disciplined kenku stronghold.
- Kestavo (mentioned): Justas and Ekko discuss the local small-gods tradition and its relation to Zeyfa and the chasm.

## Rest, Recon, and Descent
*beat-008*

**Date**: 1730-01-25
**Time Window**: afternoon
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: The group takes a short rest, tests the platform's exits, scouts the bridge ahead, and commits to entering the tunnels with Kalima.
**Long Summary**: The party settles into a short rest while Finnan checks for magic and the storm above grows more threatening. They probe both bridges and the void below, learn that the updraft makes flying down impossible, and get Kalima's warning that all paths lead to the beast and that the maze takes travelers where it wants. After Rala scouts a snow chamber with four narrow tunnels and no obvious creatures, Justas and Finnan persuade Kalima to come between them as the group prepares to jump down into the snowbank and enter the maze.
**Location**:
- Zeyfa's Labyrinth: ice platform, bridge ends, and snow-chamber entrance
  Note: The beat begins on the platform and ends with the party committing to the first chamber of the labyrinth.
**NPCs**:
- Kalima (encountered): reluctant guide. She warns that the beast does not like waiting, says all paths lead toward it, gives her name, and agrees to come only if kept between Finnan and Justas.
- Rala (companion): Jrain's bird companion. She survives the fall, recovers during the short rest, and scouts the snow chamber ahead.
**Items**: none
**Organizations**: none
