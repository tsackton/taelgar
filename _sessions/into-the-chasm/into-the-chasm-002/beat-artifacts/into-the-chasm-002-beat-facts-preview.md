## Questions for the Dead
*beat-004*

**Date**: 1730-01-25
**Time Window**: afternoon
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: In a frozen corpse chamber, the party questions a dead villager about Melusa and Zeyfa's sacrifices, then loots a few useful relics before moving on.
**Long Summary**: The party discovers a human corpse pinned to the wall by ice and uses Speak with Dead to learn that he came from Melusa, died when the wind came alive, and had entered the labyrinth as a sunrise sacrifice. Kalima confirms the village name, the corpse describes Melusa as a cliffside settlement fed by a river and fish, and Finnan's ancestral memory links the name to Melua, an old waterfall Kestavo on the Teft; once the questioning ends, the party searches the body and takes a few salvageable items before continuing down the passage.
**Location**:
- Zeyfa's Labyrinth: corpse chamber, a small frozen side chamber with a corpse pinned to the wall by ice
  Note: This fixed location is a chamber within Zeyfa's Labyrinth; the passage continues beyond it, but the beat centers on the questioning and search.
**NPCs**:
- Unnamed man of Melusa (encountered): dead sacrifice questioned with magic. He says the village chose him at sunrise, the wind killed him, and his soul still feels trapped in the maze.
- Kalima (companion): interpreter and witness. She recognizes Melusa immediately and adds what she can about the village and its lost gods.
- Zeyfa (mentioned): cruel west wind tied to sacrifice and the labyrinth. The corpse identifies the wind's killer presence as Zeyfa's minion and frames the maze as Zeyfa's domain.
- Melua (mentioned): waterfall Kestavo recalled from halfling memory. Finnan connects Melusa's name to an old shrine on the Teft associated with Melua.
**Items**:
- plain silver ring (encountered): A simple silver ring worn on a necklace by the corpse, taken as the most distinctive token from his body.
**Organizations**:
- Kestavo (mentioned): The discussion of Zeyfa, Melua, sacrifice, and the dead man's trapped soul all turns on kestavo belief.

## The Looping White Maze
*beat-005*

**Date**: 1730-01-25
**Time Window**: afternoon
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: The party leaves the corpse behind, gets trapped in a looping white maze full of wind-creatures and booming passages, and finally sneaks out through the last remaining exit.
**Long Summary**: Past the corpse chamber, the labyrinth turns into a whiteout of snow hubs, booming corridors, and passages that loop back into the same room with fewer exits each time. Kalima is dropped and injured when the kenku try to carry her through the wind, Justas heals her and forces her through on foot, and the party repeatedly dodges the shadowy wind-creatures sweeping the maze until Finnan's ink mark proves the loop and Jrain's Pass Without Trace lets everyone slip through the final opening.
**Location**:
- Zeyfa's Labyrinth (corpse chamber) -> Zeyfa's Labyrinth (calmer passage beyond the white maze): moving through booming corridors and looping snow-hub rooms in the labyrinth's whiteout interior
  Note: The same room repeats with changing exits until the party narrows this section of Zeyfa's Labyrinth down to a single path out.
**NPCs**:

- wind-creatures (enemy): shadowy hunters sweeping the blizzard rooms. They appear as bone-and-wind humanoid forms and force the party to retreat, loop, and finally sneak past.

*beat-006*

**Date**: 1730-01-25
**Time Window**: afternoon
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: The party breaks into a collapsing ice passage, rides a black chute into a stormless chamber, and ends face to face with the shaggy beast Kalima fears.
**Long Summary**: After reaching calmer ground, the party retraces the booming hazard, emerges into a circular snow room, and chooses a right-hand tunnel hung with thin ice veils. Ekko's bonfire shatters the veils and triggers a collapse and avalanche, forcing everyone to jump into darkness and slide down slick black ice into a stormless black-stone chamber where Kalima panics, warns that a beast is here, and hides behind Finnan's illusion just before Justas calls the creature out and a towering shaggy white beast appears with shadowy forms growing nearby.
**Location**:
- Zeyfa's Labyrinth (calmer passage beyond the white maze) -> Zeyfa's Labyrinth (black-stone chamber): moving deeper through the booming gap, a circular snow room, and an ice-veiled collapse chute within the labyrinth

**NPCs**:
- Kalima (companion): terrified witness. She recognizes the chamber as the beast's territory, warns the party, and hides when Finnan gives her cover.
- shaggy white beast (enemy): revealed minion in the black-stone chamber. A towering white creature with dark red eyes emerges after Justas calls it out.
