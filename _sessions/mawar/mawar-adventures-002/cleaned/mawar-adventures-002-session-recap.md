- Kind: beat

- Locations: Hamri
- NPCs: Jaeson, Orden, Samar, Reliah
- Organizations: none

#### Short
From Jaeson, Orden, Samar, and Reliah, the party learns that a ragged old man has occupied the tower for weeks, smoking gatza and cleaning away his own traces.

#### Intermediate
Leaving the tower behind for the moment, the party splits up across Hamri and the Drowned Flats to gather rumor. Their informants all point toward the same figure: an older man who appeared only recently, kept to the ruin, and took pains to hide evidence of how he had been living.

#### Long
The search turns outward as the party works its contacts across Hamri, from Jaeson's hut to the Drowned Flats and the docks. Jaeson, Orden, Samar, and Reliah each provide a fragment, and together those fragments settle into one picture: a worn old man has been using the tower for weeks, throwing out fish remains, smoking gatza, and tidying the site just enough to disguise his stay. By the time the party regroups, suspicion has stopped being vague and started to narrow around a single culprit.

### recap-008 | Parley at Shark's Dock to Azar Falls and the Aftermath

- Kind: combat
- Beat IDs: beat-008, beat-009, beat-010
- Date: 1747-08-05 to 1747-08-09
- Time: morning
- Transcript Range: u2444 -> u3597
- Locations: Hamri
- NPCs: Azar the Lost, drowned servant, Sefa
- Organizations: none
- Items: lead-lined box, knife, brass bowl, Airion's books
- Enemies: Azar the Lost, drowned servant

#### Short
At Shark's Dock the parley breaks almost at once, and the battle against Azar and his drowned servant runs from the dock into the water before ending with his confession and a hard, necessary cleanup.

#### Intermediate
The morning confrontation goes wrong as soon as Nadine's name and the knife's influence tear through Azar's remaining composure. He answers with ice, grasping water, and a drowned servant, and the fight spreads across the dock and around the phantom boat before the party finally brings him down. In the days after, his confession leads them to the knife, Sefa, Airion's books, and the rest of the damage he left behind.

#### Long
The intercept at Shark's Dock lasts only a few breaths as conversation before the pressure inside Azar breaks outward into violence. Ice magic, constricting water, and a drowned servant turn the waterfront into a grinding fight that pulls the party off the dock, around the phantom boat, and back again until Azar is finally beaten. His confession then strips the mystery down to grief, Gazankoa, and the lure of the Sentient Ocean, and the days that follow are spent searching his lair, recovering Sefa and Airion's books, sealing away the knife, identifying the brass bowl, and seeing him sent south to begin making amends.
