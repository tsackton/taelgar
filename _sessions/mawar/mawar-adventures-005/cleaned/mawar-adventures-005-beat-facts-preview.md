## Recap and Raina's Plea
*beat-001*

**Date**: 1749-05-26
**Time Window**: evening
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: At the Leviathan Inn, Raina tells the party that the ocean's song has returned and begs them to recover a trapped fragment of it from a hidden shrine near Hamri.
**Long Summary**: The session opens with a broad recap of Hamri's recent history before Wazir gathers Ryu, Trok, Nerissa, and Ander at the Leviathan Inn to hear out a storm-battered sailor named Raina. She explains that she has heard the ocean speak in visions since childhood, that the songs fell silent years ago after a nightmare of tearing and loss, and that only recently they returned with a plea about something trapped under rock yet still resting on the waves. The voice showed her a shrine of Guzu the Mariner and begged for a fragment of song to be restored, so she has come to Hamri asking for help from people she has been told can be trusted.
**Location**:
- Leviathan Inn (Hamri): common room gathering as Wazir hears out a newly arrived sailor
  Note: The whole opening scene stays inside Wazir's inn while Raina explains why she came to Hamri.
**NPCs**:
- Raina (encountered): sailor carrying the ocean's plea. She asks the party to recover the lost fragment and return it to its home.
- Caleb (mentioned): Raina's grandfather. He raised Raina on stories of Mawar and was uneasy when the ocean's voice first went silent.
- Kaiso (mentioned): Chardonian scholar recently staying in Hamri. He appears in the session recap of Hamri's recent troubles and rumors from Chardon.
- Guzu the Mariner (mentioned): Mawarin saint seen in Raina's visions. Raina's picture of the hidden place centers on a shrine to Guzu.
**Items**:
- fragment of song (mentioned): Raina describes a lost fragment of the ocean's voice trapped somewhere in the cliffs near Hamri.
**Organizations**:
- Ocean Watchers (mentioned): Raina grew up on stories about them and Hamri's strange relationship with the sea.

- trapped water elemental: The fleeing elemental is finally finished off as it tries to regroup outside the chamber.
