## Recap and Rayna's Plea
*beat-001*

**Date**: 1749-05-26
**Time Window**: evening
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: At the Leviathan Inn, Rayna tells the party that the ocean's song has returned and begs them to recover a trapped fragment of it from a hidden shrine near Hamri.
**Long Summary**: The session opens with a broad recap of Hamri's recent history before Wazir gathers Ryu, Trok, Nerissa, and Ander at the Leviathan Inn to hear out a storm-battered sailor named Rayna. She explains that she has heard the ocean speak in visions since childhood, that the songs fell silent years ago after a nightmare of tearing and loss, and that only recently they returned with a plea about something trapped under rock yet still resting on the waves. The voice showed her a shrine of Guzo the Mariner and begged for a fragment of song to be restored, so she has come to Hamri asking for help from people she has been told can be trusted.
**Location**:
- Leviathan Inn (Hamri): common room gathering as Wazir hears out a newly arrived sailor
  Note: The whole opening scene stays inside Wazir's inn while Rayna explains why she came to Hamri.
**NPCs**:
- Rayna (encountered): sailor carrying the ocean's plea. She asks the party to recover the lost fragment and return it to its home.
- Caleb (mentioned): Rayna's grandfather. He raised Rayna on stories of Mawar and was uneasy when the ocean's voice first went silent.
- Kaeso (mentioned): Chardonian scholar recently staying in Hamri. He appears in the session recap of Hamri's recent troubles and rumors from Chardon.
- Guzo the Mariner (mentioned): Mawarin saint seen in Rayna's visions. Rayna's picture of the hidden place centers on a shrine to Guzo.
**Items**:
- fragment of song (mentioned): Rayna describes a lost fragment of the ocean's voice trapped somewhere in the cliffs near Hamri.
**Organizations**:
- Ocean Watchers (mentioned): Rayna grew up on stories about them and Hamri's strange relationship with the sea.

## The Fragment Returns
*beat-010*

**Date**: 1749-05-27
**Time Window**: afternoon
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: After the fight, Nerissa hears the recovered crystal's grateful song, the party returns it to Rayna, and Rayna accepts the task of carrying it to Omni.
**Long Summary**: With combat over, the party gathers around the crystal and Nerissa hears a faint melody of peaceful sleep and quiet gratitude. A quick search confirms that the shrine is mostly ruined and flooded, with little left beyond the offerings Trok already looted, so the group leaves the cave and swims back to Rayna. When they hand her the fragment, everyone shares a vision of an undersea home being rebuilt among kelp and fishfolk; Rayna recognizes the destination and says the crystal can guide her. The party returns to the Leviathan Inn that afternoon, helps Rayna settle in for one more night, and learns she means to sail across the Mawar Ocean toward Apporia and the merfolk city of Omni in the morning.
