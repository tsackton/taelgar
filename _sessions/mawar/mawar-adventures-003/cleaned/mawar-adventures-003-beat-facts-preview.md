## Up the Mirmir
*b03*

**Date**: 1747-10-24 to 1747-10-26
**Time Window**: unknown
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: Ryu leads the party up the Mirmir and through the forest to Yerkir-khor's buried monument.
**Long Summary**: Leaving Hamri, the party climbs above the waterfall and follows the Mirmir inland through autumn forest, relying on Ryu's tracking and Blu's scouting to stay on Hiyasa's trail. They make camp after dark, resume the search the next day, find Hiyasa's abandoned campsite, and follow the cut trail farther from the river. After a day and a half of pursuit, they reach a massive stone flower-like monument inscribed in Terran as the resting place of Yerkir-khor the Undaunted, inspect the ropes and open trap door, and stop just before anyone begins the descent.
**Location**:
- Hamri -> Yerkir-khor monument (Mirmir River interior): tracking Hiyasa upriver and through the inland forest
  Note: The beat spans departure, overnight travel, the abandoned campsite, and surface inspection of the monument.
**NPCs**:
- Hiyasa (mentioned): trail quarry. The party follows the signs of her recent passage.
- Blu (companion): Ryu's osprey. Scouts during the forest search.
- Yerkir-khor (mentioned): buried earth lord. Named in the Terran inscription on the monument.
**Items**:
- Terran monument inscription (encountered): Identifies Yerkir-khor as the Undaunted Prince of Earth, Lord of the Rock Below, and Guardian of the Crystal Skies.

## Return with the Spoils
*b11*

**Date**: 1747-10-26 to 1747-10-28
**Time Window**: unknown
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: With Hiyasa safe, the party loots the tomb, returns to Hamri, and identifies the strangest treasure from the jars.
**Long Summary**: After the guardian falls, Hiyasa explains that she thought this ancient tomb would be safer to rob than ancestral Mawar graves and admits she brought scrolls and a map from her years away. The party opens the gem-stoppered jars, finding archaic coins, rough gemstones, a pearl, yellow dust protecting ancient papers, red liquid that proves to be spoiled wine, and an obsidian jar with a one-way void that later shatters. They leave the tomb, disguise the entrance, return to Hamri a few days later, learn that the coins are ancient Orban, identify the pearl as a pearl of power, split about 600 gold of value, give Hiyasa a finder's fee, and leave the fragile unknown papers for later study.
**Location**:
- Yerkir-khor tomb -> Hamri: tomb aftermath, return travel, and division of spoils at the inn
  Note: The beat spans the immediate aftermath in the tomb and the later return to town.
**NPCs**:
- Hiyasa (encountered): rescued treasure hunter. Returns with the party, accepts a finder's fee, and offers more information about the other map marks for payment.
- Orden (mentioned): Hiyasa's father. The party discusses whether to give money to him and Hiyasa.
**Items**:
- Hiyasa's treasure map (encountered): Shows three marked locations, including the tomb they just explored.
- ancient Orban coins and gems (encountered): Together worth about 600 gold after the return to Hamri.
- pearl of power (encountered): Recovered from the jars and eventually identified as a magic item that can restore a spell slot.
- yellow dust and ancient papers (encountered): The magical dust preserves a fragile sheaf of papers in an unknown language.
- obsidian void jar (encountered): Appears to contain a hole to nowhere but shatters after removal from the tomb.
**Organizations**: none
