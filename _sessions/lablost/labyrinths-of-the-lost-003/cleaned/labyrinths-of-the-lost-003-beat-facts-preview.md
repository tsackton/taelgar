## Into the Bolthole

**Time Window**: midday
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: The party descends from the haunted bedroom into the old pirate bolthole beneath Perdoli Manor and scouts the cavern beyond.

**Time Window**: midday
**Combat**: no
