#### Short
Descending from the haunted bedroom into the hidden pirate bolthole, the party destroyed the undead sentries and bluffed Arturo rather than fighting him.

#### Long
Following the ghost's directions, the party entered the old pirate hideaway beneath Perdoli Manor, scouted the burned entrance cavern, and set up an ambush that wiped out the undead pirate shades and shadows there. After a short rest, they opened the next door, talked their way past Arturo in the torchlit guard room, and moved deeper into the lair without raising the alarm.

### Oct 18th, 1748 (afternoon)

- Timeline Segment: timeline-002
- Timeline Key: (DR:: 1748-10-18), afternoon
- Resolution: part-of-day

- Items: potion bandolier, volatile potion bandolier, anti-petrification potions, healing potions, glowing poison recipe, oil of slipperiness
- Combat Beats: beat-004, beat-006, beat-007

#### Short
In the afternoon they killed Arturo, stormed Grimstone's laboratory, slew him, and barely survived the constructs and petrifying venom he left behind.

#### Long
The party decided Arturo was too dangerous to leave alive, lured him into the caverns, and cut him down before he could warn the lair. They then pushed into Grimstone's laboratory, where the masked alchemist fought with thrown potions, smoke, poison, and animated stone creatures until he was killed; even then the battle continued long enough for Acescale to be dropped and nearly petrified before the last constructs were destroyed and the lab's potions were sorted.

### Oct 18th, 1748 (night)

- Timeline Segment: timeline-003
- Timeline Key: (DR:: 1748-10-18), night
- Resolution: part-of-day
- Beat IDs: beat-008
- Locations: Perdoli Manor
- NPCs: Dilion's real dog, mimic
- Organizations: none
- Items: The Way of the Stone, pirate hideout maps, ring of birdcalls, charlatan's die
- Combat Beats: none

#### Short
That night the party finished searching the lair, recovered Dilion's real dog and the treasure cache, avoided a mimic, and settled in for a safe long rest.

#### Long
After another short rest, the party searched Grimstone's private notes on the Prime Arcana Stone, opened the storeroom, and found Dilion's real dog tied beside two treasure chests. One chest yielded pirate maps, The Way of the Stone, Acescale's poker winnings, a ring of birdcalls, and a charlatan's die; the other proved to be a mimic, so they backed away and bedded down in the cleared lair.

### Oct 19th, 1748 (morning)

- Timeline Segment: timeline-004
- Timeline Key: (DR:: 1748-10-19), morning
- Resolution: part-of-day
- Beat IDs: beat-009
- Locations: Perdoli Manor
- NPCs: none
- Organizations: none
- Items: pirate hideout maps, ring of birdcalls, charlatan's die
- Combat Beats: none

#### Short
At dawn they slipped out through the chaos at Perdoli Manor, divided the spoils on the road down into the valley, and narrated where each survivor went next.

#### Long
Early the next morning they crept out before the manor fully woke, passing hobgoblins enraged by Grimstone's disappearance while the goblins fled. Once clear, they split treasure on the road down into the valley, and each character sketched a future shaped by the maps, ring, die, and hard-won freedom.

They destroy the shades and shadows, examine the scorched skeletons, recover a melted silver-and-gold ingot, infer that a navy assault once burned the pirates alive here, and then take a short rest before pressing deeper.

After the rest, the party finds Arturo in the next cave room and bluffs their way past him instead of fighting.

#### Intermediate
Beyond the scorched cavern, the party reaches a torchlit guard room where Arturo is exercising and trying to look less afraid than he feels. Gaudin and Loria improvise a story about ingredients for Grimstone, and the lie holds long enough for the party to search the room and move on.

Leaving the scorched cavern, the party opens the incongruous wooden door into the next section of the lair and sneaks to a torchlit guard room where a burly human servant is working out. Gaudin and Loria improvise a story about bringing ingredients to Grimstone, Arturo nervously half-believes them, tries a basilisk draught on the steampup, and ultimately backs off while the party pockets empty vials and other alchemical gear.

### recap-006 | Grimstone's Last Experiment to Aftermath in the Lab

- Kind: combat
- Beat IDs: beat-006, beat-007
- Date: 1748-10-18
- Time: afternoon
- Transcript Range: u1420 -> u1965
- Locations: Perdoli Manor
- NPCs: Grimstone, stone wasp
- Organizations: none
- Items: volatile potion bandolier, anti-petrification potions, healing potions, glowing poison recipe, oil of slipperiness
- Enemies: Grimstone, stone spider swarm, stone wasp

#### Short
Grimstone's lab fight erupts as the party swarms the masked alchemist and he answers with fire, poison, smoke, and animated stone spiders, and even after he falls the room stays deadly.

#### Intermediate
The assault turns into a chaotic laboratory battle as Grimstone hurls volatile potions, fills the room with smoke, and animates stone creatures to cover himself. Loria eventually cuts him down, but the spider swarm and stone wasp keep mauling the party until Dilion and Loria stabilize Acescale, finish the constructs, and sort the surviving potions.

#### Long
Initiative breaks out in Grimstone's cluttered laboratory as he hurls volatile potions while the party dives around benches and shattered statuary to pin him down. Acescale poisons him with ray of sickness, Loria slips under the bench to cut at his legs, Gaudin trades eldritch blasts with him, and the steampup leaps the table, but Grimstone still fills the room with smoke and animates the stone spider swarm before Loria finally cuts him down. With Grimstone dead, the encounter stays dangerous as the last spiders and an animated stone wasp keep attacking, repeatedly dropping Acescale to zero with petrifying venom before Dilion and Loria finally stabilize the situation. Once the constructs are broken, the party identifies the lab's surviving stock, including healing draughts, anti-petrification potions, a potion of growth, a potion of advantage, a glowing poison recipe, and oil of slipperiness, then decides to rest before sweeping the final rooms.

They recover a key, the old book The Way of the Stone, pirate-route maps and valuables including Acescale's poker winnings, a ring of birdcalls, and a loaded gambler's die, realize the second chest is a mimic and back away, then settle in for a long rest in the cleared lair.
