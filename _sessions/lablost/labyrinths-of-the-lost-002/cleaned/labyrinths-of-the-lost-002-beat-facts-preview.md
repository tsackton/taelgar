## Ghost and Locket
*beat-007*

**Date**: 1748-10-18
**Time Window**: morning
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: The party investigates the manor's statue-filled rooms, uses a locket to calm a mourning sailor ghost, and learns Grimstone's lair lies below the manor.
**Long Summary**: The party peeks into the great hall and private rooms, finding fresh lifelike statues, older headless statues, broken mirror glass, a sailor ghost in a bedchamber, and a study containing a silver locket, smuggler's map, love letters, and documents from an old pirate or smuggler hideout. Gaudin presents the locket, disguises himself as the woman in its portrait, and draws the name Petrona from the ghost, who explains that Grimstone, the white-faced man, lied about bringing him news and rest; the ghost then reveals a trapdoor beneath the room leading to his old hideout and Grimstone's laboratory below, where Grimstone grinds stone fingers into potions while seeking the immortality of stone.
