**Main Enemies**:
- hobgoblin crypt scouts: Two hobgoblins investigate the chapel hatch and are killed in the party's trap.
**Combat Notes**: Grease, snare, and the cramped crypt let the party kill both pursuers without turning the whole manor into an open fight.

## Pirate Clues and Ghost
*beat-006*

**Date**: 1748-10-18
**Time Window**: morning
**Combat**: no
**Short Summary**: Recovering from the basilisk scare, the party explores the master chambers, pieces together pirate clues around J.M. and Petrona, and persuades the ghostly captain to reveal Grimstone's lair beneath the bedroom.
**Long Summary**: After debating whether the basilisk can be turned against the manor, the party continues through the private chambers rather than pressing the courtyard. In the old master rooms they find smashed mirrors, headless statues, a silver locket, pirate maps, and letters addressed to J.M. from P., which slowly recast the manor as a former pirate refuge layered atop the older Perdoli villa. Those clues give Gaudin enough to risk speaking with the pacing sailor ghost in the bedroom. By using the locket and then disguising himself as Petrona, Gaudin breaks through the ghost's rage and grief, learns that Grimstone lied to him for years, and gets the crucial revelation that Grimstone's hideout lies beneath the room in the old pirate base below.
**Location**:
- Perdoli Manor (Western Cymea): former master bedchambers, pirate-used rooms, and the haunted bedroom above the hidden cellar entrance
  Note: The final beat stays in the manor's private chambers and ends with the trapdoor location under the bedroom.
**NPCs**:
- J.M. (encountered): pirate ghost guarding the bedroom. The ghost mourns Petrona and finally turns against Grimstone after Gaudin's deception succeeds.
- Petrona (mentioned): lost lover identified from the locket and letters. The letters and the locket reveal that P. was Petrona.
- Grimstone (mentioned): white-faced liar beneath the manor. The ghost identifies Grimstone as the man who promised news and rest but kept him trapped instead.
**Items**:
- silver locket (encountered): Inscribed to J.M. from P.; it becomes the key to calming the ghost.
- pirate maps (encountered): The papers in the chambers include smuggling and pirate-route materials.
- love letters to J.M. (encountered): The letters explain the bond between J.M. and Petrona and help the party interpret the haunting.
**Organizations**: none
