### recap-002 | Dawn Plans and Chapel

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: beat-002
- Date: 1748-10-18
- Time: dawn
- Transcript Range: u0061 -> u0489
- Locations: Perdoli Manor
- NPCs: shadow-cloaked hobgoblin patrol
- Organizations: none
- Items: none
- Enemies: none

#### Short
The party wakes before sunrise, commits to the chapel route, draws a hobgoblin patrol away with steampup, and reaches the ruined chapel after cutting through the overgrown grounds.

#### Intermediate
Once the party wakes and compares routes, the ruined chapel becomes the favored path toward the hidden tunnels. With Gaudin watching his ring and Loria watching the walls, the group then makes a careful crossing through the vineyards and formal grounds while a shadow-cloaked hobgoblin slips along the perimeter. Dilion sends steampup off barking to pull the patrol and nearby goblins away, giving the party room to pass the medusa fountain, reach the ruined chapel, and uncover the hidden trapdoor into the crypt.

#### Long
The dawn infiltration begins with a planning conversation rather than immediate movement, as the party wakes in dim pre-sunrise light and weighs the chapel, kitchens, and other possible approaches into the manor. Once the ruined chapel wins out, the actual crossing begins badly enough to feel doomed, with stealth checks, noisy movement, and a shadow-cloaked hobgoblin patrol already ranging along the walls. Loria spots the patrol in time for the group to adjust, and Dilion's steampup becomes the key to the whole crossing by barking its way across the grounds and dragging the patrol and barracks goblins after it. That distraction buys the party the precious minutes it needs to push through the hedges, glimpse the petrified medusa at the fountain, and search the ruined chapel under mounting pressure. A suspicious patch of flowers finally gives the game away, and the buried trapdoor is exposed just in time for everyone to dive below.

### recap-003 | Ambush in the Crypt

- Kind: combat
- Beat IDs: beat-003
- Date: 1748-10-18
- Time: morning
- Transcript Range: u0490 -> u0888
- Locations: Perdoli Manor
- NPCs: hobgoblin shadowsneak, second hobgoblin pursuer
- Organizations: none
- Items: silver candlesticks
- Enemies: hobgoblin crypt scouts

#### Short
The crypt becomes a killing ground as the party traps the hatch, kills two investigating hobgoblins, and keeps the chapel above from turning into an open alarm.

#### Intermediate
The dusty chapel crypt seems peaceful at first, but Gaudin's glowing ring warns that danger is already overhead. Grease and snare turn the hatch into a perfect ambush, and the first shadowy scout dies in the trap before a second hobgoblin can follow him down to a better fate.

#### Long
The chapel crypt opens into an unexpectedly solemn family tomb, quiet enough that the party almost has time to appreciate the religious silver and stillness below. That peace breaks the moment Gaudin's ring starts glowing and footsteps sound overhead. Dilion and Loria convert the hatch into a layered trap with grease and snare, then hold their nerve until the first hobgoblin scout drops into the chamber and is cut down in brutal close quarters. Resetting the trap immediately pays off when a second pursuer follows, letting the party end the threat before the whole manor can converge on the chapel.

### recap-004 | The Hidden Manor Study

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: beat-004
- Date: 1748-10-18
- Time: morning
- Transcript Range: u0889 -> u1189
- Locations: Perdoli Manor
- NPCs: none
- Organizations: none
- Items: alchemist's fire, topaz, cure wounds scroll, potion of watchful rest
- Enemies: none

#### Short
With the crypt safe for the moment, the party uncovers hidden valuables, finds the secret route into a sealed Perdoli study, and works out the rotating bookcase that opens the way deeper into the manor.

#### Intermediate
The crypt search turns up more than relic silver, including alchemist's fire, gemstones, a hidden cure wounds scroll, and a potion tucked into the tomb. From there the party discovers a concealed passage into a dusty private study, identifies the Perdoli family name, takes a short rest in relative safety, and then decodes the fake books and hidden button that control the rotating bookcase.

#### Long
Once the immediate crypt fight is over, the party shifts back into cautious exploration and is rewarded for it. Searching the tomb thoroughly yields a few useful spoils, including alchemist's fire, a topaz, a hidden cure wounds scroll, and a potion of watchful rest tucked into a niche. More important still, the crypt hides a secret route that rises into a sealed study untouched by the traffic of Grimstone's current servants. There the Perdoli family name finally emerges from the papers and furnishings, and the room proves safe enough for a brief rest before the group starts testing the shelves, discovering the hidden mechanism in the bookcase, and preparing to push into the inhabited parts of the manor.

### recap-005 | Gallery Fight and Basilisk

- Kind: combat
- Beat IDs: beat-005
- Date: 1748-10-18
- Time: morning
- Transcript Range: u1190 -> u1357
- Locations: Perdoli Manor
- NPCs: hobgoblin manor guards, chained basilisk
- Organizations: none
- Items: none
- Enemies: hobgoblin manor guards

#### Short
The party slips through the rotating bookcase, beats a small group of manor guards without raising a full alarm, and then learns too late that a chained basilisk waits in the courtyard.

#### Intermediate
The route beyond the study offers no easy answer: one door leads toward giant insects and another across a more exposed gallery, so the group chooses the gallery and keeps moving. That decision produces a fast indoor skirmish with hobgoblin guards, and the fight is barely over before Gaudin looks over the courtyard wall and starts turning to stone under the basilisk's gaze.

#### Long
The hidden bookcase opens onto a much more dangerous stretch of the manor, where every choice carries fresh risk. A quick scout reveals a room full of giant insects to one side and a gallery route to the other, and the party chooses exposure over swarms. That choice proves good enough to keep the manor from fully waking, but not good enough to avoid another fight: a small group of hobgoblin guards rushes in and is cut down in a sharp, contained skirmish that the party wins before a wider alarm can spread. Even that success curdles immediately when Gaudin takes a cautious look into the courtyard, meets the gaze of a chained basilisk, and has to be hacked free of the stone already creeping over him.

### recap-006 | Pirate Clues and Ghost

- Kind: beat
- Beat IDs: beat-006
- Date: 1748-10-18
- Time: morning
- Transcript Range: u1358 -> u1731
- Locations: Perdoli Manor
- NPCs: J.M.
- Organizations: none
- Items: silver locket, pirate maps, love letters to J.M.
- Enemies: none

#### Short
Recovering from the basilisk scare, the party searches the master chambers, pieces together the story of J.M. and Petrona, and wins Grimstone's location from the ghost guarding the bedroom.

#### Intermediate
Rather than force the courtyard, the group turns inward and finds smashed mirrors, headless statues, a silver locket, pirate maps, and letters that reveal the manor once sheltered smugglers as well as the older Perdoli family. Those clues give Gaudin enough to face the pacing ghost in the bedroom, use Petrona's identity against his grief, and draw out the truth about Grimstone's lair below.

#### Long
The basilisk forces a pause, but not a retreat. While Acescale and the others toy with mirror schemes and ways to turn the chained monster into a weapon, the deeper mystery of the manor starts to unfold in the master chambers. Smashed mirrors, old statues, pirate maps, and a silver locket inscribed to J.M. from P. reveal that the ruined villa became a pirate refuge after the Perdoli family fell away, and the letters make it clear that Petrona and J.M. were lovers divided by the sea and the navy. Armed with that story, Gaudin confronts the ghost in the bedroom, first with the locket and then by disguising himself as Petrona. The gambit works: J.M.'s rage breaks into grief, then fury at Grimstone, and the pirate ghost finally reveals that Grimstone's hideout lies beneath the room in the old base below.
