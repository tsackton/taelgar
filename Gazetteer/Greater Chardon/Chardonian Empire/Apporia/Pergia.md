# Pergia

Pergia is a small coastal city in [[Portalia]], near the southern entrance to the [[Old Chardon Canal]] on [[Emerald Bay]].

In the chaotic years after the [[Great War]], [[Illoria|Illorian]] pirates under [[Chirce]] made Pergia their capital, profiting from the canal trade.
