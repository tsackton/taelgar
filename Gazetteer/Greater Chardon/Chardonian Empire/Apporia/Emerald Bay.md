# Emerald Bay

Emerald Bay is a broad bay on the [[Nevos Sea]] side of [[Apporia|the Apporian Peninsula]]. The [[Yeraad]] reaches the sea here, and the southern entrance to the [[Old Chardon Canal]] opens onto the bay near [[Pergia]].
