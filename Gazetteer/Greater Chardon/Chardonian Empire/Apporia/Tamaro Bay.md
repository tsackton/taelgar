# Tamaro Bay

Tamaro Bay is a bay on the western coast of [[Apporia|the Apporian Peninsula]]. It is best known in folk song: the [[Ballad of the Jade Garden]] places a legendary kelp-forest “jade garden” *a hundred leagues west o’ Tamaro Bay*.
