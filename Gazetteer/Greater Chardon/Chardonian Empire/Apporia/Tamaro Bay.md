# Tamaro Bay
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Tamaro Bay is a bay on the western coast of [[Apporia|the Apporian Peninsula]]. It is best known in folk song: the [[Ballad of the Jade Garden]] places a legendary kelp-forest “jade garden” *a hundred leagues west o’ Tamaro Bay*. It is rumored to be a location where merfolk can ocassionally be seen, and their songs heard on the waves.
