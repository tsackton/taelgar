# The Riftstone Gorge
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Riftstone Gorge is a deep, broken canyon system cut into the dry northern flank of the [[Chataan Mountains]], beneath the towering bulk of [[Thundercrown]]. The wide central canyon is flanked by a maze of cliffs, shelves, slot-canyons, and narrow riparian bottoms. Most of the year it is a land of dust, wind, pale stone, and sparse hardy woodland, but after rains it briefly greens with grasses, herbs, and sudden wildflowers. The gorge is harsh, mineral-rich, and strategically important: difficult to cross, dangerous to travel, but full of hidden seeps, mine cuts, and old roads clinging to the walls.

The Riftstone Gorge was the site of a major Chardonian defeat, the [[Reports on Chalyte in Alta Tonaro#Report of the Riftstone Gorge Disaster|Riftstone Gorge Disaster]]. The gorge remains a byword for the dangers of the deep chalyte reserves in the Chataans.
