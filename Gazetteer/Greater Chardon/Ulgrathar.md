# Ulgrathar
>[!info]+ Information

Ulgrathar is a giant realm in the [[Chataan Mountains]], remembered in Chardonian sources chiefly for its rich deposits of [[chalyte]] in the valleys below [[Thundercrown]]. In recent years it has been ruled by the stone giant [[Dhrukmir]], and stone giants such as [[Brunnar]] are known to have lived under his dominion.
