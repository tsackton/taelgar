# Urlich Pass
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Urlich Pass is a high pass through the [[Sentinel Range|Sentinels]]. Before the [[Great War]], it was an important crossing of the mountains, linking the western duchies of [[Sembara]] to the [[Chardon]] and the many diverse realms west of the [[Sentinel Range|Sentinels]]. In DR 1545, the passes of Urlich were the site of the [[Battle of Urlich Pass]], the climactic battle of the [[Great War]], where [[Cha'mutte]] was defeated. In the aftermath, the pass and the surrounding highlands were left shattered and dangerous.
