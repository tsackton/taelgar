# Lake Aeulian
*(ay-oo-LYAHN)*

A small lake at the confluence of the [[Andonne]] and the [[Valmont]], which forms the headwaters of the [[Istaros]]. Known to be very deep and, before the plagues came, said to be pure and clear.
