### The Elderwood

The southernmost part of what once was called [[Ainumarya]], an ancient forest. 

Once the southern extent of [[Ainumarya]]; now inhabited by several Deno'qai tribes: [[Te'kula]], [[Baz'aku]], [[Bek'eni]].
