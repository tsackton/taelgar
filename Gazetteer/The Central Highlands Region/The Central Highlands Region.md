# Central Highlands

### Overview

This region of Taelgar includes everything north of [[Dunmar]], east of the [[Chardonian Empire]], west of [[Sembara]], and south of [[The Far North Region]]: roughly speaking, this includes the [[~CentralMountains~]], the [[dwarven kingdoms]] within them, and the extensive forests and highlands that extend along much of the western side of the [[~CentralMountains~]]. 

### Climate

This region of Taelgar is largely an inland, [continental climate](https://geodiode.com/climate/continental), generally with balanced rainfall or with wet winters and dry summers at the lower elevations. At higher elevations, the climate is temperate [highlands](https://geodiode.com/biomes/highlands), with the highest peaks covered in ice and snow year round.
