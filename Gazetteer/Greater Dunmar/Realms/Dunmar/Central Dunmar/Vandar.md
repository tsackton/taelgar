# Vandar
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

An abandoned mining village in the [[Copper Hills]]. The village is set in the hills, on a bit of a rise. While most of the town is just ruins, the temple of the [[Five Siblings]], on a larger hill, is still mostly intact, albeit completely looted of any valuables. A short walk from the village is a flooded copper mine shaft. 

%%SECRET
Dummari ghost town around a copper mine (C3, hex 2210). There were once a number of villages in the hills; most have since disappeared without a trace, but the town of Vandar was one of the better constructed/fortified, and the ruins survive, along with the shafts of the coppers mines it was famous for.

Once a mining town, now a ruin, but with more intact buildings that many of the other abandoned villages in the area. In the past few months a pair of wyverns have moved in and now hunt here.

Other features include a flooded mine, and the still sort-of-intact temple.

Approach:  wyvern lair is hard to miss.

Wyvern Lair: main feature of the town; the wyverns will fly up and attack anyone who approaches their lair, although they will not really bother creatures just wandering in the town, as long as they have already eaten for the day.

Some treasure is found on the road, improvise

Treasure: the wyvern treasure is the remnants of travelers and horses they have killed, and they do not gather it or protect it as a hoard:

6 pouches, containing 12 gp and a small but beautiful turquoise (exquisite common gemstone); 10 gp and 68 sp; 8 gp and 122 sp; 11 gp and 420 cp; 32 sp and 433 cp; 24 gp and 142 sp (Perception check to find, need > 22 to get all of them)

Elaborate decorative leather saddle (exquisite uncommon trade good)

Golden bracelet set with tiny pearls (normal common art object)

Leather satchel with blank, high quality vellum (exquisite common trade good)

Eight bolts of silk (rare trade good) -- half in okay shape (normal), the other half damaged (inferior)

Four barrels of salt (common trade good) - mostly broken and scattered

Five chests filled with high quality parchment (uncommon trade good) -- all damaged (inferior)

Random collection of weapons and armor (generally common trade goods)

Temple: temple is only intact building, but is completely looted and trashed, no treasure to be found. Improvise details.

Flooded mine: a ways up the path from town, the copper mines are here, but the main entrance is completely flooded.

Poison mire: the venom of the wyverns flows down with the afternoon rains, collecting in a swampy area abutting the ruined wall of the town. The acrid stench can be smelled 100 feet away, and there is a greenish vapor that hangs over the area. Anyone in the contact with the water or breathing the vapor takes 2d6 poison damage per round (save for half, DC 12 CON) and is poisoned once they take 10 total poison damage.

Lurking in the water is a venom troll (except change regeneration to fire or lightning to stop) and a poison weird. The venom troll is gaunt and wasting away - this troll used to make the village its lair but was "defeated" and corrupted by the wyverns.
