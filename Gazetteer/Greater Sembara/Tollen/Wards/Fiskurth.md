An ancient district in [[Tollen]] and part of the original Skaer city, Fiskurth is a dense warren of narrow, bustling streets along the north-bank waterfront, filled with overhanging wooden buildings. It is now the heart of the fishing trade, crowded with fish markets, sailorâ€™s inns, and the low docks where the inshore fleets unload their catch. Fiskurth is loud, smelly, and full of activity -- beggars, hawkers, horsecarts of rich merchants, peddlers on packhorses selling water, throngs of people.
