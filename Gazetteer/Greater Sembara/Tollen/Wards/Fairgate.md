Fairgate was established as a ward of Tollen when the walls were expanded west to enclose the ancient fort on [[Haurhill]]. At the time, open fields lay to the north, which was kept as a space for markets. Over time, as the [[Fairgrounds (Tollen)|Fairgrounds]] were established by halfling caravans, village-like sprawl grew up outside the walls, forming [[Fairgate Outer]], with the original Fairgate ward renamed [[Fairgate Inner]].
