The [[Tollen Bridge]] is a broad stone span of many arches, built in the Age of Drankor. Its piers choke the Volta into narrow, dangerous channels. Seagoing ships never pass beyond the bridge; they dock below its shadow, and cargo is carried upriver by low-masted barges that slip under the central arches.
