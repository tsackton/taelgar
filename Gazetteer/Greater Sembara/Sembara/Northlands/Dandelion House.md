# The Dandelion House

![[dandelion-house.jpg|left|400]]Dandelion House is an ancient late-[[Drankorian Empire|Drankorian]] manor in [[Varrow Forest]], northwest of [[Tollen]]. 

The house is both majestic and decrepit: a large stone manor, hundreds of years old, with missing window panes, warped doors, vines, moss, brush, and saplings overtaking much of the structure. Despite the decay, its stonework remains solid; its facade bears a dandelion puffball motif, and its empty upper windows and open front doors can seem either like a masterpiece of late Drankorian architecture or like a face waiting to consume visitors.The grounds, though overgrown, contain the remnants of the ancient manor: parts of an old wall, a ruined gate with traces of a portcullis, and a long-swallowed moat. 

A hidden door in the root-covered lower cellar leads into a vast root tunnel connected to the Feywild.

In 1740 DR, [[Lord Holda]] claimed the overgrown estate, through an old deed that made his inheritance conditional on ending a treaty beyond the hidden door.
