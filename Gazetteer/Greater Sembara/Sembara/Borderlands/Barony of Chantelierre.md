# The Barony of Chantelierre
*(SHAWN-tel-ee-air)*
>[!info]+ Information

The Barony of Chantelierre is the easternmost of the baronies of the [[Borderlands]]. It is bounded to the east by the [[Aure]] and lies between the slopes of the [[Mostreve Hills]] and the [[Enst]] valley.
