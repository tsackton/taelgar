# Arvanko's Homestead
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The stoneborn [[Rangers|ranger]] [[Arvanko]] lives in an isolated homestead southeast of [[Roscombe]], on the eaves of the [[Torvaine Forest]]. 

His home is notable for the still-growing, hand-carved stone monument, shaped from a hillside by [[Arvanko]], dedicated to the [[Rangers]] and others who died in western [[Addermarch]]. The monument is a low circular stone wall, on which many names are carved, dating to the early days of the Rangers in the [[Serpentine Wars|wars against Avatus]]. There is space at the four cardinal points for large statues, currently occupied by uncarved standing stones with rough etchings of a fox, a tree, a watchtower, and a staff.
