# Highmoor
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Highmoor is a band of rolling downs, rocky ridges, scrub, and broken limestone outcrops north and east of [[Roscombe]], between the settled approaches of [[Carlinshire]] and the higher ground of [[Westcliff]]. It is less wooded, poorer-soiled, and more thinly settled than the [[Stonewolds]] to the west. Its people are mostly shepherds, cattle-herders, charcoal burners, and crofters living in tiny upland villages.

Rough cart tracks cross the moor between [[Roscombe]], [[Brantor]], and scattered highland settlements, but travel is slow and exposed. The moor supports pockets of woodland, sheltered dales, small springs, summer pastures, and isolated caves or hollows.

## Locations

[[Haldrenn]] is a remote Highmoor village known for cattle, charcoal, and wooden ridge-wards tied to local fey traditions.

[[Nightsong Roots]] is a fog-filled hollow east of [[Haldrenn]], where the roots of a fallen tree form a portal to the [[Feywild]].

A cave system in northern Highmoor was turned into a trap-filled lair by goblins in DR 1715, until the [[Addermarch - Session 05|lair was cleared]] by the [[Addermarch Mercenaries]] after the goblins captured [[Colette du Bois]] and stole an alchemical recipe sought by [[Caradoc]].
