# Selgrava

>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Selgrava is a small river in [[Breva]] that joins the [[Teft]]. It is the first river to join the Teft south of the [[Great Chasm]], and the forests around the confluence with the Teft is home to the [[Vejo Vaikai]].
