# Vistalba
*(VEESS-tahl-bah)*
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Vistalba is notable market town in [[Western Cymea]], a trading port on the [[Western Gulf]] with a decent harbor. The harbor dates to [[Drankorian Empire|Drankorian]] days, when Vistalba was richer and handled the movement of Cymean wine, silver, and other goods.

The town has declined from that earlier prosperity, leaving it with a run-down, lost-in-the-past character. Even so, its old harbor remains a fine and safe port, and it looks to investment from Tollen looks to revive its fortunes.
