# Everlight's Bloom
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

A land of perpetual afternoon light, a place of eternal warmth, where flowers always bloom and the bright sun turns everything to gold. Everlight's Bloom is ruled by the Golden Prince, [[Prince Aurelian]]. 

%%SECRET
Fey realm west of Amberglow.

Ruled by Prince Aurelian, known as the Golden Prince, the Hearthkeeper, and the Sunbound Oracle. 

Vibe is: beautiful and meditative but inward-focused, the Golden Prince does not leave his realm and there is maybe a hidden undercurrent of "doesn't let his people leave either". 

Idea is not: hard to leave (like Fortune's Rest), or even particularly inhospitable. It is just - we are for ourselves, and no one else. 

A land of golden afternoon light, fields of flowers, blooming trees. 

Lirien's quote about Everlight's Bloom:

- **"The sun never sets in Everlight’s Bloom. The light is warm, golden, eternal—but after a time, even gold can feel like a cage."**    
- **"Flowers bloom in every color, filling the air with a scent so rich it makes you forget hunger, weariness… even the weight of time itself."**
- **"Prince Aurelian is kind, in his way. He does not command; he simply waits. And somehow, waiting turns into staying, and staying turns into never leaving."**
- **"The meadows there ripple like living gold, endless and unchanging. In the stillness, you can almost believe the world beyond does not exist."**
- **"I left Everlight’s Bloom, but not many do. There are no chains, no walls—only the quiet pull of belonging, until you forget you ever wished to leave."**
- **"They call him the Hearthkeeper, and it is true: there is always warmth in his halls, a place set for you at his table. But ask yourself—does the hearth keep the flame, or does the flame keep the hearth?"**
- **"Time is strange in Everlight’s Bloom. A single afternoon can stretch into a lifetime, and no one ever marks the passing days. Why would they? Every moment is golden."**
- **"When I left, the flowers clung to my heels, vines whispering soft pleas. I think, in some way, the land itself wanted me to stay."**
- **"The Golden Prince does not forbid anyone from leaving. He only asks—why would you wish to?"**
- **"In that place, you are always safe. Always warm. Always content. But I have learned—there is more to life than the comfort of golden afternoons."**
