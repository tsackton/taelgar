# The Tangled Vale
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Tangled Vale is one of the four fey portals described in [[The Lore of the Feywild]]. It appears as a sheltered pocket of moss and small flowers within a dense brier of tangled thorns, and entering it is said to feel like stepping into a bright painting.

On the [[Material Plane]], the Tangled Vale is located in the [[Tawir Forest]], though its exact place supposedly changes. The crossing connects to [[Wildrun]], a fey realm roughly aligned with the Tawir Forest and the [[Mawakel Peninsula]].
