# The Golden Door
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Golden Door is one of the four fey portals between the [[Material Plane]] and the [[Feywild]] cataloged in [[The Lore of the Feywild]]. It is describes it as a door of shining afternoon sunlight that appears only to those whom fortune favors. 

Stories place the Golden Door somewhere along the ancient road following the north bank of the [[Yeraad]], but it does not appear in exactly the same place twice. Tales from the villages and towns along that road tell of people lucky at cards following a golden shimmer in the dark and vanishing for a night, or a year, before returning from a court of endless games in an eternal spring afternoon.
