# The Twilight Kingdom
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Twilight Kingdom is a [[Feywild]] realm, occupying the land west of Tollen and the Volta, near [[Varrow Forest]]. It is a land of evening, just after the sun has set, of pale blue twilight at the horizon and darkness above. A river wends quietly through rolling grassy hills under the first few unfamiliar stars of twilight. 

A road runs more or less north-south through it, with sunset over the hills to the west and a deeper darkness far off the the east. Though entirely empty of birds, animals and even insects, it is said to contain a "dozen dozen" homes, at least some of which are grand manors.
