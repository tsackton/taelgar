# Twilight's Edge
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Twilight's Edge is a small Feywild realm, ruled by [[Lord Serenveil]], the Prince of the Vesperwind and Guardian of Twilight's Edge. It is one of the lesser twilight realms, coincident with lower [[Enst]] and parts of northern [[Addermarch]], caught between the larger powers of [[Twilight's Grace]], [[Duskmire]], and the ancient domain of [[Morlaith]]. 

Located on the immediate sunward side of the [[Sunset Gate]], it is a land of the setting sun, wild in places, with unsettled borders.
