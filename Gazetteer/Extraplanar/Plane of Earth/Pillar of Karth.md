# The Pillar of Karth
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Pillar of Karth is a landmark of the [[Elemental Plane of Earth]]. It is a towering spire of impossibly hard stone that vibrates slightly with the resonances of a hidden song of the earth.

The Pillar is rumored to stand at the very center of the plane. Some believe it is a remnant of the plane's creation and contains the raw essence of earth.
