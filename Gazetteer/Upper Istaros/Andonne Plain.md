The broad, flat Andonne Plain stretches from the eastern edge of the [[Aurbez Plateau]] across the upper [[Masance]] toward [[Westcliff]] and the eaves of the [[Darkwood]]. It is a land of scrubby grasses and small, hardy trees, where rain is scarce and much of the yearâ€™s water comes from spring snowmelt and sudden floods after thunderstorms. 

Most settlement lies along the upper Masance and the [[Maseau - Dunmar Road]], where marcher lords of the [[March of Andonne]] maintain fortified ranches and waystops, which provide shelter from the ash storms that sweep the region. Few venture close to the [[Andonne]] itself, whose corrupted waters are part of the greater peril of the [[Plaguelands]].
