The southernmost of the two rivers that flow through the the [[Forest of Nightmares]]. 

%% notes
The site of the Battle of Yemo'te, where Midri and K'onisati fell silent, protecting the crossing of the Yemo'te from the endless hobgoblin hordes;
%%

%%SECRET
Battle of Ye'mote site:

A river crossing, the sound of the Yemo'te river the only noise. Mists and shadows hang low over the water, and muffle sounds. Phil's character will probably have a sense something is not right with the water.

A broken bridge crosses the river, and forest is littered with signs of an ancient battlefield: small mounds where the plants have grown over dead bodies, bits of rusting armor and weapons sticking from the ground, the still-perceptible remains of the earthen fortifications constructed to guard the crossing.

White filaments of fungus grow all across the forest on the far side of the river, and many of the bodies are wrapped in cocoons, now dried and shriveled.

As they approach within a few hundred feet, can hear a low wailing moan from near the broken bridge. If anything living thing approaches within 100' feet of the bridge, they see what looks like the remains of a hobgoblin - skeletal, the flesh falling off its face. It seems like it is bound in place by a giant mass of white filaments that wrap around its left arm, that stretch into the earth. Its legs look like they are bound by a massive cocoon, and its head lists to one side. It does not seem like it can move.
%%
