## Raziolo: Coastal Plain

Provincial capital: Roccena (Roc-CHE-na), 
Other notable settlements: Tenze (TEN-tseh), Antissus (an-TIS-sus), Sarvia

The northern tip is flatter, a wider coastal plain. Dry, but supports extensive olive production, and some wine. Olive oil is a major industry. Less seafaring. 

Rivers: Silbana
Villages: Telumis, Serraventia, Canophris
