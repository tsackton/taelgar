# Tzingola
*(tseen-GOH-lah)*

Tzingola is the local lizardfolk name for the extensive salt marshes along the western edge of the [[Mawakel Peninsula]], north of [[Hamri]]. In Hamri, the same marshes are often called the north marshes.

The lizardfolk village of [[Eskorola]] stands near the southern end of Tzingola, where tidal channels, marsh grass, barrier islands, oyster farms, and sheltered pools shape daily life. Most travel through the marsh is by swimming, canoe, or narrow elevated walkways between huts and communal islands.

In DR 1747, a fire cultist used a hidden lair in the marsh north of [[Eskorola]] to hold the dragon turtle [[Chaessenth]] under magical control. [[Ryu]], [[Nerissa]], and [[Ander]] entered the marsh, fought the cultist's servants, and freed Chaessenth.
