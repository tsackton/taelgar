# North Cliffs
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The North Cliffs are the coastal cliffs across [[Hamri]]'s sheltered bay from the port itself. The closest section of the cliffs lies near Hamri and is closely tied to the town's ocean-watching history, but the cliffs are not part of the town proper.

Their sea caves, old seawatch towers, and unstable cliff faces preserve older layers of Mawaran coastal history. The best-known site in the cliffs is the [[Shrine to Guzo the Mariner]], an old sea-cave shrine near the last tower in the line. By 1749, the shrine's old entrance had long since collapsed, but a recent breach in the rock gave the [[Adventurers of Mawar]] access to the buried cave.

## Airion's tower

One ruined seawatch tower in the closest section of the North Cliffs stands above the tidal cave where [[Illius]]'s compass led [[Ryu]], [[Wazir]], and [[Trok]] in DR 1747. The watchtower itself was built atop much older stonework, and the lower ruin preserves fading Primordial glyphs tied to [[Airion]] and water-binding magic.

The tower is directly connected to the sea cave below by stairs. The cave floods with the tide, and a lingering water illusion or planar echo there caused the interior to appear as a vast watery expanse. [[Azar the Lost]] used the tower and cave as a temporary hiding place before the party traced him through Hamri's informants.
