The northeastern‑most border fort of the [[Chardonian Empire]] in the [[Northern Provinces]], commanded by [[Soria Caronius]].

%% DM

 In winter DR 1748 the fort faced severe food shortages due to raids along the river; investigations led north toward the [[Blackwater Fens]] and ultimately the lair of [[Nymthrax]].

## Campaign Notes (GL Arc 4)

- DR 1748‑01‑17: Party arrives at the border fort and scouts river raids.
- DR 1748‑02‑24: Return via the fort after the dragon hunt north of the fens.
