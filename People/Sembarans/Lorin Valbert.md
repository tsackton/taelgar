Lorin Valbert is the lord of [[Asineau]]. He is the second cousin of the old lord, Robert Assante, who was much beloved before his death in 1715.

Lorin did not expect to inherit a manoral lordship, and regards it as beneath his station, being a backwater far from the centers of power.
