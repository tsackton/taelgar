# Alden
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A [[Sembara|Sembaran]] [[Humans|human]] (he/him)  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Alden is a human servant and guide in Lord Holda's employ at [[Dandelion House]]. He is short, round, pale, quiet, and stronger than he looks. Although attentive and usually smiling, he rarely volunteers much conversation. He has a pronounced fear of fire, keeping his distance from campfires and open flame. He has a deep, almost rapturous, affection for Dandelion House and its dandelion imagery.
