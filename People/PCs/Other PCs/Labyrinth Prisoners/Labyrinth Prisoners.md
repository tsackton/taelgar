# The Labyrinth Prisoners
>[!info]+ Information  
> An adventuring party  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

The Labyrinth Prisoners were an ad hoc adventuring party formed by four captives of [[Grimstone|Grimstone's]] prisoner-taking operation at [[Perdoli Manor]] in [[Western Cymea]]. They came from different roads and backgrounds, but all were taken by goblin raiders and woke in a crude prison cell in the manor's abandoned smithy on October 17th, 1748.

 After they killed [[Grimstone]] and escaped [[Grimstone's Lair|his lair]], they went their separate ways.

## Members

- [[Acescale]], a [[Lizardfolk|lizardfolk]] alchemist and gambler from [[Latazaro]]
- [[Gaudin|Gadin]], an [[Elves|elf]] warlock and smuggler with fey patron
- [[Loria Underbough|Loria Underbough]], a [[Halflings|halfling]] wanderer and ranger from the [[Enst]] river valley
- [[Dilion|Dilon]], a [[Humans|human]] artificer and battlesmith who traveled with [[Pikeia]] and the [[Steampup]]
