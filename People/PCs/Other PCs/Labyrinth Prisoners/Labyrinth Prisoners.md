# The Labyrinth Prisoners

The Labyrinth Prisoners were an ad hoc adventuring party formed by four captives of [[Grimstone|Grimstone's]] prisoner-taking operation at [[Perdoli Manor]] in [[Western Cymea]]. The name refers to their shared captivity and escape rather than to a formal organization.

The group consisted of [[Acescale]], [[Gaudin|Gadin]], [[Loria|Loria Underbough]], and [[Dilion|Dilon]]. They came from different roads and backgrounds, but all were taken by goblin raiders and woke in a crude prison cell in the manor's abandoned smithy on October 17th, 1748.

Over the next two days, they escaped the smithy, learned the outlines of Grimstone's arrangement with the goblins, crossed the ruined manor through the chapel crypt and hidden Perdoli archive, found the old pirate route beneath the master bedroom, and defeated Grimstone in his laboratory below the estate. On October 19th, 1748, they escaped the manor in the confusion after Grimstone failed to appear for his usual ritual, divided the recovered treasure on the road, and went their separate ways.

## Members

- [[Acescale]], a [[Lizardfolk|lizardfolk]] alchemist and gambler from [[Latazaro]]
- [[Gaudin|Gadin]], an [[Elves|elf]] warlock and smuggler with an unknown fey patron
- [[Loria|Loria Underbough]], a [[Halflings|halfling]] wanderer and ranger from the [[Enst]] river valley
- [[Dilion|Dilon]], a [[Humans|human]] artificer and battlesmith who traveled with [[Pikeia|Kaiko]] and the [[steampup]]
