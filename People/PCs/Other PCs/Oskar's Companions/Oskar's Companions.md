# Oskar's Companions

Oskar's Companions are the adventurers connected to [[Oskar in Tharn Todor]], the episodic adventures around [[Tharn Todor]] during [[Riswynn]]'s separation from the [[Dunmar Fellowship]].

The recurring companions include [[Oskar]], [[Agnor]], and [[Stoneclaw]], with [[Riswynn]] joining the Tharn Todor adventures.
