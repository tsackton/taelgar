Oskar grew up in [[Nardith]], in [[Tharn Todor]]. His parents were retired travelers and explorerers, who now owned an inn, [[The Red Shield]]. He grew up around weapons, training with scimitars and crossbows from a young age, encouraged by his two older sisters. 

After he came of age, he wanted badly to travel, and left for a time to explore, reaching as far north as [[Voltara]], but eventually returned to Tharn Todor. 

He travels with his hyena companion, [[Stoneclaw]].
