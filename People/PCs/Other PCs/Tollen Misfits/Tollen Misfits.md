# The Tollen Misfits

The Tollen Misfits are the player characters in [[Lost in the Feywild]], a found-family band of adventurers from around [[Tollen]].

The group includes:

- [[Cato Min]], an elven performer and opportunist
- [[Edric]], a generous wanderer devoted to [[The Night Queen]]
- [[Tarek]], a quick-handed traveler and salvager
- [[Ayveen]], a hopeful Tollish adventurer
- [[Txarro]], a druid whose memories include the wild dangers around Tollen
