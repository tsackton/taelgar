%% Ander and Ryu are childhood friends but ages don't match up %%

Ander is a native of Hamri, who left at 16 for Chardon. There, he enlisted in the legions, and learned to fight. Unsettled, he bounced from unit to unit around the frontier for a time, becoming a skilled warrior and rising to the rank of Captain over the course of 15 years. For the past 5 years, he served in the [[Northern Provinces]], primarily in [[Greater Voltara]]. He fought for [[Voltara]] in [[Grumella's War]]; soon after, he decided it was time to come home and left for [[Hamri]]. 

Ander and Ryu are childhood friends, recently reconnected; Ander and Nerissa met a few times, while Nerissa was wandering the [[Northern Provinces]].
