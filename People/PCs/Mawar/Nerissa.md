# Nerissa

An older woman, not from Mawar but from the north, who retired to Hamri after a long adventuring career to listen and learn the songs of the ocean.

%%SECRET
Backstory

. Born somewhere in the North in the early 1680s, at the tail end of the Mindknife War and at the beginning of Chardon's expansion north. 2. Became a master swordfighter and member of the Whistlers, and traveled the north as part of that organization for many years, becoming skilled with a blade and with magic and music. 3. Retired from the adventuring life maybe 5 years ago, to focus on a "second career" as a loremaster and gatherer of songs, inspired by nature. -- First settled in the Tawir Forest, a coastal redwood forest just north of the Mawakel Peninsula and largely unclaimed and unsettled by humans, with only a few small communities of Northerners eking out a living on the lumber trade with Mawar and Chardon. -- After a few years there, moved to the coastal community of Mendin, in the Mawar Confederacy. -- About two months ago, settled in Hamri, claiming an abandoned watcher tower on the north side of the bay.

Allies

Dinia, a bard and member of the Whistlers living an isolated life in the mountains north and west of Voltara, just beyond the northern border of Chardon.

Organizations

The Whistlers: the whistlers are a secretive organization of bards, lore-gatherers, and keepers of history. Based in the northlands and the territory north of Dunmar and west of the mountains, the mission of the Whistlers is to keep alive the memory, culture, and history of those places destroyed by war, especially the Great War against Cha'mutte. They gather tales from those who survived, explore lost ruins, and try to keep the ancient languages, religions, and stories alive. They are a trans-national organization, not associated with any particular kingdom or country. While they are not necessarily in opposite to Chardon and the growing Chardonian Empire, they are against wars of conquest in all their forms.
