# Azzan
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A [[Humans|human]] (he/him)  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_PageDatedValue")`

Azzan was a hero and general remembered in stories of the [[Great War]]. He fought against [[Cha'mutte]] alongside [[Ikos]], a Chardonian friend and ally.

Later tradition in parts of the [[Northern Provinces]] and beyond, recounted to the [[Silver Tempests]] by [[Dinia]], holds that [[Chardon]] abandoned Azzan's people when a great evil swept from the north in the immediate aftermath of the [[Great War]], despite Ikos's pleas for aid.
