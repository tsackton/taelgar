### Odim Mavdyrson

A fire giant, the son of [[Mavdyr]] (who was killed in the [[Fire War]]). Sought [[Dwarves]] for revenge; killed in the process but not before seeing [[Hagrim]] turn on [[Nora Silverspark|Nora]].
