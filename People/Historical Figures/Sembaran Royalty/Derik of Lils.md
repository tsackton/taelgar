## Derik of Lils
*he/him*, b. 1574, d. 1633

The third child of [[Elaine I]] and [[Cynan]], and the founder of the cadet branch of the house, the [[House of Lils]]. His great-granddaughter, [[Elaine II]], eventually became queen of Sembara.
