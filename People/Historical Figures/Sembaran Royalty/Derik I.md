# King Derik I
>[!info]+ Biographical Summary
>King of [[Sembara]]
>[[Humans|human]]  ([[Sembara|Sembaran]]), he/him of [[House of Sewick|The House of Sewick]]
>`$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_PageDatedValue")`
>`$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_RegnalValue")`

The founder of the [[House of Sewick]], he established modern Sembara at the [[Treaty of Valarin]] in the fall of 1430 and reigned over a united Sembara and Tyrwingha until his death in DR 1462.

He was succeeded by his son, [[Derik II]].
