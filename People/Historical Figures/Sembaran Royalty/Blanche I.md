## Blanche I
*she/her*, b. 1541, d. 1567

A ruler of Sembara during the 1560s, she was the second child of [[Reginald]]. Her death launched the [[Interregum of 1568]], as her sisters, [[Anne]] and [[Elaine I]] disputed the succession.
