# Ovina
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A [[Dwarves|dwarf]] (she/her)  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Ovina is a dwarven blacksmith who has lived in Hamri for over 50 years, with a shop and smithy near the Shark's Dock. She is somewhat isolated from Hamri community, and has a bit of a disreputable reputation. However, she makes a decent living in trade with ships at dock, especially those who need to get rid of treasure of dubious origin, which she smelts down and resells to the next ship. 

She knows the [[Adventurers of Mawar]], particularly [[Kaleho]] who she is friendly with.
