# Illius

Illius is a [[Merfolk|merfolk]] scout. In DR 1747, they came to [[Hamri]] as part of an expedition sent to recover a stolen drop of the [[Sentient Ocean]], and were the sole survivor after the expedition was attacked by water elementals. 

Aided by [[Trok]], [[Wazir]], and [[Ryu]], Illius recovered the drop of the [[Sentient Ocean]]; after the recovery, Illius swallowed the drop of the Sentient Ocean as a means of returning home, then swam away from Hamri toward the Endless Ocean.
