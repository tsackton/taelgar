# Aris
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A [[Centaurs|centaur]] (she/her)  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Aris is a [[Centaurs|centaur]] warrior of [[Herasto]]'s centaur band. Young, strong, and brash, she led the centaur warriors who joined the defense of [[Voltara]] during [[Grumella's War]]. During the [[Battle of Voltara]], she fought alongside [[Adrik]] against one of [[Grumella]]'s hill giants. 

She was wounded in the battle, and might have died were it not for the [[Silver Tempests]]. She was left with a lasting limp and a deep affection for the [[Silver Tempests]]. When the rest of her herd left on the next stage of their migration, she remained in the Voltara region.
