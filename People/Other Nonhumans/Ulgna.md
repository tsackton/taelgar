# Ulgna
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A goblin (she/her)  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Ulgna is a goblin bonesplitter associated with the goblin occupation of [[Perdoli Manor]]. She led the goblin faction that disliked serving [[Grimstone]] and resented the hobgoblin authority around the manor, preferring independent raiding to [[Grimstone]]'s prisoner-taking arrangement.

When Grimstone failed to appear for the usual ritual on October 19th, the goblins fled the estate in the resulting confusion, but Ulgna's later fate is not recorded. 

%% note -  [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] did not meet Ulgna directly. They first heard her name from drunk goblin guards, who complained that she did not like working for Grimstone, and later from [[Folcan]], who blamed her as cowardly and unreliable. %%
