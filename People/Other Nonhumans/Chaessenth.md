# Chaessenth
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A dragon turtle (he/him)  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Chaessenth is a young dragon turtle, who was captured very young (probably taken as an egg). He was held in a hidden lair in [[Tzingola]], where a fire mage controlled him through a burning black spear and used him as the apparent monster behind attacks and [[Gatza|gatza]] demands near [[Eskorola]].

After his release, Chaessenth left for the deep western ocean. He knows songs of the [[Sentient Ocean|Wanui Teora]], evoking the stars below and the dazzling lights of the deep sea. Chaessenth formed a particular bond with [[Nerissa]], who calmed and healed him after the spear was removed and began to pick up the melody of his ocean song.
