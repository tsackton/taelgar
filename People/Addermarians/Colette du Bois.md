Colette Du Bois is a squire of in [[Elowen Duval]]'s household guard, apprenticed to Sir [[Gareth Howell]]. She is often restless, and eager to prove herself, which can get her in trouble at times, as when she scouted a goblin lair in the [[Highmoor]] ahead of official orders and was captured. 

Though her captivity has slightly tempered her recklessness, she remains impatient and hungry for opportunities for real adventure. She is a reliable scout and source of local knowledge. 

## Events
- (DR:: 1715-04-29): Captured by goblin bandits while scouting in the [[Highmoor]]
- (DR:: 1715-05-08): Rescued from the goblin queen’s cells by the [[Addermarch Mercenaries]] during their assault on the [[Highmoor]] lair.
