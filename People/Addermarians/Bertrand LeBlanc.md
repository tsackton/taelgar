Bertrand LeBlanc is a charming middle‑aged innkeeper who runs [[The Traveler's Rest]] in [[Roscombe]]. Friendly toward the [[Addermarch Mercenaries]] and most travelers without need for persuasion, he prides himself on fair dealing, a well‑kept cellar, and a welcoming common room.

Born and raised in Roscombe, Bertrand has a personal connection to the Wanderer and enjoys talking with travelers from near and far.
