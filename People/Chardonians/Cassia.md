Cassia is a paladin and adventurer based in [[Chardon]]. In March DR 1748, she explored the lower [[Goldpeak Mines]] with [[Alton]] and [[Brottor]], where the beholder [[Vilaxes]] had taken control of the depths beneath [[Goldpeak Mountain]].

After Brottor fell under Vilaxes's influence, Cassia and Alton joined the [[Silver Tempests]] in the fight against the beholder. Cassia survived the expedition after [[Brelith]] raised her from death at the end of the battle.
