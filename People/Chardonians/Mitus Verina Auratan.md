Role playing:
- A little brash and a little arrogant: rising to a position of such power at a young age will do that
- genuinely believes in the necessity of the Chardonian Empire. not in this for money or power, exactly (maybe a little fame). grew up rich though and maybe doesn't quite see how the poor suffer.
- cannot be worse than being slaves to the Cleansed or crushed under the heel of an evil lich is the basic attitude; surely we must stick together or we will die separately. 
- Drankor had its flaws but at its height it lifted people up to live their best lives. "I may not be perfect, but I am at least trying"
- will not blame the party for being played by Fausto - we all got played, even the University
- will defend actions in Dunmar; acted on the best knowledge we had at the time. "You could help me! You are obviously strong yourself, if we work together we could have stopped this in its tracks."
- His argument is precisely because of all that has happened that Chardon is needed. 

Mitus:
- doesn't trust anyone, goal is to wrest back control; not happy with university who he feels got played by Fausto, not happy with riots, knows something of the seedy underbelly of chalyte refining but too valuable to completely give up, and worried about losing the north
- currently relies on [[Windcallers]] as his primarily trusted advisors; navy as well but they are occupied in darba
- still trying to understand chaos but will have spoken to [[Marius Cusico]] (who is currently holed up in a monastery trying to make sense of the last 12 years of his life), and knows about the control
- windwalkers do use chalyte but have tricks to use it (relatively) safely, so mitus has a chalyte connection
