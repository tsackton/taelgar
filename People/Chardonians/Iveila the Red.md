![[iveila-the-red.png|right|400]]Iveila the Red, usually just called "Red" by friends, acquaintances, and enemies alike, is a muscular woman with frizzy flame-red hair. She is a crane operator in the [[Ragwater Basin]], working to unload raw chalyte ore for processing. She is also an informal labor organizer and became prominent as a member of the [[Eightfold Flame]] during the [[Chardon Chalyte Riots]] in DR 1749.
