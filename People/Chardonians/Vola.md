## Overview

A Chardonian adventurer, treasure hunter, and sellsword.

Vola was born on the streets of [[Chardon]], living a life of squalor, disease, and poverty. Grew up in the Riverside slurms, near the chalyte refineries. %%SECRET During this time, she found herself manifesting strange powers: telepathy, the ability to move things with her mind. ### Roleplaying notes:
- **She is not very forthcoming, and she is confident and commanding. Definitely not a nervous prisoner or a damsel in distress type.**
- **Swears some: "bloody hells" "nine hells" "blood and ash" "damn"**
- **For vocal characterization: Try to do a bit of a breathy voice, and go with brusque, short declarative sentences. Doesn't run her mouth, not one for lots of I statements or storytelling. Try really hard to avoid "ums/ahs" etc. Tends to use swears (especially bloody hells or nine hells) as interjections/vocal tics. Sort of the opposite of Alesh, and much plainer spoken and less long-winded than Candrosa. Try to use simple vocab.**

### Motivation

Her dream is simply to belong somewhere that values her, and to never again experience the crushing poverty of her childhood. The Society has been good to her and that fulfills these desires for now.

Now, as she has been Cleansed, her only motivation is to ensure the rise of New Hkar and the purity of the human race. 

### Beliefs

On the Society of the Open Scroll:

"After the fall of Drankor many, many years ago, much was lost and humanity was poorer for it. Only a stroke of luck preserved a portion of the accumulated magical knowledge of the empire in the Great Library at Chardon. Again, during the Great War, much was lost. Up north, many kingdoms were wiped from the face of the world and much knowledge lost with them. Here in Dunmar, the same thing happened. The Society of the Open Scroll was founded to preserve *all* knowledge against the next disaster, so we can rise stronger and more quickly."

On why they are out in Dunmar now:

"We have heritage in this land too, of course. Chardon is the successor to the Drankorian Empire. After the new Magistros crushed the Illorian pirates and made the sea routes to Dunmar safe again, the Great Library was given permission by King Karnas Nayan in Nayahar to travel through the land to recover what knowledge we could."

## Relationships

- Friendly with [[Dunmar Fellowship]], especially with [[Kenzo]], who had a similar childhood in Chardon. Somewhat awed by [[Delwath]]. Less friendy with [[Riswynn]], largely because or [[Riswynn]]'s distain. 
- Friendly with other adventurers for the Society of the Open Scroll, in particular [[Dee Wildcloak]], [[Dain Goldhammer]], and [[Alban]]
- Looks up to her mentor, [[Agon]]
- Knows many other Chardonians, including [[Kadmos]], [[Marcella]], [[Hektor]], [[Antonia]], and [[Casian]], whom she traveled with in early 1748, and [[Crispus]] and [[People/Chardonians/Fausto]], who are the funders of expeditions she has been on or knows of. Particularly friendly with [[Marcella]]. Dislikes [[Casian]], whom she considers an arrogant snob who believes he can take whatever he wants.
