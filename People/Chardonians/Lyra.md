Background:  
Although she passes for a Chardonian, Lyra actually grew up among the Dunmari. She comes from a merchant family based in \<Southport\>;  and she was born on a trading ship plying the Dunmari coast. She and her father stayed in a Dunamri settlement for many years after the death during childbirth of her mother; her dad remarried a local. Enamored with the past and especially the heroic and tragic history of the Dunmari people, she left home as a young woman to seek her cousins’ in Chardon and learn what she could of her adopted people’s history. She dreams of a day when the glory and deeds of the Dunmari will be celebrated and the people will reemerge in triumph. To this end, she has taken a position in the Great Library working to restore the lost knowledge of history.  
    
Appearance:  
Lyra is a tall, lanky woman with long blonde hair and a stern face and a commanding gaze.
 
Personality:  
Lyra is a stern, authoritative person, direct and to the point. She does not tolerate nonsense and expects the people she works with to be prepared and professional. She does, however, have a soft spot for the Dunmari and will warm up when the subject comes up, especially the great battle outside Drankor during the Plague Wars and the great battle against Cha’mutte. 
 
## Motives/etc
 
She will be taken with Kenzo and if he opens up about his story that will be an easy way to win her over.
 
Her real passion is history and the tales of the Great War - will also be taken with Delwath if she learns the truth.
 
Her work on the elemental scrolls is kind of a side project, not what she really wants to be doing but what Crispus assigned her to (she does not really get along with him). She knows Fausto has the eyes through the connection to elemental magic.
 
She also knows Galen's secret and will reveal it if swayed over.
 
She can get the party a meeting with Crispus if needed. She does not know about his Cleansed involvement - she does not know about the Cleansed at all.
