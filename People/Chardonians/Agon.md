## Appearance
Sharp features, olive brown skin with curly brown hair. Wears elegant and neatly tailored clothes, but without much adornment. Thin scar on his left cheek.

## Mannerisms
He is suspicious of outsiders and obviously and straightforwardly racist, but this comes across as a Drankorian boosterism more than anything else. Although clear this social circles do not include any non-Chardonians.

However, he is not stupid and he knows that many people don't think this way (in his mind, not yet, because they haven't seen the benefits of Hkaran rule). He knows that to grow the power of the Cleansed he needs to indoctrinate people and thus his focus on e.g. Vola and others. Has a common tactic of rescuing Chardonians from poverty, pointing out how little the empire does for them, and slowly encouraging them to think they are being denied their birthright.

Especially happy with Vola given the unusual powers she has displayed and her increasing turn towards associating these with her pure Dranokrian / Hkaran blood. He is currently teaching her Drankorian.

**Trait (Value): The descendants of Hkar should take their rightful place as the rulers of humanity and anyone who gets in their way must be stopped, with force if necessary**

## Motives/Challenges

Agon is a true believer in the mission of the Cleansed. He will not hesitate to attempt to set the party up if he catches a whiff of their true names, etc. Since Fausto would be the bait, happy to try to lure them in for an ambush at Fausto's tower. Also a dedicated member of [[The Cleansed]]
