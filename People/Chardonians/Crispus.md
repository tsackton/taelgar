A senior Archivist at the [[Great Library]], in charge of external mercenary contracts, with general responsibility for organizing missions to recover lost treasures for the Great Library. 

%%SECRET 
Purpose: information

Trait (Value): The pinnacle of human achievement was Drankor and we are all just trying to find our way back by learning what we can about what they created - manifests as arrogance directed at non-Drankorians (with exceptions for elves and maybe dwarves); obsession with material culture from the past, especially magic; desire to tinker/learn/acquire.

Not a spellcaster, but a deeply experienced student of magic items. Might have a few artificer levels but no spells.

Mentioned to the party as the person to ask for in Chardon if they want more information about artifacts collected, etc. A funder of Society of the Open Scroll expeditions but not a member himself (not particularly interested in the scholarship, more of an engineer / bureaucrat).

Recently recruited to the Cleansed but not a full member so does not know much. But will report party to Agon/Fausto if they are nosy.

## Appearance

Indeterminate age, could be anywhere from 30-55. Plain face, clean shaved, short cropped brown hair, almost a buzz cut, piercing green eyes.

## Mannerisms

Stares for a long time before saying anything.

## Motives/Challenges

The real challenge here is getting a meeting, will need something to tempt him. Mostly very interested in magical items, their construction, history, use, and abuse. He is kind of a tinkerer/engineer type, doesn't care for the theory at all, just wants to see things work. Particularly fascinated with constructs - working on a homunculus servant and various other artificer-like things (but is low-level, maybe 1st working towards 2nd).

He is indifferent and as long as the party doesn't offend him, will offer up information with a relatively easy persuasion roll (DC 10) if they can get a meeting.

He knows a lot - he has funded a number of expeditions and is basically the "grants officer" for the Great Library. Acquittance with Lyra, on decent terms with the adventurers (Dee and Dain).
