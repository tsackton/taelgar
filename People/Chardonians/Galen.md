A showman, scholar, academic rival of sorts to [[Ulfgar Frostbeard]], and member of the Faculty of Magic at the [[University of Chardon]]. Known for elaborate lectures on the magic of the planes, with a particular focus on elemental magic. 

%%^Campaign:DuFr%%
Had possessed the [[Chalice of the Runepriest]], but sold it to [[Riswynn]] for a large sum of money in [[Session 49 (DuFr)]]. 
%%^End%%


%%SECRET

An independently wealthy scholar and relatively famous scholar, member of the Faculty of Metaphysics, student of the planes, and member of the Hetaeri Magica. Has a secret history as a warlock that he tries to hide, passing off his magic as product of study. Older man. Now in possession of the Chalice of the Runepriest, having pulled strings to acquire it for his research after hearing rumors it gave visions of a strange, otherworldly place. Not associated with the Cleansed in any way (perhaps not even Chardonian originally).
 
## Appearance
 
Fat man, looks like he is maybe 65 but actually just in his 50s. Balding, with thinning gray hair. Moves slowly and ostentatiously shows off his wealth. Wears many rings with different jewels, and wears an ornate bejeweled metal bottle on a chain around his neck. Dressed in silks.
 
## Mannerisms
 
Likes to show off and talk about his work. He is the stereotype of the great academic who knows his way is correct. Often telling stories of his brilliant insights.
 
## Motives/Challenges
 
He will be more than willing to trade for the right item.
 
He will be put off by being challenged or threatened - intimidation or accusing him of theft will not end will.
 
He can easily be flattered by asking questions about his research and studies and just showing an interest in him as he talks. He likes to talk a lot, about his work but also about his life and riches and experiences.
 
He has two secrets he does not like to share, and learning one of these can put him on the defensive and could be used as blackmail (although at the cost of making him an enemy).
 
1. He is not a wizard - he gets his powers from his pact with a djinni. He never had the mind to study magic and took a shortcut. [Lyra is the only one who knows this]
2. He is not originally Chardonian. His parents were poor refugees fleeing from one of the destroyed northern kingdoms, and essentially gave him up for adoption to get him citizenship and a place as a student at the university. He has very mixed and unexamined feelings about this.
    1. He was adopted by a childless rich Chardonian merchant who saw potential in him, and this is the source of his wealth (think like in the Sorcerer to the Crown book basically).
