![[brimskarda.png|right|400]]Brimskarda is a frost giant king, the ruler of the Kingdom of [[Kaldhalla]], centered in the northern half of the [[Svinjo Mountains]]. Though violent and prone to see the world in terms of strength of arms alone, he is also protective of his kingdom and those who have exchanged their service for his protection (not always willingly), and there have been no large-scale dragon attacks on [[Kaldhalla]] since he established himself as king. 

%%SECRET
## Personality
Brimskarda is boastful and proud. He will respond well to flattery and anyone talking up his status or appreciating his stories. Will not respond well to pissing matches about who is more powerful, and will not acknowledge the party as equals as that would involve major loss of face (in his eyes). But can depend partly on roll - DC very high for boasting (25+), much lower for flattery (15 or so).

If pushed / threatened, will likely challenge someone to single combat. Depending on how it goes might imprison person, requiring prison break, or otherwise try to bind them to service. Doesn't really care for gold but might eye the skyship thoughtfully.
## History
In DR 1673, tired of what he perceives as the weakneess of Freyvidra, Brimskarda launches a coup, and defeats Freyvidra in single combat, claiming the kingship of Kaldhalla. From DR 1673-1692, Brimskarda fights the dragon wars, and defeats three of Vimfrost's children, reclaiming their treasure for his hall.

Over the course of these 20 years, Brimskarda slowly conquers a number of dwarven and stoneborn settlements of the [[Svinjo Mountains]]. He turns his back on Freyvidra's meddling in the lowlands, and brings back what he considers a traditional frost giant ethos, where prowess in arms is how the worth of a person is judged, and those who do not fight are only good to be servants, or worse. Nonetheless, since DR 1692, when the last dragon was repealed, no dragon has dared attack the [[Svinjo Mountains]]. 

For the past 60 years, Brimskarda has ruled, fat and happy, the palace of [[Isenborg]].
