Hugo Dupont is scholar and theologian, and a lecturer at the university, known for his classes on comparative divinity and theological science, particularly a series of lectures and scholastic discourse on the nature of intercessionary prayer, and the intertwined divinities of the [[Mos Numena|Eight Divines]]. 

He is well-known in the Isinguese community, and also well connected across the non-human community, including with [[Caelynn]].
