%%SECRET
A very old man, maybe 75-80 in appearance, but surprisingly fit and spry. White hair, wears white robes lined with white fur, and a heavy silver amulet on a thick chain around his neck. 

He is a member of the Philosopher's Guild, a strange and secretive organization, supposedly founded in the first years of [[Tollen]]'s history. 

Will probably seek out the party. Interested - very interested - in magic, ancient magic especially. Will ask a bunch of questions with an air of mystery ("you have brought a great deal of magic into this city - truly more than I have seen for many years").

An area of quiet follows him: when you are nearby, the crowd noise fades. And it is very hard to make out his conversation with others, even to Wellby. 

Won't really answer questions, but if he feels satisfied about the answers the party gives, and if feels he has learned something, will offer them a favor in return. Potentially will be interested in trading magic items, especially. See how it plays out.
