![[nika-hyne.png|right|320]]Nika Hyne is a sly young woman in her early twenties known to frequent [[The Windward Sail]], listening to stories and doing odd jobs for coin. She was briefly a student at the [[University of Tollen]]: although she dropped out, she knows the area and the people well. She is a bit of a hustler. 

%%SECRET
Sly, vaguely con artist is attitude. Always hustling.

NIka Hyne (Nika the Tall in Vos) is from very Tollish family. Her father is originally from Vosland, a Vos who ended up taking work on a Tollender naval ship. Her mother is from an old Tollender family. She grew up in Fiskurth with her mother, her father rarely home, but his stories of far-off lands became an obsession. In her late teens she had saved up enough money to pay for classes at the University, where she took courses in language and rhetoric, but she never completed her degree. Now she makes money as an occasional assistant to Thomas Hawke, a scholar at the University, helping him with small jobs, scribing, copying and translating texts, and the like. 

Nika is indifferent to the party, but polite and curious. Can be temporality shifted to friendly if they provide some good tales and stories for her notes. 

If the party gives her a good story she thinks Thomas will like, will agreed to introduce them but will make sure she can meet with him first and get paid for their story. 

If they don't give her a good story he will like, she will ask for coin, with amount depending on persuasion check. She can tell they are rich and powerful so minimum will be 10 gp, and up to 100 gp if they don't roll well.
