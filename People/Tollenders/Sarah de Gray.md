![[sarah-de-gray.png|right|320]]Sarah de Grey, a 16-year-old from the distinguished Isinguer family, is a student at the [[University of Tollen]]. Originally from the [[Duchy of Maseau]] in the south, she has come to [[Tollen]] to study, and has developed connections to the Insinguer community in the city, especially [[Guy Marchand]].  

%%SECRET
Sarah might come across as naive, with her inexperience evident from her interactions; she's likely never met an elf and dwarves are equally unfamiliar. Born as the youngest of five in a marcher lord's family in the Duchy of Maseau, Sarah's latent sorcery abilities surfaced during her adolescence. To learn to harness her newfound power, she was sent to [[Tollen]], possibly attaining a level or two in sorcery. Despite the rich heritage she claims, her family's wealth doesn't quite compare to [[Tollen]]'s merchant class. This leaves Sarah frequently in financial straits, trying to maintain an illusion of affluence. With roots tracing back to Isingue, she occasionally receives covert support from Guy Marchand. Desperate for funds, Sarah could be a bridge for introductions to Thomas Hawke or notably, Hugo Dupont.

Her father was definitely in the assimilationist camp and she doesn't see the point of rebuilding. She is very happy in [[Tollen]], if struggling with being poorer than she expected. 

She tries to keep up the appearance of coming from a rich and prosperous lord's castle, despite the fact that many middle class traders and craftspeople in [[Tollen]] have much higher standards of living than her family. Somewhat out of her depth, she is constantly in need of money.

Her family originally hails from Isingue, and she knows and is occasionally supported under the table by Guy Marchand. 

She wants money, but can introduce party to Thomas Hawke or especially Hugo Dupont.
