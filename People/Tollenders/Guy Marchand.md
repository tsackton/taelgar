Guy Marchand is a mapmaker from [[Tollen]], known for his meticulous craftsmanship and his thriving shop in [[Nordgate]].
## Overview

Guy Marchand, a mapmaker and bookseller, resides in the Nordgate district of [[Tollen]], where he runs a shop on the bustling [[Scrollwright Street]]. This major thoroughfare connects [[Magnus Street]] and [[Godshome]], and is the heart of book production and related trades in [[Tollen]]. He is of Isinguese ancestry, and his shop doubles as a local center for the Isinguer diaspora in [[Tollen]]. 
## Description

![[guy-marchand.png]]

Guy, an aging man in his mid-60s, looks young for his age; he has lived a comfortable life. His attire is modest but well-crafted, reflecting his middle-class merchant roots. He lives and works in a spacious, if cluttered, mapmaking shop on [[Scrollwright Street]].
## Relationships

- **The Isinguese Diaspora:** Guy has established himself at the center of a loose, informal network of Isinguese in [[Tollen]]. While some dream of rebuilding [[Isingue]], Guy and his circle are content preserving the memory and traditions of their fallen homeland.
- **Wanderers and Explorers:** While Guy himself doesn't travel, his insatiable curiosity about distant places has made him a friend of many wanderers. He exchanges tales, rumors, and knowledge about far-off lands with them, enriching his mapmaking skills, and making his shop something of a center for rumors and information. 
- **[[Wellby]]:** Guy remembers [[Wellby]] fondly. 
- **Sarah de Grey:** Sarah frequently dines at Guy's shop, with a few other local Isinguese.

## Roleplaying Notes

- **Meticulous and Observant:** Guy is known for his careful and methodical nature. This extends beyond just his professional work, as he's keenly observant of those around him.
- **Warm Reception for [[Wellby]]:** If roleplayed in a scenario involving [[Wellby]], Guy should exhibit genuine happiness and curiosity about [[Wellby]]'s travels.
- **Benevolent and Curious:** Guy displays a genuine interest in people's stories, especially if they involve distant places. His benevolence shines when dealing with fellow Isinguese, particularly those in need.
- **Guardian of the Past:** Guy exudes a gentle pride in his heritage and a sense of duty to preserve the Isinguese traditions. He can recount tales of [[Isingue]]'s glorious past and tragic fall, providing insights into the mindset of those who fled the city's destruction.
- **Generosity:** Without an heir to pass on his legacy or his earnings, Guy often supports local Isinguese projects or offers assistance to those in dire straits.
## Secrets

- Over the decades, Guy's shop has amassed a trove of Isinguese artifacts, literature, and historical documents, making it a mini-museum of sorts. Some of these are rare and perhaps controversial, which he keeps in a hidden compartment in his shop.
- He has made quiet arrangements for his vast collection of Isinguese artifacts, literature, and maps to be preserved and displayed for future generations after his time, ensuring that the memories of [[Isingue]] live on.
