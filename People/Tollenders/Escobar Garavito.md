## Roleplaying Notes

- Suave, fully aware of his good looks and not hesitant to flaunt it.
- Exhibits youthful exuberance, being talkative and gregarious. However, his interactions feel genuine rather than shallow.
- Recognizes many in [[Tollen]], often greeting them on the streets with waves or friendly hellos.
## Background

Originally hailing from the quiet village of [[Lavila]] in [[Cymea]], Escobar felt stifled and dreamt of bigger horizons. Leveraging his wit and charm, he talked his way aboard a Tollender trader ship. However, the sea's allure faded after six months, leading him to blend into the city's throng upon docking at [[Tollen]].

Life as a laborer awaited him, but destiny had other plans. A chance encounter with [[Cecilia Lister]], a renowned tattoo artist skilled in magical inks, turned his fortunes. With her support and the [[Dyer's Guild]]'s backing, he secured a scholarship at the prestigious University of [[Tollen]]. The understanding being that after his studies, he'd become Cecilia's apprentice.

For those bearing a letter of introduction from Cecilia, Escobar can be of immense help, even offering introductions to individuals like [[Caelynn]]. Otherwise, he remains his cheerful self, offering advice for a pint of ale but not much else.
