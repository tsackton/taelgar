# Jacopo Marcelli

Jacopo Marcelli was a pirate captain, active in [[Western Cymea]] in the early DR 1700s. In life, Jacopo loved [[Petrona]], who urged him to return safely and abandon the pirate life. He died in a naval raid on a pirate hideout under the [[Perdoli Manor]], without learning whether she had escaped to safety, and his ghost remained bound to the bedroom. 

When [[Grimstone]] occupied [[Perdoli Manor]], Jacopo's ghost served as a useful guard, kept under Grimstone's control by false promises. On October 18th, 1748, [[Gaudin]] and the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]] used magic to take Petrona's appearance, convincing Jacopo to that Grimstone had lied to him. The sight of his lost love softened Jacopo: he refused to keep guarding the way below, and faded away.
