# Orden
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A Mawaran [[Humans|human]] (he/him)  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

![[orden.png|right|300]]
Orden is a beach-dwelling Mawaran who lives in a shack north of the main port of [[Hamri]]. He was born into a prominent local family, but now lives as an outcast, making do with salvage, fishing, scavenging, and whatever work the shore provides. He is short and stocky, with great bushy hair, a bushy beard, many piercings, and proud eyes.

Orden is a friend of [[Trok]]. They sometimes work salvage together, and sometimes smoke [[Gatza|gatza]] on the beach late at night, each seeking their own solace. 

He is also [[Hiyasa]]'s father. When [[Hiyasa]] returned to [[Hamri]] after roughly ten years away and then vanished while treasure hunting up the Mirmir, Orden went to [[Trok]] for help. His request sent [[Trok]], [[Nerissa]], [[Kaleho]], and [[Ryu]] after her, leading them to the [[Tomb of Yerkir-Khor|tomb of Yerkir-khor]] and[[Mawar Adventures Episode 03|her eventual rescue]].

He was a rebellious youth, shipped out as a teen with some "merchants" who turned out ot be mercenaries or maybe just pirates, narrowly escaped, joined a criminal gang to survive for a while in a nearby coastal city, and later returned to Hamri, hoping to be welcomed by his family, but they rejected him. He subsists on what the ocean offers, or what he can beg or maybe steal, and fraternizes with the local lowlifes.
