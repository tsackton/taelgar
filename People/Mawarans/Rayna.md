# Rayna
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A [[Mawar Confederacy|Mawaran]] [[Humans|human]] (she/her)

>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Rayna is a Mawaran sailor descended from [[Society of Ocean Watchers|Ocean Watchers]]. She was born at sea in 1713 and raised mainly by her maternal grandfather, [[Caleb]], who taught her stories of [[Mawar Confederacy|Mawar]]'s golden age and the old ocean-watching traditions.

Since her youth, Rayna has heard the ocean in songs and visions. Those songs fell silent for years after a nightmare of tearing and loss, then returned in 1749 with a plea to recover a trapped fragment of song from an old shrine of [[Guzo the Mariner]] near [[Hamri]].

In May 1749, Rayna came to the [[Leviathan Inn]] and asked the [[Adventurers of Mawar]] to find the lost song. After the party recovered the fragment from the [[Shrine to Guzo the Mariner]], she took it westward, sailing toward [[Apporia]] and the merfolk city of [[Omi]].
