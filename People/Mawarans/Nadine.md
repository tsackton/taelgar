# Nadine

Nadine was the wife of [[Azar the Lost]]. She and their two teenage sons drowned while traveling to market from a small village on the eastern coast of the [[Mawakel Peninsula]].

Her death drove Azar into grief, [[Gatza|gatza]] addiction, and eventually the influence of [[Gazankoa]]. Azar believed that the voice he heard through gatza dreams was Nadine, and his attempts to restore her led to the murders in [[Hamri]].
