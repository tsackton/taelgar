# Nadine
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A [[Mawar Confederacy|Mawaran]] [[Humans|human]] (she/her)  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_PageDatedValue")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Nadine was the wife of [[Azar the Lost]]. She and their two teenage sons drowned in a summer storm in DR 1741, while traveling to market from a small village on the eastern coast of the [[Mawakel Peninsula]].

Her death drove Azar into grief, [[Gatza|gatza]] addiction, and eventually the influence of [[Gazankoa]]. Azar believed that the voice he heard through gatza dreams was Nadine, and his [[Azar's Tale|attempts to restore her]] led to [[Mawar Adventures Episode 02|murders]] in [[Hamri]].
