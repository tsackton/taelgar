# Guzo the Mariner
>[!info]+ Biographical Info
> A Mawaran [[Humans|human]] (he/him)
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Guzo the Mariner is a Mawaran hero and saint associated especially with [[Hamri]], ocean voyages, and protection for those lost at sea. In Hamri's local religious practice, prayers to the [[Mos Numena|Eight Divines]] may pass through Guzo and other heroic ancestors as intercessors.

Guzo appears in traditions around the [[Sentient Ocean]] under the name Ma'haya Kabir, "the ancestor of voyages and the western horizon." Rayna's ocean visions showed him standing on the prow of a ship and later pointed her toward his old sea-cave shrine in the [[North Cliffs]].

His abandoned shrine north of Hamri preserved a fragment connected to the ocean's song until the [[Adventurers of Mawar]] recovered it in 1749 and returned it to [[Rayna]].
