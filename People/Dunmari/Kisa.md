Kisa is a short elderly woman with graying hair and a limp, known affectionately as Elder Kisa. She is one of the respected elders of Karawa and serves as an unofficial leader of the village. She is a slow and deliberate speaker, always thinking first of the safety of the village and its people. 

%%SECRET 
**One-word key: matronly leader
Demeanor: protective of Karawa, her first thought is always the safety of the people of the village
Accent/mannerisms: Speaks deliberately and directly; waits to gather her thoughts and then makes them clear with plain language**

Argued for a strong defense of Karawa in early council before Festival of Rebirth, but grateful the people did not stay after seeing the damage done to the village by the gnoll attacks. Eternally grateful to The Dunmar Fellowship for their assistance.
