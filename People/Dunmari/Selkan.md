Speaks Dunmari and Common.  
Prematurely aged Dunmari man, maybe in his late 30s or early 40s but looks much older. Bald, with weary eyes full of pain. His tongue has been cut out, and he is missing both his thumbs.
 
**Attitude:** Selkan was scared by the risks the Order of the Awakened Soul was taking, and he came to Agata for information about other planes, about what might be lurking at the center of the Plaguelands. \<MAYBE???\> - Agata wanted information out of him about the Order, their movements, their capabilities, their past and future, and especially about Rai. He realized his mistake, and decided he could not reveal the secrets of the Order to her. So she threw him in the mirror to concoct a plan. Realizing that she could charm him and force him to speak, he cut out his own tongue, and cut off his own thumbs so he could not write.
 
**Goals:** Return to the Monastery of Bhishma and help tend the Hall of Stories where he could be reminded of the souls of the Dunmari.
 
Spoke briefly with Wellby in Session 32; has taken vow of silence but allowed telepathy to break it.
