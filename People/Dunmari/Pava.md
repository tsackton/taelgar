History

Born in DR 1670s at the western edge of Dunmar, near [[Songara]].  apprentice through DR 1690s, wanderer through DR 1710s, and masters here since after the events of DR 1717 and the downfall of Rai / Awakened Soul Supremes (say since DR 1720, so 28 years).

Loner as a kid, drawn to stories. Back at that time the Mystai of Bhishma traveled around gathering tales and histories of the people, and drawn to this life. Met Pava at the Feast of Bhishma the year they were both raised out of their apprenticeship, and have been traveling together since.

After the breaking of the Order in 1717, took up residence here, watch the comings and goings on the plains, rescue the lost and the desperate who come seeking treasure, glory, or death in the desert. Quiet life but time for contemplation and still many stories of lives lived on these lands that are forgotten.

PC Interactions

Found Delwath wandering in the desert.

Met PCs again at the Stone House. Impressed with them, and came around on Delwath. Allies of the party and especially Kenzo. Pava trained Kenzo on monk abilities.
