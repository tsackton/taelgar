%%SECRET

Vibe is very much the fear of calling for help and hearing no answer, of panicked screams in the night from which no help comes, and then slowly the terror that closes your throat and keeps you from screaming at all. 

Darkfall is ruled by Harrow, the Shadow That Looms
Hollowdark is ruled by [[Wend|Wend]], the Silence That Follows

"If you see Harrow, do not run—he does not follow. It is [[Wend|Wend]] you must fear."
"You can flee the shadow, but only if the being stands still. Pray that Harrow lingers, for [[Wend|Wend]] is always moving."
"They do not hunt. They do not chase. They only wait. And eventually, all things pass where they stand."

## ChatGPT bBrainstorming Notes

### **The Relationship: The Being & The Shadow**

- One **exists fully in form**, a figure that **obscures light**, blocking it entirely, casting a **deep, cold void** into the space around them. Their presence is like **standing before something massive and wrong**, something that **should not be blocking the sky, but is.**
- The other is **merely a shadow**—not cast by light, but **clinging to the being**, a **wispy afterimage that moves when it should not**. They **absorb all sound**, so that **no step echoes, no cry carries, and even one’s own heartbeat feels muffled.**
- At unknown intervals—**without warning**—they **switch places.**
    - The **shadow lengthens, thickens, and becomes real**—while the being **thins, wavers, and fades into a silent wisp.**
    - The transition is **imperceptible**—it is not sudden, nor is it slow. One moment, one is present. **The next, the other is.**
- Those who have encountered them in **separate meetings** often believe they are **two distinct beings**.
- But those who witness the shift **never describe it.** Not because they refuse—but because **the Hollowdark stole their voice before they could.**

---

### The Aesthetic: The Terror of Vanishing Sound & Light

- Their presence evokes **the nightmare of calling for help and knowing no one hears you**.
- Whoever is the **shadow absorbs all sound**:
    - **No footsteps. No voice. No breath.**
    - If you scream, you feel your throat strain—but **no sound reaches your ears.**
    - **Your own thoughts feel quieter.**
- Whoever is the **being swallows all light**:
    - **Their form is an absence**, **not black, not dark—just missing.**
    - Light does not illuminate them, nor does it bend around them—it **ceases to exist in their presence.**
    - Those who look directly at them often **lose sense of depth**—**is the being near, or impossibly far?**
- Their realm reflects them:
    - **No wind, yet movement.**
    - **No echoes, yet distant rustling.**
    - **No moon, but the feeling of something high above watching.**
    - Paths that are **visible when walked upon, but disappear behind you.**
- They do not **hunt**—they do not need to.
    - They simply **exist, and let the fearful come to them.**
    - Those who do not fear them may pass unscathed.
    - Those who do…
        - **Do they die?**
        - **Or are they merely somewhere else, walking in silence, unheard, unseen, forever?**
