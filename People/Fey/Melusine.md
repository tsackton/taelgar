A water nymph in [[Amberglow]]. She resides in a small, secluded grotto near a river, overgrown with vines and foliage. 

%%SECRET

She is a nymph, originally a water spirit in [[Amberglow]] from long ago, when the [[Cloudspinner]] ruled and the realm was full of color. After [[Cloudspinner]] left, the evil hag [[Agata]] Dustmother corrupted Melusine, forced her into a bargain of servitude, making her a servant of [[Agata]] against her will. Now, she is shy and reclusive, but reluctantly fulfills her obligations to help Agata.

As Melusine, I have mixed feelings about the [[Cloudspinner]]. On the one hand, I remember the time when she ruled [[Amberglow]], and the realm was a place of beauty and wonder. I remember her spinning magical thread from the colors of the sky, and I was filled with awe and admiration for her. On the other hand, I also remember the day when she vanished, and the realm fell into decay. I have lived through the hardships and dangers of life in [[Amberglow]], and I have seen the cruelty and malice of the unseelie fey who now rule here. I sometimes wonder if the [[Cloudspinner]] abandoned us, or if she is unable to return. I also fear that she may be angry with us for what has happened to her realm in her absence. Overall, I still hold the [[Cloudspinner]] in high regard, but I also have doubts and fears about her and her intentions.

Melusine's lair is a small, secluded grotto located near a river in the realm of [[Amberglow]]. The grotto is hidden from view, surrounded by tall trees and overgrown with vines and foliage. The entrance is small and narrow, making it difficult for anyone to enter without being noticed. Inside, the grotto is cool and damp, with a small pool of water in the center. The walls are covered with intricate carvings and symbols, depicting the history and legends of [[Amberglow]]. Melusine has decorated the grotto with items that she has collected over the years, including treasures, trinkets, and magical items. She has also placed traps and wards around the grotto, to protect herself from unwanted visitors. Overall, Melusine's lair is a hidden and protected place, where she can retreat from the dangers and hardships of life in [[Amberglow]].
