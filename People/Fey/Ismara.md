# Ismara
>[!info]+ Biographical Info  
> A [[Fey|fey]] (she/her)  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

![[ismara.jpg|right|300]]Ismara is a wanderer and a scholar of humanity, an archivist of the [[Material Plane|Mundane World]]. She is curious and bright, and prone to travel where fate takes her, although she tends to shy from danger. 

%%SECRET

**Who She Is:**  
Ismara is a **fey scholar-traveler** from **Gleamwater**, a land of **golden canals and drifting gondolas.** She studies **motion, the way knowledge flows, and how choices shape the world.** Unlike traditional archivists, she does not **record history**—she **follows it.**

**Why She’s at Sunwine Hall:**  
She arrived here **on her way to either the Shrouded Archive or the Sunset Gate**, but she has **not yet chosen which.** She is watching **how the revel shifts, waiting for something to push her in a direction.**

**Why She Might Leave With the Party:**

- If they **present an unexpected course**—a path **she had not considered.**
- If they convince her that **inaction is itself a choice.**
- If they seem **like an event in motion**—something she must witness firsthand.

**How She Acts & Speaks:**

- **Moves like water, never hurried, never still.**
- **Speaks in fluid, looping sentences**, sometimes **pausing mid-thought, listening.**
- **Rarely gives direct answers—prefers to tease out meaning.**

**Example Quotes:**

- _"I came here because it felt like the right place to stop. Now I am looking for the right place to move."_
- _"The Shrouded Archive is a still pool, the Sunset Gate is a flowing river. Which do you think holds more truth?"_
- _"The question is not where I will go, but when."_
