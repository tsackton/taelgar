Lord Endless Ending, the lord of a manor in the [[Twilight Kingdom]], is a nearly seven foot figure of ambiguous gender presentation. They wear their long hair pulled back behind their neck, and wear a velvet evening jacket that fades from dark blue to pale blue like the evening sky at sunset. Their face is expressionless, almost mask-like -- mortals can only see the Lord's emotions as flickers seen out of the corner of an eye, though when you stare at it for too long, you feel as though the face you see them wearing is not actually what their face is. It is as if something else is inside of a human mask, something older and wilder, something less contained, less containable, like an unfinished story.

Lord Endless Ending, once known as Lord Revel Beginning, is the host of an endlessly ending party at their manor in the [[Twilight Kingdom]].
