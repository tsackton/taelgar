Generally speaking, the full consequences and series of events here is not worked out but likely doesn't matter in many contexts. 

Important backstory is at this point Apollyon had been exerting extremely widespread magical mind control via the [[Crown of Purity]] and the [[Scepter of Command]] to crush dissent across vast distances. 

When Apollyon is imprisoned and his attunement to the crown and the scepter shattered, the administrative infrastructure of the Empire collapses essentially overnight. This would probably cause widespread famine and civil unrest.
