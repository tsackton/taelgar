# The Battle of Heartroot Vale
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_PageDatedValue")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Battle of Heartroot Vale was a climatic victory by the forces of the [[Refounded Alliance of Aurbez]] and the [[Dunmar Fellowship]] against the [[Iron Fang]] hobgoblins and the [[Empress of Chaos]]. 

The [[Rangers]] and the [[Refounded Alliance of Aurbez|Aurbeze]] and [[Lizardfolk]] defenders, aided by a vast magical illusion and shapeshifting magic provided by [[Seeker]] and his simulacrum, Seeker 5, [[Session 132 (DuFr)|lured the hobgoblin host into an ambush]]. With [[Vindristjarna]] and an illusory dragon providing air support, the [[Iron Fang]] hobgoblins were bogged down in battle. But with the defenders bogged down as well, the [[Empress of Chaos]] sped past aiming for the [[Heartroot]] itself, using decoy troops on flying chaos-metal hawks to distract [[Vindristjarna]] and the [[Dunmar Fellowship]]. 

Noticing the deception, the [[Dunmar Fellowship]] raced to intercept the [[Empress of Chaos]], using _Teleport_ to arrive at the vale in time. With a _Force Cage_ protecting the [[Heartroot]] itself, the [[Dunmar Fellowship|Fellowship]] attacked. The battle seemed to be won when the party defeated the [[Empress of Chaos|Empress]], but her [[Chaos Metal]] armor erupted into a cataclysm of elemental chaos, unleashing a devastating attack that disrupted the _Force Cage_ and exposed the vulnerable [[Heartroot]]. To stop it, [[Kenzo]] transformed into a silver dragon, destroying the elemental cataclysm, saving the [[Heartroot]], and then turning on the last of the [[iron Fang]] survivors, leading a devastating counterattack that shattered the hobgoblin lines.
