The Great War is the name given to the war against  [[Cha'mutte]] that united many human and non-human realms to fight against the vast armies of hobgoblins, monsters, and other allies gathered by [[Cha'mutte]]. It was fought on many fronts, with major battles involving [[Dunmar]], the [[Istabor Alliance]], [[Sembara]], and [[Chardon]], among others. The war ended in DR 1545 with Cha'mutteâ€™s defeat by the [[Heroes of the Great War]] at the [[Battle of Urlich Pass]].

The Great War is remembered as an existential war: a struggle not merely against invasion, but against an implacable enemy who sought to destroy the very gods themselves and overthrow the order of the world.

## Legacy

The Great War still leaves a lasting legacy on the world today. The battles of the Great War and its aftermath reshaped the western coast, seeing Chardon turn from a city of mages and merchants to an imperial capital, while many of the other realms in this part of the world, including the great elven realm of [[Ainumarya]], were destroyed. In Dunmar, the landscape itself was radically altered, changing the climate. In the Green Sea, the Plaguelands grew where [[Cha'mutte's Plague]] had lain heaviest, and the passes through the [[Sentinel Range|Sentinels]] at [[Urlich Pass]] were destroyed in a cataclysm upon Cha'mutte's death.
