%%SECRET

In 1584, war erupts in the mountain interior. Still need to figure out exact details about who is attacking, and how this starts. But the consequences are very bad:
- The formerly thriving dwarven and elven populations in the interior are destroyed. The dwarves entirely so, the elves mostly so, with the few survivors retreating to stoneborn communities, who become isolated bastions of safety. 
- A few years later, the enemy breaks out of the mountains, attacking into the coast. Upland Cedrano is conquered, Portalia is destroyed, and the canal is damaged/taken. This is definitive end of the pirate kingdoms, who retreat south into Emerald Bay. This is a violent occupation. Anyone caught is killed, chaos reigns, and any kind of rulership is gone. People struggle just to survive and hide. This is also a really rough time for halflings on the Gulf of Chardon, who are hunted and summarily executed. 
- In 1590s sometime, enemy builds an armada, aiming to attack Chardon. This is the instigator of Chardon's turn into a naval power. 
- Chardon wins, conquers Portalia, repairs the canal, and wins a series of wars in the inland. Raziolo is already well-fortified, having survived; Cedrano is basically unassailable from land (and also survived). More forts are built in the inland, for a generation watch is kept, and communication flows between stoneborn redoubts and Chardonians. Raziolo is still independent at this point.
