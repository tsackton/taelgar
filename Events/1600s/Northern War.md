# The Northern War
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_PageDatedValue")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

A brutal war fought in the north of [[Dunmar]] against the [[Blackened Claw]] hobgoblin clan. Ended is a complete and total Dunmari victory, with the hobgoblins crushed. 

It was during this war that the old fortress of [[Raven's Hold]] was occupied by hobgoblin troops, who used it as a base of operations to raid against Dunmari to the south and west.

%%SECRET

Possibly not canonical idea about the start of this war:

In DR 1599, the current ruler dies. His named heir, yet another descendant of Kundar, a niece, despite having received supported when chose as heir, is not accepted by the Mulaak as Samraat, and the argument that rule in Dunmar has never been hereditary carries the day. A compromise candidate, a woman who is the informal head of one of the largest clans living in the region between Tokra and Songara, is chosen. 

She refuses to settle her court in Nayahar, and instead follows the Dunmari tradition of a traveling court, moving to each part of the kingdom with the changing of the seasons. While she was no one’s first choice, she turns out to be a skilled ruler and a cultural unifier, and under her rule the concept of a Greater Dunmar begins to emerge, the goal of reclaiming the lost lands of the desolation of Cha’mutte, and building a Dunmar under the gods for all ways of life, begins to emerge. She also begins to organize funds to finish the dwarven peace monument and rebuild relations with the Yuvanti dwarves. ~Askandi founded here, becomes center of Dwarven trade in east~


Her reign is tragically cut short, though, by a hobgoblin ambush in DR 1603 (orchestrated by Agata, who fears her) as her court is traveling north of the Trade Road towards the upper reaches of the Hara river. The Dunmari response is swift. A new Mulaak votes almost unanimously to appoint her skilled commander as Samraat, and with a unified Dunmar behind him, he launches a costly but decisive war against the Blackened Claw clan in the north. By DR 1610, the Blackened Claw clan has been crushed decisively, slaughtered nearly in its entirety, the last remnants of Cha’mutte’s army fleeing north and east to try to scratch out a living in the borders of the Plaguelands.
