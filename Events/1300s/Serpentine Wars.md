# The Serpentine Wars
>[!info]+ Information

The Serpentine Wars describe the fifty year period in the second have of the DR 1300s, during which the [[Dominion of Avatus]] expanded to control much of the [[Duchy of Maseau]] and the [[Maseau Gap]]. While the entire period was marked by border tension, it can be separated into five significant wars where substantial fighting occurred. 

## The Westcliff War
_DR 1353 - DR 1356_

%% brainstorming suggests the start of this war is because Avatus exhausted his supplies of serpentine, but this is not introduced in game anywhere and the early causes of this war could change as needed %%

In DR 1353, Avatus attacked north of the Maseau Gap for the first time. Avoiding the large and well-defended [[Duchy of Maseau]], to the west, his armies focused on conquering a narrow, lightly settled, and poorly defended strip of land along the western edge of the [[Westcliff]]. After four years of campaigning, this war ended quietly with [[Avatus]] in control of a small, but important, stretch of land between [[Duchy of Maseau|Maseau]] and [[Addermarch]]. The [[Duchy of Maseau]] and [[Addermarch]] both remain officially neutral.

###

- (DR:: 1353): The Westclif War starts as Avatus attacks into the northeastern parts of the [[Maseau Gap]]
- (DR:: 1356): The Westclif War ends with Avatus establishing a vassal state on the western edge of the [[Westcliff]]. 

## The Berserker War
_DR 1359 - DR 1362_

%% the instigating logic can change, but the basic framework is that Maseau agrees to a somewhat oppressive treaty imposing serpentine obligations, while Addermarch does not. Thus, Avatus attacks Addermarch in retaliation. The details are not canonically established, but the basic framework is. %%

After victory in the Westcliff War, Avatus presses for favorable trade deals with [[Addermarch]] and the [[Duchy of Maseau]], seeking guarantees of [[serpentine]] delivery. Maseau agrees; Addermarch does not. This quickly precipitates the Berserker War, so named for the serpentine berserkers who formed the elite of Avatus' armies. 

Over three years of campaigning, Avatus established a foothold in some narrow passes of the [[Westcliff]], but the terrain is challenging and it is nearly impossible to march large armies over the mountains. The Berserker War ended in a stalemate, with both Addermarian and Avatan forces retreating in part due to the effects of a brutal, cold winter that led to widespread famine in DR 1362 - DR 1363.

###
- (DR:: 1359): The Berserker War begins with probing raids by the Dominion of Avatus against the western shires of [[Addermarch]]. 
- (DR:: 1360), spring: Avatus' armies begin widespread campaigns in western Addermarch; fighting continues over the next several years.
- (DR:: 1362), fall: Widespread and unseasonable snows end the campaigning season early and close the Westcliff passes. Conventionally considered the end of the Berserker War.
- (DR:: 1363): Very late thaw disrupts spring plantings, leading to widespread famine.

## The Broken Scales War
_DR 1364 - DR 1377_

In the fall of DR 1364, after two hard years of famine, a Maseaun trade embassy to the Dominion of Avatus refused to accept a demand to increase [[serpentine]] trade shipments, symbolically breaking their weighing scales in protest.  This precipitated the Broken Scales War, also known as the Great Serpentine War. 

In a series of campaigns over the course of 13 years, the armies of Avatus break through the lines of defense along the Westcliff, loccupying significant parts of western Addermarch. Avatus also destroys numerous fortifications in eastern Maseau, forcing the Duchy to capitulate and pay a steep tribune in serpentine. Many Maseua lords flee into exile. 

In Addermarch, the Broken Scales War was the beginning of a long period of occupation and guerrilla  warfare; the [[Rangers]] trace their origin, in part, to the guerrilla fighters of this era. 

###
- (DR:: 1364), fall: Maseau refuses to increase serpentine trade shipments, provoking war with Avatus
- ((DR:: 1369)): Avatus occupies significant parts of [[Weskershire]] and [[Carlinshire]] in [[Addermarch]] %% the exact timing and details of how far into Addermarch Avatus gets, and when this happens, are undecided; what is crucial for the Addermarch campaign is that he occupied much of the [[Highmoor]] and parts of [[Torvaine Forest]], and he didn't occupy [[Adderfell]]. But anything from Torvaine Forest serving as a barrier to a failed siege of Adderfell and a shift in resources to Maseau are possible. %%
- (DR:: 1377): Maseau capitulates to Avatus, ending the Broken Scales War. Maseau becomes a tributary state but is allowed to retain independence; Addermarch refuses to capitulate and remains occupied. 

## The Addermarch Liberation War
_DR 1385 - DR 1388_

In DR 1385, the long-simmering guerrilla war fought against Avatus' occupying forces in Addermarch broke into open warfare. Over the next few years, the Addermarians successfully drove Avatus out of western Addermarch. In Addermarch, this war is often simply called the Liberation War. 

- (DR:: 1385) - (DR_end:: 1388): The Addermarch LIberation War, when Addermarch drives Avatus' forces out of western Addermarch. 

## The Exiles' War
_DR 1400 - DR 1403_

In the years following the liberation of Addermarch, a group of exiled Maseaun lords began to make a plot to drive Avatus out of Maseau. In DR 1400, these exiles launched an attack, and were badly beaten.

### The Serpentine Wars
*(1350s - 1405ish)*

In the 1350s, Avatus exhausted the easy supplies of serpentine, which was crucial in some way to (something something, probably never really clear outside his realm). This leads to a series of conflicts between Avatus and his northern neighbors, primarily Maseau, unnamed minor duchy, and Addermarch. 

- (DR:: 1353) - (DR_end:: 1357): ~First Serpentine War~. Avatus first uses as a pretext raids from unnamed minor duchy to launch an attack north, along the narrow strip of land (7 in map above), capturing some minor serpentine deposits on the western edges of the Westcliff. Maseau is convinced to remain officially neutral (though some probably push to take the opportunity to attack Avatus) - though Avatus is deeply creepy in >100 years has never attacked Maseau, while minor unnamed duchy has. Notably, Avatus probably sells the fiction, promoting the war officially as stabilizing the gap / punishing raiders for everyone's benefit. 
- (DR:: 1357) - (DR_end:: 1359): Avatus has no interest in Vaubonne (maybe it doesn't have deposits of serpentine), but is deeply interested in the rest of Westcliff and in the southern/western mountains in Maseau. Establishes a vassal / client state between his Dominion and Vaubonne as a buffer (in northern part of 7 on the map). Sends increasingly strident demands to negotiate mineral deals to both Addermarch and Maseau. Initially, Maseau agrees, signing a treaty in which they will ship X amount of serpentine to Avatus, in exchange for Y silver or something (this is surface-level fair but Maseau likely doesn't know true value of serpentine; Avatus perhaps also later twists this, starts ramping up demands, etc). Addermarch refuses.
- (DR:: 1359) - (DR_end:: 1362): ~First Addermarch War~. Avatus attacks Addermarch, in a series of brutal campaigns where serpentine berserkers appear on the battlefield. Maseau gets scared and begins planning to violate treaty. Addermarch holds on, but barely. 
- (DR:: 1362) - (DR_end:: 1363), winter: Brutal, cold winter and late spring causes widespread famine, interrupting the summer campaigning season (or something else, vibe is basically things suck but Avatus takes a break)
- (DR:: 1363): Maseau refuses to deliver serpentine per treaty. 
- (DR:: 1364) - (DR_end:: 1377): ~Second Serpentine War~. Avatus unleashes hobgoblins for the first time, fighting a two front war to defeat Maseau and Addermarch. This proceeds in fits and starts with some years consumed by stalemate and local skirmishing. By the end of the war, Maseau has been forced to capitulate and now must pay tribute in serpentine, destroy fortifications, and other things; many lords are forced into exile in Brovna and Wisford. Addermarch refuses to surrender, and most of west Addermarch is occupied. 
- (DR:: 1377) - (DR_end:: 1385): Proto-Rangers in Addermarch begin campaign of guerilla harassment against Avatus forces. During this time, Avatus brings word of Cha'mutte's rise to Wisford and Brovna, pitching them on an my enemy of my enemy is my friend alliance, assuring them the troubles in Maseau and Addermarch are just mundane squabbles over broken treaties and the like. Of course people are suspicious, but treaties are signed, etc, even while the Addermarch guerilla campaign continues apace. 
- (DR:: 1385) - (DR_end:: 1388): ~Second Addermarch War~. Addermarch rises up and throws off Avatus. 
- (DR:: 1388) - (DR_end:: 1400): Growing discontent among exiled barons of Maseau, and inspiration from Addermarch. Plots hatched by especially the sons of exiled Maseau lords to take their territory back. 
- (DR:: 1400) - (DR_end:: 1403): ~Maseau War~. Exiled Maseau lords attack from the north, in coordination with local uprisings, and are badly beaten by Avatus. Maseau nobility executed and hobgoblin lords installed in their place. Avatus formally annexes the mineral rich parts of Maseau (south and west), and leaves a rump client state in place as a buffer to the north. 

This period ends with Avatus in control of two vassal states in the north (rump Duchy of Maseau) and the vassal state established in late 1350s.
