# Cavarrio's Chronicles of Drankor

A list of the Emperors of Drankor, based on Cavarrio's Chronicles of Drankor, considered the definitive history of the Empire in post-Great War Chardon. May not be accurate; written based exclusively on secondary sources, many of which have since been lost. Takes the form of annals of emperors.

Cavarrio divides the history of Drankor into five eras:
- Founding Era, when the Drankorian Empire was established and first grew across what is now the lower Istaros and the Dunmari Basin, and then into the western Green Sea. 
- First Peace, a long period of peace and prosperity and stability
- Western Expansion, when the Empire expanded north along the west coast
- Golden Age, a second period of peace and prosperity. 
- Decline, marked by civil war and then fruitless battles in the east, leading up to the destruction of Drankor.

## From Campaign Notes
Many generations ago, the Drankorian Empire was at the height of its power. As it grew, and thrived, a line of Emperors came to power who had ambitions not of conquest, but of peace ("Golden Age"). The borders of the Empire stopped growing; treaties were signed; while the influence of Drankor grew, its military might and magical power shrank. Many in the ruling elite married outsiders, and the strength of the line of Hkar diminished. For some, this was unacceptable. To be ruled by one of impure blood was an insult not to be borne.

Generations of peace were shattered when the [[Omnis Pura]], a group that had been growing in secret, dedicated to the superiority of the Hkaran line, assassinated the Emperor Helea, the third in a line of non-Hkaran Emperors, and the first non-Hkaran woman to rule, with the help of mysterious figure known only as Cha'mutte. At first, the [[Omnis Pura]] only sought to control the Empire and return rule to a pure-blooded Hkaran line. But over time, this became an obsession with purity, with the inferiority of other races, and eventually the [[Omnis Pura]] turned to genocide, after years of growing restrictions on non-Hkarans. <this potentially involves them wiping a race out entirely, and also vicious attacks on non-humans and non-Hkarans, including potentially a massive slaughter of halflings, or possibly some other race if Schwartz doesn't want to play a character faced with that bit of history>.

Cha'mutte became displeased with this turn of events. He wanted to see the Elder races freed from the influence of the Firstborn, not crushed under the heel of another god. He valued chaos, yes, and creative destruction, but genocide and fascism did not serve his purpose, and he did not want the Elder races disappearing from the land. When the [[Omnis Pura]] gained access to the power to destroy a race, Cha'mutte struck. He unleashed a devasting Plague that killed all in its wake, destroying the Drankorian Empire and causing chaos to spread.
