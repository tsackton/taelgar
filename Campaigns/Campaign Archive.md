# Campaign Archive

Here are collected the stories of campaigns and adventures that, while no longer active, continue to shape the history and myth of Taelgar. 

The most significant of these are:
- [[Dunmari Frontier Campaign]]: the story of the [[Dunmar Fellowship]], starting from their early adventures in [[Karawa]] and growing to their heroic exploits that reshaped [[Chardon]], [[Drankor]], and the [[Plaguelands]]. 
- [[Great War Campaign]]: the story of the [[Heroes of the Great War]], who defeated [[Cha'mutte]] in the [[Great War]]. Though many of their exploits are only faintly remembered as the stuff of myth and legend in the modern day, their names are still recognized across much of Taelgar. 

Past adventures also include:
- [[Labyrinths of the Lost]]: the story of the [[Labyrinth Prisoners]], four prisoners caught in the machinations of a mad scientist in [[Western Cymea]].
- [[Into the Chasm]]: the story of the [[Chasm Explorers]], a group of travelers who came together to escape from mysterious circumstances in the [[Great Chasm]].
- [[Oskar in Tharn Todor]]: the story of [[Oskar]] and [[Riswynn]]'s adventures together, with a variety of companions, around [[Tharn Todor]].
