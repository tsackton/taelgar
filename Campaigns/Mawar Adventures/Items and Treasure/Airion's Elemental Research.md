# Airion's Research on the Elemental Plane of Water

Three annotated books concerning the [[Elemental Plane of Water]] and the [[Sentient Ocean]], once owned by [[Airion|Airion the Mistspeaker]]. His marginal glosses connect their content to his own research into elemental magic, planar weak points, genies, and the [[Sentient Ocean]].

The three books are:

- [[On the Mysteries of the Infinite Depths]], a Drankorian natural history treatise on leviathans and other deep creatures of the [[Elemental Plane of Water]], including [[Gazankoa]].
- [[On Elemental Forms]], a Drankorian-era treatise on elemental beings, especially the sentient and powerful genies.
- [[Studies of the Westward Deeps]], an Elvish natural history and maritime survey of the western ocean, preserved here in Airion's annotated copy, which includes a discussion of the [[Sentient Ocean]]. 

Azar studied the annotated books after emerging in [[Mendin]] with Airion's treasure. From Airion's glosses, he learned enough about Yi'weti to believe that living water could make his false resurrection ritual work. That conclusion led him to use [[Airion's Ring of Elemental Binding]] to compel [[Nimessa]], a [[Marid|marid]], to steal living water from the [[Sentient Ocean]].

After Azar's defeat at [[Hamri#The Shark's Dock|Shark's Dock]], the [[Adventurers of Mawar]] searched his lair in the Splinters and recovered the books along with the brass bowl and coins.
