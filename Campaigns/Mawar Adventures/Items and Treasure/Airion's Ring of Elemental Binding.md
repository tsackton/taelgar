# The Ring of Elemental Binding
>[!info]+ Information  
> (ring)  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

One of the treasures of [[Airion|Airion the Mistspeaker]], recovered by [[Azar the Lost]] during his search for lore about elemental magic, genies, and living ocean water. According to [[Azar's Tale]], the ring could bind a genie and command one service from it.

Azar used the ring to compel [[Nimessa]], a [[Marid|marid]] of the [[Elemental Plane of Water]], to steal living water from the [[Sentient Ocean]] and bring it to [[Hamri]]. [[The Ciphered Scroll]] records that Nimessa had little choice but to obey and that she could not harm Azar until a thousand and one days had passed after the command.

The spent ring, no longer magical, remains a valuable gold-and-sapphire ring, given by Azar to [[Ovina]] as payment for the construction of a lead-lined box meant to quiet the [[Blood Knife]].
