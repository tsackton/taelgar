# Airion's Bowl of Commanding Water Elementals
>[!info]+ Information  
> (elemental command bowl)  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

A magical bowl recovered from Azar's lair in the [[Splinters]]. It was one of the treasures of [[Airion|Airion the Mistspeaker]] that [[Azar the Lost]] carried with him after returning from the mountains to the western coast of the [[Mawakel Peninsula]].

After the fight at [[Hamri#The Shark's Dock|Shark's Dock]], [[Wazir]] identified it more specifically as a bowl of commanding water elementals. It must be filled with fresh ocean water and used immediately in order to work.
