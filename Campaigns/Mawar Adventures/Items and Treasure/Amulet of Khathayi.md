# The Amulet of Khathayi
>[!info]+ Information  
> (amulet)  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

A golden amulet carried by a fire cultist dedicated to a mysterious entity known as [[Khathayi|Khathayi the Flametongue]]. It was found by [[Ryu]], [[Nerissa]], and [[Ander]], who noticed that the motif on the amulet matched a symbol on [[Hiyasa's Treasure Map]] drawn near an island north of the peninsula.
