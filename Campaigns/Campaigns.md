# Campaigns

Campaign pages collect player-facing material from games set in Taelgar: landing pages, session notes, player characters, handouts, timelines, treasure, and other campaign-specific records.

Start with [[Current Games]] for active games and recent adventures. Finished and older campaigns are collected in the [[Campaign Archive]].

## Current Games

- [[Addermarch Campaign]], following the [[Addermarch Mercenaries]] in DR 1715.
- [[Great Library Campaign]], following the [[Silver Tempests]] around [[Voltara]] and the northern provinces of Chardon.
- [[Mawar Adventures]], episodic adventures around [[Hamri]] and the [[Mawakel Peninsula]].
- [[Lost in the Feywild]], a short adventure beginning near [[Tollen]].

The Cleenseau game is run by Mike Sackton and is published separately at [Mike's TaelgarVerse](https://msackton.github.io/taelgarverse1720/).

## Campaign Archive

- [[Dunmari Frontier Campaign]], the finished long-running campaign of the [[Dunmar Fellowship]].
- [[Great War Campaign]], an older campaign whose heroes and broad arc remain canonical.
- [[Labyrinths of the Lost]], a short adventure in [[Western Cymea]].
- [[Into the Chasm]], a short adventure in and around the [[Great Chasm]].
- [[Oskar in Tharn Todor]], episodic adventures with [[Oskar]], [[Riswynn]], and companions.
