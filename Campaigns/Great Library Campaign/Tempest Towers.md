The Tempest Towers are the [[Silver Tempests]]' manor and home base in central [[Voltara]], near the baths and market. The manor was gifted by [[Anton Lavernia]], the proconsul of Voltara, after the party's role in [[Great Library Session Notes - Arc 2|the defense of Voltara]] during [[Grumella's War]]. 

The manor has a courtyard garden, service and common rooms around the courtyard, plenty of space for entertaining, private towers for members of the [[Silver Tempests]], and a treasury below the courtyard. The treasury is well-protected, by a _Hallow_ spell, a _Glyph of Warding_, and an extraplanar vault installed by [[Eldeth Redhammer]]. 

[[Julius of Voltara]] serves as steward.
