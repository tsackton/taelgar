<!-- SLOT: combat.summary -->
- Distraction and Shade Ambush: pirate shades, pirate shadows. The party turns magical light and tinkering into a distraction that covers the rope ladder descent, then destroys the pirate shades and shadows in the lower cavern.
- Human Guard Ambush: human guard. After [[Grimstone]] refuses to be lured out, the party stops the returning guard from raising help; he chases [[Gaudin]] into an ambush and is killed by the [[Steampup|steampup]].
- Grimstone's Lab Fight: [[Grimstone]], stone spider swarm, stone wasp. The party kills [[Grimstone]], survives his animated stone spider swarm and stone wasp, and prevents [[Acescale]] from fully petrifying.
