## Treasure from the Tower of the Colossus

- A [[Taurion's Manual of Golems|manual of stone golems]], found in the basement storeroom guarded by a stone golem.
- Material for making spell scrolls, containing enough inks and special paper to produce 4 scrolls: 3 up to 3rd level, and 1 up to 5th level. Each scroll requires 8 hours of work to produce, with the kit
- Four spell scrolls: Sending, Private Sanctum, Reverse Gravity, and Passwall
- Six magical lenses of a variety of sizes, once apparently part of a telepscope. %%SECRET can be used to construct a spyglass of far seeing. When looking through the spyglass, you can see up to 1 mile away with no difficulty, able to discern even fine details as though looking at something no more than 100 feet away from you, assuming clear conditions. Once per day, you can use the spyglass to cast clairvoyance, targeting sight only with a range of a point you can see within 10 miles.%%
- A collection of about 150 scrolls, highly fragile and prone to disintegration if handled. A sampling of six scrolls revealed poems and plays, in Drankorian and Elvish. All but one particularly sturdy one left behind in the tower. %%SECRET handling these scrolls requires a DC 15 Dexterity check, replaced by a spell casting ability check if using mage hand. Failure means the scroll disintegrations. This is the literary collection of Taurion.%%
- Copies of five scrolls read in the tower, but not brought along for risk of disintegration.
