![[wayfarer-bow.jpg|right|400]]This wooden bow is made of an unusual dark wood, much harder than any mundane wood but incredibly light and flexible. 

%%^Campaign:DuFr%%
This bow was part of the hoard of the black dragon [[Ra'ghemdros]], kept on display as part of her collection of rare and unusual items. It is now carried by [[Wellby]].
%%^End%%

%%SECRET

This is a sentient magic item, possessed by a spirit of exploration, and a desire to see and experience new places. It spent many centuries stuck in Ra'ghemdros' hoard, a super-boring place to be, and is now slowly awakening in Wellby's possession.

It can communicate by emotion only, but has darkvision and sight and hearing. Can respond to telepathic thoughts.

Properties not listed in the D&D Beyond entry:
- Advantage on Search action when the target is within range of the bow's sight
- If fire an arrow of discovery without a destination in mind, the bow will pick something interesting
- Might gain one or two more special arrows as it "wakes up"
%%
