# The Cairn Dor Sigil
>[!info]+ Information  
> ([[Shadowfolds]] symbol)  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

The Cairn Dor Sigil is a shimmering green mark associated with the disappearance of [[Urkabi]] and other [[Lizardfolk Village Disappearances|lizardfolk villages]] in the DR 1730s and DR 1740s. It was often seen, shimmering sickly green, in the aftermath of the disappearance of a village. 

The source and cause was not discovered until much later, when the lizardfolk [[Samso]] and the [[Silver Tempests]] traveled to [[Cairn Dor]] and discovered that the sigil was connected to the [[Shemra Azem]] and the sleepless queen of Carin Dor, [[Yeshara]].
