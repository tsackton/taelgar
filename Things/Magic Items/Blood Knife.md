# The Blood Knife
>[!info]+ Information  
> (magic dagger)  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

The Blood Knife is a wicked-looking curved dagger made from some kind of black metal. It is connected to [[Gazankoa]] and was used by [[Azar the Lost|Azar]] in the murders around [[Hamri]], where victims were stabbed, drained of blood, and used in his failed attempts to restore the drowned dead.

The knife is somehow associated with Gazankoa, and possession of it brings dangerous dreams and influence. After Azar's defeat, the knife was recovered from his lair and sealed inside a lead-lined box, which appeared to quiet or contain it.

%%SECRET

Potential ability: When you reduce a creature to 0 hit points with this dagger, it drains their blood completely, and you gain 2d6 temporary hit points. Azar used it for his rituals.

Sources and context: [[Mawar Adventures Episode 02]], [[Azar's Tale]], [[The Ciphered Scroll]], [[Gazankoa]], [[Mawar Ep 2 - DM Notes]], [[Azar’s Lair]].
