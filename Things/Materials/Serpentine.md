# Serpentine
>[!info]+ Information  
> (material)

%% add links to relevant wars once pages exist %%

Serpentine is a pale green gemstone that is associated with necromancy and enchantment magic. 

Serpentine weapons, produced by [[Avatus]], played a major role in the wars between the [[Dominion of Avatus]] and [[Addermarch]]. Tracking and safely disposing these cursed weapons after these wars was an early formative task for the [[Rangers]], who recorded the [[Serpentine Register]] to document their efforts.
