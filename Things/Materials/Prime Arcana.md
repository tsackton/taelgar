# Prime Arcana
>[!info]+ Information  
> (hypothesized alchemical substance)  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

The Prime Arcana is a theoretical alchemical substance, discussed in [[The Way of the Stone]]. Rumors and legends suggest that many alchemists have sought this material, but there are no conclusive proofs it exists. It is said to be a key ingredient in many concoctions of great power, especially those granting invulnerability and youth. 

The mad alchemist [[Grimstone]], working in [[Western Cymea]], believed the Prime Arcana was related to petrification. He thought that transmuted flesh retained the essence of flesh, that magically created stone retained magic, and that a living soul turned into stone might produce the substance he needed. This led him to capture prisoners and use a captive basilisk to petrify them for study. He was [[Labyrinths of the Lost|killed]] when several of his [[Labyrinth Prisoners|kidnap victims]] turned on him.
