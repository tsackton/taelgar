# Codex Maledictum

> A cosmological treatise

*Codex Maledictum* is an infamous collection of accounts from supposed planar travelers who claimed to have glimpsed [[Pandemonium]]. The surviving descriptions gathered in the book are fragmentary and contradictory, depicting Pandemonium as a howling void of endless tunnels, wind-carved stone, and shadows that twist into laughter.
