# On the Classification of Divinity
>[!info]+ Information  
> A [[Drankorian Empire|Drankorian]] theological treatise, written during the [[History of the Drankorian Empire|Drankorian Era]]  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*On the Classification of Divinity* is an influential early Drankorian work of theological cosmology. Later scholars often cite it as source for the distinction between [[Embodied Gods]], powerful celestial beings with embodied manifestations, and [[Incorporeal Gods]], spiritual essences drawn from the divine power of creation, though the treatise itself does not claim to have discovered this theological distinction.
