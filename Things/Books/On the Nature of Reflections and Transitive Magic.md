# On the Nature of Reflections and Transitive Magic
>[!info]+ Information  
> A cosmological treatise  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*On the Nature of Reflections and Transitive Magic* collects the most extensive speculation on the hypothesized [[Mirror Realm]]. The work claims the Mirror Realm is real but dangerous to access, because a traveler who enters it allows their reflection to leave and potentially replace them in the [[Material Plane]].
