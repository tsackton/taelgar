# The Lay of Estar
>[!info]+ Information  
> An [[Elves|Elvish]] poem, written in ancient times  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*The Lay of Estar* is an ancient elven poem about Estar, a friend of wanderers and one of the first elves to discover, explore, and name the elemental planes. Its description of the [[Elemental Plane of Air]] presents the plane as a realm of endless skies, drifting clouds, howling winds, and floating earth motes.
