# On the Power of Creation
>[!info]+ Information  
> A [[Stoneborn]] cosmological treatise, by [[Yendalo]]  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*On the Power of Creation* is a treatise by [[Yendalo]], the Stoneborn metaphysicist. Yendalo argues that the [[Land of the Dead]] is best understood as the Divine Veil, a cosmological barrier shielding the Inner Realms from the full force of the [[Plane of Creation]], rather than simply a gathering place for souls.
