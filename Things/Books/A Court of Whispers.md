# A Court of Whispers
>[!info]+ Information  
> An [[Apporia|Apporian]] play, written in the DR 1400s  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*A Court of Whispers* is a tragedy about Avim of Cedrano, who enters a strange fey court full of games of chance and rare delicacies. Avim believes he spends only an afternoon there, but returns to [[Cedrano]] to find that ten years have passed.
