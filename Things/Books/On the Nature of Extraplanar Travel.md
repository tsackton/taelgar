# On the Nature of Extraplanar Travel
>[!info]+ Information  
> A cosmological treatise  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*On the Nature of Extraplanar Travel* is a seminal work recording careful experimentation with planar travel. Its best-attested conclusion is that [[Extraplanar Weak Point|extraplanar weak points]] do not make it easier to physically cross from one plane to another.
