# Studies of the Aerial Sphere
>[!info]+ Information  
> A [[Drankorian Empire|Drankorian]] cosmological monograph series, written during the [[History of the Drankorian Empire|Drankorian Era]], by members of the [[Occulta Ludum]]  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*Studies of the Aerial Sphere* is a monograph series on travel in the [[Elemental Plane of Air]], published by the Drankorian scholarly society known as the [[Occulta Ludum]]. Only three of a reported ten volumes survive in full, but the surviving volumes preserve first-hand accounts of falling, flight, wind currents, and navigation in the plane.
