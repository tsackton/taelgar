# On the Nature of Heat
>[!info]+ Information  
> A [[Drankorian Empire|Drankorian]] natural history treatise, written during the [[History of the Drankorian Empire|Drankorian Era]]  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*On the Nature of Heat* is a famous Drankorian work of natural history. Although not primarily a planar treatise, it provides the best-known summary of [[Frostfell]] as a land of bitter cold, ice fields, freezing pools, glaciers, icebergs, and endless storms of snow and wind.
