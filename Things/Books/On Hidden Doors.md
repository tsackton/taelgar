# On Hidden Doors
>[!info]+ Information  
> A [[Tyrwingha|Tyrwinghan]] cosmological treatise, written in the DR 1600s, by [[Emyr of Tafolwern]]  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*On Hidden Doors* is a forceful polemic by [[Emyr of Tafolwern]] on temporary planar connections. Emyr argues that [[Elemental Vortex|elemental vortexes]] are part of a broader class of temporary planar connections, including uncontrolled or unpredictable fey crossings, and that planar drift helps explain why permanent portals sometimes appear or disappear.
