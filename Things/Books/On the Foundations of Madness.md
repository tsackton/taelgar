# On the Foundations of Madness
>[!info]+ Information  
> A [[Tollen|Tollish]] theological treatise, written in the 1600s, by [[Nicole Ardouin]]  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*On the Foundations of Madness* is a rarely studied work by [[Nicole Ardouin]] on [[Pandemonium]]. Nicole collects fragments of lost writing and deep history concerning the plane, arguing that Pandemonium may be a divine realm of the embodied god known as the [[Mad One]], and that its apparent connection to [[Lycanthropes|lycanthropy]] is important evidence for this claim.
