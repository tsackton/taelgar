# The Riven Veil
>[!info]+ Information  
> A [[Drankorian Empire|Drankorian]] theological treatise, written during the [[History of the Drankorian Empire|Drankorian Era]], by [[Marcion of Iridel]]  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*The Riven Veil* is an ancient Drankorian text by the theological cosmologist [[Marcion of Iridel]]. Marcion claims that the [[Abyss]] arose from the worst impulses of humanity, drawing on [[Chironides the Wanderer]]'s argument that human preaching of hellfire and damnation helped shape the Abyss out of [[Soulstuff]].
