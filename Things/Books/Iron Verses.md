# Iron Verses
>[!info]+ Information  
> A [[Dwarves|Dwarven]] religious epic  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

*Iron Verses* is among the most famous dwarven religious epics describing the [[Heart of the Mountain]], the divine realm of the [[Bahrazel]]. One passage frequently translated into human tongues describes this realm as a vast cavern of endless tunnels, crystal lanterns, glittering gemstones, a dark central pool, and the distant sound of hammer on iron.
