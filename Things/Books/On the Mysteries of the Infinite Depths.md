# On the Mysteries of the Infinite Depths
>[!info]+ Information  
> A [[Drankorian Empire|Drankorian]] natural history treatise, written during the [[History of the Drankorian Empire|Drankorian Era]], by [[Leandros of Drankor]]

*On the Mysteries of the Infinite Depths* is [[Leandros of Drankor]]'s catalog of leviathans and other deep creatures of the [[Elemental Plane of Water]]. It describes colossal squids the size of cities, endless serpents, shadowy unidentified forms, and the vast being called [[Gazankoa]].
