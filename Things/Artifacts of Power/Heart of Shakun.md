![[heart_of_shakun.jpg|right|400]]The Heart of Shakun, at first glance, appears to be nothing more than a rough hewn piece of ochre, about the size of a fist. The more one watches it, though, the stranger it appears. It pulses slightly, almost imperceptibly, with a slow, regular rhythm, one beat a minute or so, getting just a bit larger then just a bit smaller. Across the surface, multihued veins run -- all muted, a bit, green and bronze and purple, like etchings done with powdered gemstones of emerald and amber and amethyst, but faint and barely visible below the surface of the rock itself. These veins seem to sparkle, and flow, gradually dancing over the surface of the stone in a mesmerizing dance, hard to perceive and yet once you notice it, hard to look away from.

It functions as a locus for the power of [[Shakun]] on the material plane, and must remain in the [[Red Mesa]], the physical embodiment of [[Shakun]] in this world, in order to function as the heartbeat of the [[Shakun Mystai]] and allow [[Shakun's Protection]] to be maintained on the eastern borders of [[Dunmar]].

One Note
Shakun's Heart is a fist-sized ochre stone, rough hewn with flecks of multicolored gemstones embedded within. If it is held, it Is much heavier than it seems. Every minute or so, the flecks of gems seem to subtly shift patterns.
 
Shakun's Heart is a poweful and mysterious artifact that represents the embodied divine spirit of Shakun on Taelgar, and when it is connected to the Mystai of Shakun functions in many ways like a powerful ley line node for divine magic.
 
It also powers the complex illusions that guard the borders of Dunmar, although this requires the combined action of many Mystai working in unison with the Heart.
 
However, it is also a powerful artifact in its own right, granting the attuned bearer a number of benefits (can improvise this based on what makes sense for Cintra). Can only be attuned by a divine soul sorcerer who is also a Mystai of Shakun.
 
### Appearance/Message
 
When each character approaches the heart they will experience different things:
 _Delwath_  

Delwath's patron has a special interest in Shakun's Heart. As a wellspring of divine belief they will be drawn to it, but since it is not *their* divine belief it is like fire. Sitting close is nice but get too close and you will be burned.
 
Delwath should feel this physically. When near it, feels warm, cozy, a sense of ease. But get too close and it burns.
 
Shadows gather around it, but can't get too close. When Delwath is in the shadows feels nice, stepping closer hurts.
 _Kenzo_  

Kenzo feels the Heart calling to him, feels the connection from his Lakan and Bhishma heritage. The heart will pull at him, trying to draw him in. If he is willing to accept the initiation, will be pulled through the web of connections, seeing brief glimpses of memories of the Mystai and sharing pieces of his memories in turn.
 _Wellby_  

Wellby will feel a deep sense of stillness, a certainly that this thing has a place in the world. Also will catch glimpses of the many halflings who have passed through Karawa over the centuries, fleeing war and plague and death, bringing goods, wanderers and scouts traveling for no real purpose, a blur of halfling life.
 _Seeker_  

Seeker will see a puzzle, a complex contraption that seems impossible, like somehow the world itself was folded around this point in space and some other world or reality intruded.
 _Drikod_  

Drikod will feel the desert wind, taste the dust and sand, balanced by the cool water of an oasis amid the rock.
