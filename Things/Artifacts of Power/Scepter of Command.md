It was carried by the fire giant who destroyed [[Kharsan]], you presume [[Mavdyr]] who led an [[Orcs|orc]] horde to attack [[Tokra]] in 1552 by the Drankorian Reckoning, 7 years after the death of [[Cha'mutte]]. ## Destruction
The Scepter of Command was destroyed in 1748, by [[Dunmar Fellowship]]. It was immersed in the sacred waters on the island of [[Vetta|Vetta]] in the Skaer Islands for 24 hours during a full moon, and then shattered with the combined power of [[Kenzo]]'s staff and the might of [[Kaikkea]]'s waters.

## Visions of the Scepter of Command in Eudomes’ Mirror

As you hold up the scepter, concentrating on the story of the last person who wielded it, you see, for a moment, [[Agata]] holding the scepter. A look of power, joy, and intense desire is on her face as she grabs hold of it. As she grabs it, you can just glimpse her other hand passing someone an elaborate scroll case, the end cap familiar. It is the same end cap of the scroll case you found in  Raven’s Hold, left in the dust and sand of the summoning circle there. 

Then, the mirror and the scepter have other ideas, and you find yourself being pulled and pulled further back in time. You see a brief moment of fire and destruction, as you, the scepter, are hanging unused from the belt of an enormous giant, stomping through a city you recognize as Kharsan, setting fire to it with a massive flaming axe. You can almost feel through the mirror deep anger from the scepter at just sitting, unused, on the belt of some dumb brute.

Then the image shifts again, and you see an image of a city laid out before you. A vast city, pyramids rising up, huge monuments surrounding a vast open square below and in front of you. You are standing and looking down at an army of thousands filling the square. You, the scepter are raised up, and you see a shimmering cloak on the back of the person holding you, almost an illusory multicolored rainbow of a cloak. Words are coming out of the mouth of the one who holds you, and the army is excited, animated with eagerness and loyalty. They pump their fists, slam spears and swords on shields, and chant something you cannot hear. You recognize this as a Drankorian army, and for a brief moment seen visions of them marching through rolling fields, before the image fades. 

And then you swirl back even further in time. And you see a figure, probably Drankorian from their features. Sharp eyes, dark hair, a proud, haughty demeanor. Old, and strong, and assured of their skill and value. The same person who was leading the armies in the last vision. He is holding the scepter, newly made, and turns to look out the window, revealing a vista of a back tower, nothing else except the sea, the waves crashing into the coast of this perfectly circular island. 

The mirror then fades to black.

## Destruction Lore from Session 75

- created by Apollyon by shredding the soul of a devil, a high ranking fiend in the service of [Fierna](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/mtof/the-blood-war#FiernaandBelial), the infernal leader of a [cult of Fierna](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/mtof/the-blood-war#CultofFierna), binding the power of this fiend and of Fierna herself to the magic of the item
- to destroy the item requires bathing it water imbued with the power of Divinity by generations of human prayer, for 24 hours during a full moon, and then striking the scepter with a magical weapon while doing at least 20 points of radiant damage to it
	- on a success, the scepter shatters and something bad happens but party might not know about this (probably involves summoning a devil?)

More specifically:

1) Bathe the scepter in holy water for 24 hours, during a full moon. The holy water must have accumulated the power of the prayers of humans for at least 13 generations (~300-350 years), so must be a sacred spring or other kind of sacred source that has had continual prayer and presence since and through the great war. 
2) Strike the scepter with a magical weapon or attack roll spell (it is unaffected by anything that forces a saving throw) that does radiant damage, until it is destroyed. The scepter has an AC of 15, 30 hit points, and can only take damage from attacks from a magical weapon. It is immune to all damage except radiant (can finesse this a bit, the point is you need to strike it with a magic weapon, really)

When the scepter shatters, the soul of the destroyed devil is released. A psychic wind, a mental burning flashback of all the terrible things the shredded devilish soul has done, blows through a 30 foot radius area around the scepter. Everyone in that area must make a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw, taking 10d6 psychic damage on a failure, half that on a success. In addition, a creature that fails their save by 5 or more is embedded with a fragment of the devil's soul. Whenever they attempt to take a long rest, they must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, they gain no benefit of the rest, as terrible nightmares of a burning hell haunt their dreams. This effect can be removed by any magic that ends possession (dispel good and evil, hallow, or similar). 

What legend lore reveals:

- The scepter was created by binding a shredded essence of a devil princeling, a servant of the Archdevil Fierna, to the scepter. Fierna is an archdevil known as the most cunning manipulator of mortal souls in the Nine Hells, save the King of Devils himself. 
- The devil's essence is completely bound in with the scepter, and protects it from almost any damage. Fire, no matter how strong, will do nothing; nor will lightning, or any mortal force. 
- Only if the scepter is bathed in sacred water, that has accumulated the power of the prayers of humans for at least 13 generations, can the hold of the devilish essence on this item be weakened, allowing it to be destroyed. And this is only strong enough when the moon is full and the extraplanar tides pull close to Taelgar's shores. 
- The scepter must rest in these waters for a day and a night, and only then, when drawn from the waters, will it be vulnerable. Even then, only an attack that deals damage with divine fire can shatter the scepter and destroy the devilish essence trapped within.
