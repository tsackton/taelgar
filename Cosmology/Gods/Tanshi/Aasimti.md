# Aasimti

The spirit of forest leaves and dappled shadows, associated with the [[Elderwood]] in particular. Became entangled with [[Rai]] and protected the [[Te'kula]] from [[Mezzar|Grimbaskal]] until [[Mezzar|Grimbaskal]]'s death.
