%%^Campaign:dufr%%
Shakun's Realm, as taught by the [[Shakun Mystai]] who guard the eastern borders of [[Dunmar]], is a demiplane of divine power, created by the goddess [[Shakun]] and connected to the [[Material Plane]] by the physical manifestation of [[Heart of Shakun|her heart]] and [[Red Mesa|her body]]. To the enemies of Dunmar, who find themselves pulled by [[Shakun's Protection]], this realm is a maze of rocky, broken badlands and twisting canyons, from which there is no escape. To the Dunmari who travel there, it is something else, but none can say exactly what, for the plane appears as [[Shakun]] herself wishes it. 

Few among the [[Shakun Mystai]] bother to attempt to classify the mystery of [[Shakun]] as the planar geographers and other scholars on the [[Faculty of Metaphysics]] might, though if they did, it would likely be thought of as a demiplane of the [[Divine Realms]], spun of [[Soulstuff]] and the [[Plane of Creation]], anchored to the [[Material Plane]] by the physicality of Shakun herself, and the echoes of her mother [[Bhishma]], who was born as a mortal. 

Shakun's Realm is accessible from [[Shakun’s Wellspring]], on the southern edge of the [[Red Mesa]], where a portal exists that is open for a short time around dawn and dusk each day.

%%SECRET
Spun from the divine energy of Arkaash is the demiplane of Shakun. This is a realm of dense, impenetrable forests; vast empty plains; rocky, broken badlands; and twisting canyonlands and deserts of rock and sand. Everywhere navigation is impossible, and the land shifts slowly with time, although the sun never moves in the sky, and day and night do not come in predictable patterns.

This is the land that claims those who try to penetrate Shakun’s protections around the eastern border of Dunmar. The most persistent creatures of evil intent find themselves slowly wandering here, until they go mad and slowly waste away. Fragments of creatures that got lost her can still be found, witless and wandering, in the dusts. 

Shakun's Domain is a twisted labyrinth of a place, a maze that is where the enemies of Dunmar go when they are lost in the borderlands. Strange, angry spirits; wild weather; and the ghosts of those who died haunt the land.

But all is not grim for there are also boons to be found by those who honor Shakun, and for those who can follow the hidden paths it can be a realm of great beauty.

### General Terrain

Shakun's realm is a mis-mash of all the terrain that has ever existing in Dunmar, but all in forms that are maze-like and confusing to traverse.

- Great ancient forests filled with dense undergrowth, thickets, and plant life.
- Twisting canyonlands with dead ends and sharp turns at every corner
- Vast plains with no way to tell direction in a flat gray sky and grass that grows taller than the average human.
- Cracked badlands with scrub and thorns blocking every accessible route, sharp drops and steep, gravelly cliffs than are hard to scale (Eman Muiyl from LOTR)

Everywhere is difficult terrain and everywhere is very easy to get lost. Also, notably, there is no open water anywhere in Shakun's realm (although plant life seems to grow just fine), and the sun never changes, it is always pre-dawn or dusk, and always dim light.

The terrain is constantly shifting, so that paths vanish and desert can be adjacent to old growth forest. Backtracking is impossible.

### Navigation

Direction and time are mostly meaningless in Shakun's realm, so normal forms of navigation are, if not meaningless, hard to use.

Navigation is in large part by mood, feeling, that sort of thing.
