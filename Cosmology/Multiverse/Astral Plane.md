Often described as a vast, empty, silvery sea, marked only by wisps of stray thought, the Astral Plane is the interstitial space in which the spiritual realms exist. 

%%SECRET
The Astral Plane is the interstitial "glue" in which the spiritual realms exist. 

The Plane of Souls, and its echos (the Dreamworld, the Nightmare Realm) can be reached from the "Border Astral" plane, the closest bit to the material plane. Indeed, in many ways the Plane of Souls can be thought of as the "border astral", a psychic parallel of the border ethereal. 

The further "out / up" you travel in the astral plane, the closer you get to the River of Souls; this border cuts across the Astral Plane and forms a difficult-to-cross boundary. 

Beyond the River of Souls, you are in the "deep Astral", where the divine realms can be found. 

In some ways, it is probably more correct to describe the Plane of Souls and the Land of the Dead as regions of the Astral Plane. From another perspective, one could say that the Astral Plane only truly exists beyond the River of Souls / Land of the Dead. 

From a mechanical / game perspective, the Astral Projection spell allows you to materialize beyond the Land of the Dead, in the "Deep Astral". Other, less powerful astral effects allow you to materialize / perceive the "Border Astral", which connects to the Plane of Souls / Dreamworld / Nightmare Realm only, and "ends" at the Land of the Dead.
