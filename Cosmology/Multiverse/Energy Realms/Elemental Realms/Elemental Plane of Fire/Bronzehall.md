Bronzehall is a sprawling city carved into the side of a massive obsidian mountain. Lava rivers flow through its grand avenues, illuminating the city with a fiery glow. The architecture is a blend of practicality and art, with bronze statues and artworks adorning the city's many plazas. Being one of the few Azer strongholds, it is a hub for trade, especially in magical materials and artifacts. Frankar and Zephyra were frequently seen traveling through Bronzehall, acquiring rare materials and knowledge for the Crucible. Rumors suggest that Zephyra had contacts in the city, possibly other efreeti or powerful Azer artisans who held the secrets necessary for the Crucible's completion.
