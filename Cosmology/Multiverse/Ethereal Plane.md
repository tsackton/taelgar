# The Ethereal Plane
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Ethereal Plane is a strange realm of mists and fog that stretches from the borders of the Material Plane to unknown depths. It is rumored that a skilled traveler in the Ethereal Plane can reach any of the [[Energy Realms]], and even the Plane of Magic itself, but few are foolhardy enough to leave the safe confines of the Ethereal shores, where the ghostly, permeable echoes of the material plane can still be perceived. 

%%SECRET
The Ethereal Plane is the interstitial "glue" in which the [[energy realms]] exist. 

The "border Ethereal" overlaps with the material world: ley lines are, in a sense, a manifestation of the border ethereal. In the Ethereal Plane, ley lines appear to be rushing rivers, for example. 

The further "down / in" you travel, the stranger things get, until at the greatest "depths" from the material plane you reach the Plane of Magic
