# Ironroot
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Ironroot is a poorly described [[Energy Realms|Energy Realm]], associated in [[Airion|Airion the Mistspeaker]]'s system with strength, metal, and the mingling of Earth and Magic.

It is variously considered a compound elemental realm, mixing the [[Elemental Plane of Earth]] and the [[Plane of Magic]]; a border region of the [[Elemental Plane of Earth]]; or its own unique realm of metal. Some consider this to be what the dwarves describe as the [[Elemental Plane of Metal]].
