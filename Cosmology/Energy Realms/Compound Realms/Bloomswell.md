# Bloomswell
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Bloomswell is a poorly described [[Energy Realms|Energy Realm]], associated by [[Airion|Airion the Mistspeaker]] with abundance, and described as a vast ocean bursting with color, lush with vibrant life filling the waters of the realm. 

It is variously considered a compound elemental realm, mixing the [[Elemental Plane of Water]] and the [[Plane of Magic]]; a border region of the [[Elemental Plane of Water]]; or its own unique realm of life and growth.
