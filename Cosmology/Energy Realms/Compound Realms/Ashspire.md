# Ashspire
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

Ashspire is a poorly described [[Energy Realms|Energy Realm]], a land of explosive destruction, ashy wastes, and death. Few venture here, and reliable reports are hard to come back. 

It is variously considered a compound elemental realm, mixing the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]] and the [[Plane of Magic]]; a border region of the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]]; or its own unique realm of death and destruction.
