# The Elemental Plane of Metal
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Elemental Plane of Metal is a hypothesized Energy Realm, that is implied to exist by the traditional dwarven model of seven fundamental energies: Spirit, Magic, Earth, Air, Fire, Water, and Metal. In that model, the non-Spirit energies are each assumed to derive from a specific energy realm, requiring the existence of an energy realm associated with metal. 

The plane's existence is disputed. [[Lyrannis of Aquilona]] dismissed the idea, citing a lack of evidence for pure metal elementals. Other metaphysicists point to descriptions of massive twisted caverns of iron, usually assumed to be part of the [[Elemental Plane of Earth]], as possible evidence for the Elemental Plane of Metal. Alternatively, the dwarven Plane of Metal may actually be [[Airion|Airion's]] [[Ironroot]] and [[Pyreforge]], two compound realms associated with the Elemental Plane of Earth.
