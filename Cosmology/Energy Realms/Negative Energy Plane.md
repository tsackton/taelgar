# The Negative Energy Plane
>[!info]+ Information
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

The Negative Energy Plane is a hypothesized [[Energy Realms|Energy Realm]], described in the epic lizardfolk poem [[The Endless Currents]]. In this work, the lizardfolk mystic [[Aznoke|Aznoke]] speaks of a Plane of Still Waters: a tranquil, shadowed realm where the currents of life slow and pool, allowing death to cleanse the essence of life, and prepare for renewal. Some scholars in the [[Faculty of Metaphysics]] speculate that this may correspond to the Negative Energy Plane.

No planar traveler is known to have reached the Negative Energy Plane and returned safely.
