# Giants

The giants of old were great crafters and creators, masters of weaving magic and reality together to create items of great power. Vast skyships sailed above the land, held aloft by the runes of giantkind, and great forges and metalworks produced artifacts of unparalleled beauty and power. 

Although much reduced in power since the days of myth and legend, giants still maintain the customs and traditions of the ancient days.

In this age, the dragons are the masters of true magic, of weaving spells and shaping reality with the energy of other planes. But dragons do not craft things, they do not build.

The giants were the masters of creating permanence, of weaving magic and reality together to create items of great power. Vast skyships sailed above the land, held aloft by the runes of giantkin, and great forges and metalworks produced artifacts of unparalleled beauty and power.

But this empire was not to last, and in the great battles between giants and dragons the earth was shattered. The few elves who walked the world in this age hid quietly in the shadows, protected and guarded by the Firstborn, the Wild Star and the Living Tree, kept out of sight of the great beings that strode across the world.

Finally, [[Divine Presence|Arha]] and [[Divine Presence|Malik]] put a stop to this, and pulled the worlds apart, so magic was diminished. The firstborn races grew, and the dragons and giants shrank, but never disappeared, and some retain something of the magic and power of that age.

The Elder Titans, the oldest and most power of the giants, were born from the earth and the sky and the form and elements made conscious. Thus, unlike dragons which are fundamentally creatures of magic, giants are of the earth itself and their many forms reflect the elements and nature of the world.

From these elder titans, the races of giants are descended. Where the elder titans are now, no one knows, but when the world was sundered they presumably were cast to other planes.

Legacy of the Giants

The giants of old were great crafters and creators. They learned to weave magic into everything they did, and it is said that the Runemaster practiced the art of rune magic with Agmir, one of the greatest of the elder titans. While dwarven runes can twist the workings of magic to bind an effect, the giant runes it is said could reshape reality itself, creating connections to the realm of magic where none existed before. Indeed, many nodes where ley lines cross are said to be relics of the age of titans.

Only hidden bits of the vast empire of the giants remain, after many remakings of the world and the passage of time.

Kinds of Giants

While the elder titans were as like gods, the giants themselves, while strong, are a mortal race. Many kinds of giants walk the earth, reflecting different aspects of the elements. Some have forsaken their legacy entirely, and some have been twisted by evil chance and retain little of the great intellect of their former selves. Others have held true to the ideals of the great empire of long ago.

All giants await the return of the elder titans and the reforging of the kingdom of giants on Taelgar. They are a strongly ordered society.
