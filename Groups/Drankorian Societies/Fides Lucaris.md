# Fides Lucaris
>[!info]+ Information  
> An organization  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

The Fides Lucaris was a Drankorian secret society associated with clandestine actions against Drankorian imperial power in the years following the [[Drankorian Civil War]]. 

%%SECRET

"Helea loyalist" faction, the losers of the civil war who wanted to maintain the growing cosmopolitan / open society of Golden Age Drankor, exemplified by Fides Lucaris (FEE-dehs loo-KAH-rees). Perhaps maintained some low-scale IRA-style terrorism during the Expansion War era, probably were dramatically crushed by Apollyon. Presumably this is the faction that Rai's ancestors were from. Not everyone in this faction was perfect, of course; might of also attracted some "Flag-Smasher" types (anarchists from Falcon and Winter Solider), but generally these were the "good guys" and part of the story of Drankor is the decline and fall associated with the good guys losing the civil war.
