# The Ko'zula
>[!info]+ Information  
> One of the the [[Northern Tribes|Northern Tribes of the Deno'qai]]

The Ko'zula are one of the [[Northern Tribes|northern]] [[Deno'qai]] tribes. They are closely associated with the northern mountains, the [[Forest of Nightmares]], and the stories of the lost tanshi known as the [[Meswati]].

## Territory

The Ko'zula live in the far north, in the mountains and cold forests of the [[Northern Sentinels]]. The [[Forest of Nightmares]] lies on the western border of Ko'zula territory.

## Local Culture

Like other northern Deno'qai, the Ko'zula practice [[Tanshi Worship]] and preserve customs shaped by high mountains, colder forests, hunting, and long contact with [[Stoneborn]] and [[Dwarves]]. They are especially noted for learning terraced farming from the stoneborn and growing hardy crops in the long days of short northern summers.

Northern Deno'qai custom emphasizes hunting, reindeer, mammoth hunts, yearly gatherings, marriage outside the village, and a strong expectation that individuals serve the good of the community.

## Recent history

The Ko'zula were among the northern Deno'qai survivors who fled into the mountains during the Great War and its aftermath. In recent generations, they have remained in the peaks and icy pine forests of the high slopes, where they have prospered in relative safety.

In 1748 DR, [[Delwath]] came among the Ko'zula seeking knowledge of the [[Meswati]]. The Ko'zula preserved the memory of the lost tanshi and aided his quest to free them.
