# The Shu'anra
>[!info]+ Information  
> One of the the [[Northern Tribes|Northern Tribes of the Deno'qai]]

The Shu'anra are one of the [[Northern Tribes|northern]] [[Deno'qai]] tribes. They are associated with the forests east of the [[Blackwater Fens]], the low mountain passes, and northern stories of the Great War and the lost tanshi known as the [[Meswati]].

## Territory

The Shu'anra live largely in the forests east of the [[Blackwater Fens]] and in the low passes of the [[Northern Sentinels]]. Some Shu'anra families later moved south from the marginal northern lands, including families that settled among the [[Te'kula]] in the [[Elderwood]].

## Local Culture

As northern Deno'qai, the Shu'anra practice [[Tanshi Worship]] and share the customs of the [[Northern Tribes]], including hunting, reindeer, heavy furs, lamellar armor, yearly gatherings, and strong community obligations. Recent Shu'anra trade includes [[Dwarves|dwarves]], [[Kenku|kenku]], and [[Stoneborn|stoneborn]].

## Recent history

The Shu'anra were among the northern Deno'qai survivors after the devastation of the early [[Great War]]. They fled into the mountains, survived the [[Blood Years]] in marginal lands, and later reemerged, with some families drifting south in search of safer and less marginal homes.

Stories preserved by [[Ariel]] remember dragonfire, the flight into the Sentinels, and the lost tanshi called the [[Meswati]].
