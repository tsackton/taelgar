# The Te'kula
>[!info]+ Information  
> One of the the [[Elderwood Tribes|Elderwood Tribes of the Deno'qai]]  
>> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Whereabouts")`

 %% Written from DR (POV:: 1749) point of view %%

The Te'kula are a [[Deno'qai]] tribe of the [[Elderwood]]. They are closely associated with the mystery of [[Rai]], the tanshi [[Aasimti]], and the years in which their village was hidden from [[Mezzar|Grimbaskal]].

## Territory

The Te'kula live in the [[Elderwood]]. In [[On the Lost People of the Forests]], [[Gnaeus]] places them in the heart of the Elderwood, near the source of the [[Kayan]].

## Local Culture

Gnaeus records the Te'kula as distinct from the other Deno'qai tribes he encountered, centered on worship of [[Rai]], whom the Te'kula understood as [[Aasimti]], an old god or tanshi associated with the Elderwood and the ancient spirits of great trees. Later events preserve the same association between the Te'kula, Rai, Aasimti, and the village's Godcallers.

## Recent history

The Te'kula were long hidden by the magic of [[Rai]] and [[Aasimti]] in a pocket dimension, protecting them from [[Mezzar|Grimbaskal]]. They returned to the world after Grimbaskal's defeat in 1748 DR.
