%% Written from a DR (POV:: 1748) point of view, before the [[Chardonian Chalyte Trade Reforms]] %%

The Patelios dynasty is one of the four major oligarch families that controls [[Chardon]]'s [[chalyte]] trade. Historically the second most prominent, after the [[Auratan Family]], the current generation of leaders of the family is highly ambitious and eager to take advantage of fragmentation and bitterness among branches of the [[Auratan Family]] to expand their influence. 

They have invested heavily in refinery operations in [[Chardon]], rapidly expanding the fraction of refined ore controlled by them, though some wonder about the source of the funds to support this seemingly endless expansion.
