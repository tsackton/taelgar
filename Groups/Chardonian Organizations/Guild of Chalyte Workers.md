# The Guild of Chalyte Workers

The Guild of Chalyte Workers is a formal association representing workers in [[Chardon]]'s [[chalyte]] refining trade, emerging out of the [[Chardonian Chalyte Trade Reforms|chalyte reforms]] caused by the [[Session 116 (DuFr)|defeat of Fausto]], the [[Eightfold Flame]] riots, and the [[Session 128 (DuFr)|opening of the chalyte trade]] in the [[Chataan Mountains]]. 

The Guild regulates who is allowed to work in the chalyte refining trade, as well as the quality and standards for processed chalyte crystal and powder, based on a legal requirement that only Guild-approved workers can work in the refineries managed by the [[Chalyte Refining Corporation]]. The [[Chalyte Refining Corporation]] both has a legal monopoly on the sale of refined chalyte, and is partially controlled by the Guild itself.
