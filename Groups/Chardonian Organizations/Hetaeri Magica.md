## Hetaeri Magica

Founded in DR 1125 as the joint magical arm of the University of Chardon and the Great Library, with responsibilities both to safeguard magical knowledge, train new wizards, and regulate magic in the academy (and eventually, in coordination with the Imperial court, the city).

This is basically a magical secret society, although its existence is not particularly secret. Technically ability to cast spells is not required, but is customary to be a member. Has a symbiotic relationship with the University (many members of the Faculty of Magic are in the Hetaeri Magica) and a governance relationship with the Great Library (the Committee on Archives serves as the board of directors of the Great Library, and about 2/3rds the Great Library's funds come via this committee).

Promulgates rules about the use of magic within Precinct IV, which are similar, but not identical, to the rules enforced by the Pracentis magical wing (the mageguard).

Center of political connections outside the academia, much more so than the University or the Library.
