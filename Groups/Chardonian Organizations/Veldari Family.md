%% Written from a DR (POV:: 1748) point of view, before the [[Chardonian Chalyte Trade Reforms]] %%

The Veldari Family is one of the four major oligarch families controlling [[Chardon]]'s [[chalyte]] trade. Though the smallest of four families, the Veldari are organized, efficient, and unified, leaving them in a strong position to benefit from vertical integration across mining, transport, and final production. 

Though small, they are powerful in Chardon, and frequently act to quash rumors of bribery and shady dealings with the [[Chamber of Stamps]].
