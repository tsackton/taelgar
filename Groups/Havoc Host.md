# The Havoc Host
>[!info]+ Information
> A mercenary company
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

The Havoc Host is mysterious mercenary company of unknown loyalties, based in the [[Northwest Coast]] beyond the edges of the [[Chardonian Empire]]. They were involved in several kidnappings perpetrated by the [[Aboleths|aboleth]] [[Ithu’rax]] in DR 1748, including the kidnapping of [[Dinia]] from the [[Fiatara Mountains]] and [[Mikel]] from [[Suwi]], though their subsequent fate is unknown.
