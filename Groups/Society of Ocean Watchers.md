# The Society of Ocean Watchers
>[!info]+ Information  
> A group  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

The Society of Ocean Watchers is a Mawaran organization associated with the old ocean-watching traditions of the [[Mawar Confederacy]]: the people who keep watch for danger, wrecks, and stranger signs from the western sea. Ocean watchers are known as keepers of stories, warnings, and sea-lore, and some are said to commune with the waters of the [[Endless Ocean]].
