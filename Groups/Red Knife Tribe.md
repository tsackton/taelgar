The Red Knife tribe is a group of [[Goblins|goblins]] that occupy the rough lands in the foothills of the [[Fiatara Mountains]], northwest of [[Voltara]]. Though a nuisance to Voltara in the DR 1740s, since the rise and fall [[Grumella]] they have not been regularly seen.
