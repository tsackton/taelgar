# Grash's Horde
>[!info]+ Information  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_PageDatedValue")`  
> `$=dv.view("_scripts/view/get_Affiliations")`

The [[Orcs|orc]] horde commanded by [[Grash]], based out of [[Kharsan]]. This horde formed in the summer of DR 1747, as [[Grash]] began gathering the various scattered minor groups of [[Orcs|orcs]] that had been scratching out a living in the marginal lands of the [[Nashtkar]]. 

These [[Orcs|orcs]] worship [[Grash]] as a demigod of [[Thark]], and most believe fully that [[Grash]] has granted them freedom from death as long as they fight for his cause. 

This horde began constructing a fortified warren a few miles south of [[Kharsan]] in early DR 1748, and marched to war under [[Grash]]'s leadership against the [[People of the Rainbow]] based in [[Xurkhaz]] in December 1748, during the [[War of the Cloak]].

## Numbers

The fort has around 140 adults:
-5 leaders (blades, war chiefs, etc)
-15 spellcasters (mostly acolytes, about 10 low level, 5 more powerful)
-75 trained warriors
-45 in training, or work crews, or tending young, etc

And also 85 young:
-40 yearlings, in care of creche mothers
-20 youth, training
-15 teens, can fight but not yet full grown

At any one time, the base camp usually has about 50 of the young and about 80 of the adults. The rest are:

-work crews doing stuff in [[Kharsan]]
-patrols
-hunting parties after food

The loss of 40 [[Orcs]] in the war party, plus another 5 [[Orcs]] killed and 1 captured at the monastery is a serious blow. This in total was about 1/3 of their fighting strength, and [[Grash]] is currently pissed at the defeat. This has caused some rethinking and consternation from [[Apollyon|Apollyon]], who didn't really want [[Grash]] to launch an attack against [[Dunmar]], not yet anyway, not until he first captures the [[Cloak of Rainbows]]. 

Fortunately for [[Grash]] and unfortunately for the Dunmari, another 100 or so [[Orcs]] that have died since [[Grash]] arrived -- some fighting the gnolls, some to bad luck, some to intra-tribe conflict -- are revivable as zombies. The worst part of the loss to [[Havdar]] was that the bodies were burned.

Over the summer of DR 1748, many more [[Orcs]] gathered in [[Grash]]'s horde, as the preparations for war began and recruitment ramped up. By the time the army marched, the main tribe based around [[Kharsan]] had been supplemented by maybe 10 times their number of smaller family groups gathered from nearly the entire [[Nashtkar]]. 
## Motives

Play the [[Orcs]] up as thoroughly in [[Grash]]'s sway. They really believe in endless power and that [[Grash]] can give them immortality. They have seen the zombies fight in the stadium, seemingly die, and rise again. These are true fanatics.

They can be intimidated or threatened, but don't fear death (although do fear fire).

A credible threat to torch their fort is about the only thing that may stop them.

If they capture party, won't kill them, as [[Grash]] has demanded all intruders be brought to him.
